coming
1. Approaching; of the future, especially the near future; the next; as, the coming week or year; the coming exhibition. Welcome the coming, speed the parting, guest. Pope. Your coming days and years. Byron. 2. Ready to come; complaisant; fond. [Obs.] Pope.\n\n1. Approach; advent; manifestation; as, the coming of the train. 2. Specifically: The Second Advent of Christ. Coming in. (a) Entrance; entrance way; manner of entering; beginning. \"The goings out thereof, and the comings in thereof.\" Ezek. xliii. 11 (b) Income or revenue. \"What are thy comings in\" Shak.
