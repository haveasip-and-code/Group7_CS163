residentiary
Having residence; as, a canon residentary; a residentiary guardian. Dr. H. More.\n\n1. One who is resident. The residentiary, or the frequent visitor of the favored spot, . . . will discover that both have been there. Coleridge. 2. An ecclesiastic who keeps a certain residence. Syn. -- Inhabitant; inhabiter; dweller; sojourner.
