fleck
A flake; also, a lock, as of wool. [Obs.] J. Martin.\n\nA spot; a streak; a speckle. \"A sunny fleck.\" Longfellow. Life is dashed with flecks of sin. tennyson.\n\nTo spot; to streak or stripe; to variegate; to dapple. Both flecked with white, the true Arcadian strain. Dryden. A bird, a cloud, flecking the sunny air. Trench.
