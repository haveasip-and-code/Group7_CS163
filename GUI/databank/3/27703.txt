restless
1. Never resting; unquiet; uneasy; continually moving; as, a restless child. Chaucer. \"Restless revolution day by day.\" Milton. 2. Not satisfied to be at rest or in peace; averse to repose or quiet; eager for change; discontented; as, restless schemers; restless ambition; restless subjects. \"Restless at home , and ever prone to range.\" Dryden. 3. Deprived of rest or sleep. Restless he passed the remnants of the night. Dryden. 4. Passed in unquietness; as, the patient has had a restless night. 5. Not affording rest; as, a restless chair.
