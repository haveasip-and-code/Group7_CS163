transverse
Lying or being across, or in a crosswise direction; athwart; -- often opposed to Ant: longitudinal. Transverse axis (of an ellipse or hyperbola) (Geom.), that axis which passes through the foci. -- Transverse partition (Bot.), a partition, as of a pericarp, at right angles with the valves, as in the siliques of mustard.\n\n1. Anything that is transverse or athwart. 2. (Geom.)  The longer, or transverse, axis of an ellipse.\n\nTo overturn; to change. [R.] C. Leslie.\n\nTo change from prose into verse, or from verse into prose. [Obs.] Duke of Buckingham.
