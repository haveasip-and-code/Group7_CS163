pliable
1. Capable of being plied, turned, or bent; easy to be bent; flexible; pliant; supple; limber; yielding; as, willow is a pliable plant. 2. Flexible in disposition; readily yielding to influence, arguments, persuasion, or discipline; easy to be persuaded; -- sometimes in a bad sense; as, a pliable youth. \"Pliable she promised to be.\" Dr. H. More. -- Pli\"a*ble*ness, n. -- Pli\"a*bly, adv.
