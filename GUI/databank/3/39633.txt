ghostly
1. Relating to the soul; not carnal or secular; spiritual; as, a ghostly confessor. Save and defend us from our ghostly enemies. Book of Common Prayer [Ch. of Eng. ] One of the gostly children of St. Jerome. Jer. Taylor. 2. Of or pertaining to apparitions. Akenside.\n\nSpiritually; mystically. Chaucer.
