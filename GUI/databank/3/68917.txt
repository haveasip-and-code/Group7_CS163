suppliant
1. Asking earnestly and submissively; entreating; beseeching; supplicating. The rich grow suppliant, and the poor grow proud. Dryden. 2. Manifesting entreaty; expressive of supplication. To bow and sue for grace With suppliant knee. Milton. Syn. -- Entreating; beseeching; suing; begging; supplicating; imploring. -- Sup\"pli*ant*ly, adv. -- Sup\"pli*ant*ness, n.\n\nOne who supplicates; a humble petitioner; one who entreats submissively. Hear thy suppliant's prayer. Dryden.
