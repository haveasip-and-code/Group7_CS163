sightless
1. Wanting sight; without sight; blind. Of all who blindly creep or sightless soar. Pope. 2. That can not be seen; invisible. [Obs.] The sightless couriers of the air. Shak. 3. Offensive or unpleasing to the eye; unsightly; as, sightless stains. [R.] Shak. -- Sight\"less*ly, adv.- Sight\"less*ness, n.
