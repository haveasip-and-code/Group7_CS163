pedagogue
1. (Gr. Antiq.)  A slave who led his master's children to school, and had the charge of them generally. 2. A teacher of children; one whose occupation is to teach the young; a schoolmaster. 3. One who by teaching has become formal, positive, or pedantic in his ways; one who has the manner of a schoolmaster; a pedant. Goldsmith.\n\nTo play the pedagogue toward. [Obs.] Prior.
