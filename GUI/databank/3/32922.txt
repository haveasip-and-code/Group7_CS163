repercussive
1. Tending or able to repercuss; having the power of sending back; causing to reverberate. Ye repercussive rocks! repeat the sound. W. Pattison. 2. Repellent. [Obs.] \"Blood is stanched by astringent and repecussive medicines.\" Bacon. 3. Driven back; rebounding; reverberated. \"Rages loud the repercussive roar.\" Thomson.\n\nA repellent. [Obs.] Bacon.
