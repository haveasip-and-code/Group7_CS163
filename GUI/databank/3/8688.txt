malleus
1. (Anat.)  The outermost of the three small auditory bones, ossicles; the hammer. It is attached to the tympanic membrane by a long process, the handle or manubrium. See Illust. of Far. 2. (Zoöl.)  One of the hard lateral pieces of the mastax of Rotifera. See Mastax. 3. (Zoöl.)  A genus of bivalve shells; the hammer shell.
