gargle
See Gargoyle.\n\n1. To wash or rinse, as the mouth or throat, particular the latter, agitating the liquid (water or a medicinal preparation) by an expulsion of air from the lungs. 2. To warble; to sing as if gargling [Obs.] Waller.\n\nA liquid, as water or some medicated preparation, used to cleanse the mouth and throat, especially for a medical effect.
