satanic
Of or pertaining to Satan; having the qualities of Satan; resembling Satan; extremely malicious or wicked; devilish; infernal. \"Satanic strength.\" \"Satanic host.\" Milton. Detest the slander which, with a Satanic smile, exults over the character it has ruined. Dr. T. Dwight. -- Sa*tan\"ic*al*ly, adv. -- Sa*tan\"ic*al*ness, n.
