atonic
1. (Med.)  Characterized by atony, or want of vital energy; as, an atonic disease. 2. (Gram.)  Unaccented; as, an atonic syllable. 3. Destitute of tone vocality; surd. Rush.\n\n1. (Gram.)  A word that has no accent. 2. An element of speech entirely destitute of vocality, or produced by the breath alone; a nonvocal or surd consonant; a breathing. Rush. 3. (Med.)  A remedy capable of allaying organic excitement or irritation. Dunglison.
