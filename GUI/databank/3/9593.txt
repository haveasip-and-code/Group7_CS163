circumference
1. The line that goes round or encompasses a circular figure; a periphery. Millon. 2. A circle; anything circular. His ponderous shield . . . Behind him cast. The broad circumference Hung on his shoulders like the moon. Milton. 3. The external surface of a sphere, or of any orbicular body.\n\nTo include in a circular space; to bound. [Obs.] Sir T. Browne.
