forthright
Straight forward; in a straight direction. [Archaic] Sir P. Sidney.\n\nDirect; straightforward; as, a forthright man. [Archaic] Lowell. They were Night and Day, and Day and Night, Piligrims wight with steps forthright. Emerson.\n\nA straight path. [Archaic] Here's a maze trod, indeed, Through forthrights and meanders! Shak.
