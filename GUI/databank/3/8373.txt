homespun
1. Spun or wrought at home; of domestic manufacture; coarse; plain. \"Homespun country garbs.\" W. Irving. 2. Plain in manner or style; not elegant; rude; coarse. \"Our homespun English proverb.\" Dryden. \"Our homespun authors.\" Addison.\n\n1. Cloth made at home; as, he was dressed in homespun. 2. An unpolished, rustic person. [Obs.] Shak.
