argent
1. Silver, or money. [Archaic] 2. (Fig. & Poet.)  Whiteness; anything that is white. The polished argent of her breast. Tennyson. 3. (Her.)  The white color in coats of arms, intended to represent silver, or, figuratively, purity, innocence, beauty, or gentleness; -- represented in engraving by a plain white surface. Weale.\n\nMade of silver; of a silvery color; white; shining. Yonder argent fields above. Pope.
