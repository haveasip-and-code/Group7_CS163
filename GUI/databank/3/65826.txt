blowtube
1. A blowgun. Tylor. 2. A similar instrument, commonly of tin, used by boys for discharging paper wads and other light missiles. 3. (Glassmaking)  A long wrought iron tube, on the end of which the workman gathers a quantity of \"metal\" (melted glass), and through which he blows to expand or shape it; -- called also blowing tube, and blowpipe.
