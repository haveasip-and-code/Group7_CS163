significative
1. Betokening or representing by an external sign. The holy symbols or signs are not barely significative. Brerewood. 2. Having signification or meaning; expressive of a meaning or purpose; significant. Neither in the degrees of kindred they were destitute of significative words. Camden. -- Sig*nif\"i*ca*tive*ly, adv. -- Sig*nif\"i*ca*tive*ness, n.
