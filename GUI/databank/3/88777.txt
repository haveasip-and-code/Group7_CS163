epicurean
1. Pertaining to Epicurus, or following his philosophy. \"The sect Epicurean.\" Milton. 2. Given to luxury; adapted to luxurious tastes; luxurious; pertaining to good eating. Courses of the most refined and epicurean dishes. Prescott. Epicurean philosophy. See Atomic philosophy, under Atomic.\n\n1. A follower or Epicurus. 2. One given to epicurean indulgence.
