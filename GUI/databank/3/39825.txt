revest
1. To clothe again; to cover, as with a robe; to robe. Her, nathless, . . . the enchanterrevest and decked with due habiliments. Spenser. 2. To vest again with possession or office; as, to revest a magistrate with authority.\n\nTo take effect or vest again, as a title; to revert to former owner; as, the title or right revels in A after alienation.
