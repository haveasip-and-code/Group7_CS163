pertinent
1. Belonging or related to the subject or matter in hand; fit or appropriate in any way; adapted to the end proposed; apposite; material; relevant; as, pertinent illustrations or arguments; pertinent evidence. 2. Regarding; concerning; belonging; pertaining. [R.] \"Pertinent unto faith.\" Hooker. Syn. -- Apposite; relevant; suitable; appropriate; fit. -- Per\"ti*nent*ly, adv. -- Per\"ti*nent*ness, n.
