thomsenolite
A fluoride of aluminium, calcium, and sodium occurring with the cryolite of Greenland. THOMSEN'S DISEASE Thom\"sen's dis*ease\". Etym: [From Thomsen, a physician of Sleswick.] (Med.)  An affection apparently congenital, consisting in tonic contraction and stiffness of the voluntary muscles occurring after a period of muscular inaction.
