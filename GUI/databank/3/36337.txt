incontinent
1. Not continent; uncontrolled; not restraining the passions or appetites, particularly the sexual appetite; indulging unlawful lust; unchaste; lewd. 2. (Med.)  Unable to restrain natural evacuations.\n\nOne who is unchaste. B. Jonson.\n\nIncontinently; instantly immediately. [Obs.] He says he will return incontinent. Shak.
