grout
1. Coarse meal; ground malt; pl. groats. 2. Formerly, a kind of beer or ale. [Eng.] 3. pl.  Lees; dregs; grounds. [Eng.] \"Grouts of tea.\" Dickens. 4. A thin, coarse mortar, used for pouring into the joints of masonry and brickwork; also, a finer material, used in finishing the best ceilings. Gwilt.\n\nTo fill up or finish with grout, as the joints between stones.
