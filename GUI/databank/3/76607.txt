reis effendi
A title formerly given to one of the chief Turkish officers of state. He was chancellor of the empire, etc. REISSNER'S MEMBRANE Reiss\"ner's mem\"brane (rs\"nrz mm\"brn). Etym: [Named from E. Reissner, A German anatomist.] (Anat.)  The thin membrane which separates the canal of the cochlea from the vestibular scala in the internal ear.
