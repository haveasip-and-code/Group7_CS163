felon
1. (Law)  A person who has committed a felony. 2. A person guilty or capable of heinous crime. 3. (Med.)  A kind of whitlow; a painful imflammation of the periosteum of a finger, usually of the last joint. Syn. -- Criminal; convict; malefactor; culprit.\n\nCharacteristic of a felon; malignant; fierce; malicious; cruel; traitorous; disloyal. Vain shows of love to vail his felon hate. Pope.
