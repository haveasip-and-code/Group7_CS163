eleemosynary
1. Relating to charity, alms, or almsgiving; intended for the distribution of charity; as, an eleemosynary corporation. 2. Given in charity or alms; having the nature of alms; as, eleemosynary assistance. \"Eleemosynary cures.\" Boyle. 3. Supported by charity; as, eleemosynary poor.\n\nOne who subsists on charity; a dependent. South.
