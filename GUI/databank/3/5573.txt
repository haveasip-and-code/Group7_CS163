hideous
1. Frightful, shocking, or offensive to the eyes; dreadful to behold; as, a hideous monster; hideous looks. \"A piteous and hideous spectacle.\" Macaulay. 2. Distressing or offensive to the ear; exciting terror or dismay; as, a hideous noise. \"Hideous cries.\" Shak. 3. Hateful; shocking. \"Sure, you have some hideous matter to deliver.\" Shak. Syn. -- Frightful; ghastly; grim; grisly; horrid; dreadful; terrible. -- Hid\"e*ous*ly, adv. -- Hid\"e*ous*ness, n.
