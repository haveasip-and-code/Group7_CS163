sportful
1. Full of sport; merry; frolicsome; full of jesting; indulging in mirth or play; playful; wanton; as, a sportful companion. Down he alights among the sportful herd. Milton. 2. Done in jest, or for mere play; sportive. They are no sportful productions of the soil. Bentley. -- Sport\"ful*ly, adv. -- Sport\"ful*ness, n.
