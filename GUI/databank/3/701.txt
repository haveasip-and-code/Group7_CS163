exogenous
1. (Bot.)  Pertaining to, or having the character of, an exogen; -- the opposite of endogenous. 2. (Biol.)  Growing by addition to the exterior. 3. (Anat.)  Growing from previously ossified parts; -- opposed to autogenous. Owen. Exogenous aneurism (Med.), an aneurism which is produced by causes acting from without, as from injury.
