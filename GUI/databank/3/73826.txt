obituary
Of or pertaining to the death of a person or persons; as, an obituary notice; obituary poetry.\n\n1. That which pertains to, or is called forth by, the obit or death of a person; esp., an account of a deceased person; a notice of the death of a person, accompanied by a biographical sketch. 2. (R.C.Ch.)  A list of the dead, or a register of anniversary days when service is performed for the dead.
