lionly
Like a lion; fierce. [Obs.] Milton. LION'S EAR Li\"on's ear`. (Bot.)  A name given in Western South America to certain plants with shaggy tomentose leaves, as species of Culcitium, and Espeletia. LION'S FOOT Li\"on's foot`. (Bot.) (a) A composite plant of the genus Prenanthes, of which several species are found in the United States. (b) The edelweiss.
