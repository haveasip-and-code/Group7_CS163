pinner
1. One who, or that which, pins or fastens, as with pins. 2. (Costume) (a) A headdress like a cap, with long lappets. (b) An apron with a bib; a pinafore. (c) A cloth band for a gown. [Obs.] With kerchief starched, and pinners clean. Gay. 3. A pin maker.\n\nOne who pins or impounds cattle. See Pin, v. t. [Obs.]
