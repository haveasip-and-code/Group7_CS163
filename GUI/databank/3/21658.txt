winder
1. One who, or that which, winds; hence, a creeping or winding plant. 2. An apparatus used for winding silk, cotton, etc., on spools, bobbins, reels, or the like. 3. (Arch.)  One in a flight of steps which are curved in plan, so that each tread is broader at one end than at the other; -- distinguished from flyer.\n\nTo fan; to clean grain with a fan. [Prov. Eng.]\n\nA blow taking away the breath. [Slang]\n\nTo wither; to fail. [Obs.] Holland.
