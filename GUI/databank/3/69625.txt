dioptric
Of or pertaining to the dioptre, or to the metric system of numbering glasses. -- n.  A dioptre. See Dioptre.\n\nOf or pertaining to dioptrics; assisting vision by means of the refraction of light; refractive; as, the dioptric system; a dioptric glass or telescope. \"Dioptrical principles.\" Nichol. Dioptric curve (Geom.), a Cartesian oval. See under Cartesian.
