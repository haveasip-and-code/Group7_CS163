darter
1. One who darts, or who throw darts; that which darts. 2. (Zoöl.)  The snakebird, a water bird of the genus Plotus; -- so called because it darts out its long, snakelike neck at its prey. See Snakebird. 3. (Zoöl.)  A small fresh-water etheostomoid fish. The group includes numerous genera and species, all of them American. See Etheostomoid.
