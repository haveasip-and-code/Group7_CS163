troglodyte
1. (Ethnol.)  One of any savage race that dwells in caves, instead of constructing dwellings; a cave dweller. Most of the primitive races of man were troglodytes. In the troglodytes' country there is a lake, for the hurtful water it beareth called the \"mad lake.\" Holland. 2. (Zoöl.)  An anthropoid ape, as the chimpanzee. 3. (Zoöl.)  The wren.
