chuckle
1. To call, as a hen her chickens; to cluck. [Obs.] Dryden. 2. To fondle; to cocker. [Obs.] Dryden.\n\nA short, suppressed laugh; the expression of satisfaction, exultation, or derision.\n\nTo laugh in a suppressed or broken manner, as expressing inward satisfaction, exultation, or derision.
