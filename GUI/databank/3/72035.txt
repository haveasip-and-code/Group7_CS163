campaign
1. An open field; a large, open plain without considerable hills. SeeChampaign. Grath. 2. (Mil.)  A connected series of military operations forming a distinct stage in a war; the time during which an army keeps the field. Wilhelm. 3. Political operations preceding an election; a canvass. [Cant, U. S.] 4. (Metal.)  The period during which a blast furnace is continuously in operation.\n\nTo serve in a campaign.
