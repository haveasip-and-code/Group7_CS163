contradictory
1. Affirming the contrary; implying a denial of what has been asserted; also, mutually contradicting; inconsistent. \"Contradictory assertions.\" South. 2. Opposing or opposed; repugnant. Schemes . . . contradictory to common sense. Addisn.\n\n1. A proposition or thing which denies or opposes another; contrariety. It is common with princes to will contradictories. Bacon. 2. pl. (Logic)  propositions with the same terms, but opposed to each other both in quality and quantity.
