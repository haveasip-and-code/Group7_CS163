foal
The young of any animal of the Horse family (Equidæ); a colt; a filly. Foal teeth (Zoöl.), the first set of teeth of a horse. -- In foal, With foal, being with young; pregnant; -- said of a mare or she ass.\n\nTo bring forth (a colt); -- said of a mare or a she ass.\n\nTo bring forth young, as an animal of the horse kind.
