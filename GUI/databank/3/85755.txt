pasty
Like paste, as in color, softness, stickness. \"A pasty complexion.\" G. Eliot.\n\nA pie consisting usually of meat wholly surrounded with a crust made of a sheet of paste, and often baked without a dish; a meat pie. \"If ye pinch me like a pasty.\" Shak. \"Apple pasties.\" Dickens. A large pasty baked in a pewter platter. Sir W. Scott.
