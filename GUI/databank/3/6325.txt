lenten
Lent. [Obs.] Piers Plowman.\n\n1. Of or pertaining to the fast called Lent; used in, or suitable to, Lent; as, the Lenten season. She quenched her fury at the flood. And with a Lenten salad cooled her blood. Dryden. 2. Spare, meager; plain; somber; unostentatious; not abundant or showy. \"Lenten entertainment.\" \" Lenten answer.\" Shak. \" Lenten suit.\" Beau. & Fl. Lenten color, black or violet. F. G. Lee.
