thirteenth
1. Next in order after the twelfth; the third after the tenth; -- the ordinal of thirteen; as, the thirteenth day of the month. 2. Constituting or being one of thirteen equal parts into which anything is divided.\n\n1. The quotient of a unit divided by thirteen; one of thirteen equal parts into which anything is divided. 2. The next in order after the twelfth. 3. (Mus.)  The interval comprising an octave and a sixth.
