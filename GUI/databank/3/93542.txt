incisive
1. Having the quality of incising, cutting, or penetrating, as with a sharp instrument; cutting; hence, sharp; acute; sarcastic; biting. \"An incisive, high voice.\" G. Eliot. And her incisive smile accrediting That treason of false witness in my blush. Mrs. Browning. 2. (Anat.)  Of or pertaining to the incisors; incisor; as, the incisive bones, the premaxillaries.
