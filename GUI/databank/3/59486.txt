soup
A liquid food of many kinds, usually made by boiling meat and vegetables, or either of them, in water, -- commonly seasoned or flavored; strong broth. Soup kitchen, an establishment for preparing and supplying soup to the poor. -- Soup ticket, a ticket conferring the privilege of receiving soup at a soup kitchen.\n\nTo sup or swallow. [Obs.] Wyclif.\n\nTo breathe out. [Obs.] amden.\n\nTo sweep. See Sweep, and Swoop. [Obs.]
