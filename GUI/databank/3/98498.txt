repast
1. The act of taking food. From dance to sweet repast they turn. Milton. 2. That which is taken as food; a meal; figuratively, any refreshment. \"Sleep . . . thy best repast.\" Denham. Go and get me some repast. Shak.\n\nTo supply food to; to feast; to take food. [Obs.] \"Repast them with my blood.\" Shak. He then, also, as before, left arbitrary the dieting and repasting of our minds. Milton.
