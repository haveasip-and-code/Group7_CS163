dialysis
1. (Gram.)  Diæresis. See Diæresis, 1. 2. (Rhet.)  Same as Asyndeton. 3. (Med.) (a) Debility. (b) A solution of continuity; division; separation of parts. 4. (Chem.)  The separation of different substances in solution, as crystalloids and colloids, by means of their unequal diffusion, especially through natural or artificial membranes.
