reputable
Having, or worthy of, good repute; held in esteem; honorable; praiseworthy; as, a reputable man or character; reputable conduct. In the article of danger, it is as reputable to elude an enemy as defeat one. Broome. Syn. -- Respectable; creditable; estimable. -- Rep\"u ta*ble*ness, n. -- Rep\"u*ta*bly, adv.
