studied
1. Closely examined; read with diligence and attention; made the subject of study; well considered; as, a studied lesson. 2. Well versed in any branch of learning; qualified by study; learned; as, a man well studied in geometry. I shrewdly suspect that he is little studied of a theory of moral proportions. Burke. 3. Premeditated; planned; designed; as, a studied insult. \"Studied magnificence.\" Hawthorne. 4. Intent; inclined. [Obs.] Shak.
