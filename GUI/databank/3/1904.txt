To drop the head or person suddenly; to bow. The learned pate Ducks to the golden fool. Shak.\n\n1. (Zool.)  Any bird of the subfamily Anatinæ, family Anatidæ. Note: The genera and species are numerous. They are divided into river ducks and sea ducks. Among the former are the common domestic duck (Anas boschas); the wood duck (Aix sponsa); the beautiful mandarin duck of China (Dendronessa galeriliculata); the Muscovy duck, originally of South America (Cairina moschata). Among the sea ducks are the eider, canvasback, scoter, etc. 2. A sudden inclination of the bead or dropping of the person, resembling the motion of a duck in water.
