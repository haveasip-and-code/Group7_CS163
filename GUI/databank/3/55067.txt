exemplar
1. A model, original, or pattern, to be copied or imitated; a specimen; sometimes; an ideal model or type, as that which an artist conceives. Such grand exemplar as make their own abilities the sole measure of what is fit or unfit. South. 2. A copy of a book or writing. [Obs.] Udall.\n\nExemplary. [Obs.] The exemplar piety of the father of a family. Jer. Taylor.
