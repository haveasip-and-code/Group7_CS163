1. (Zo√∂l.)  A minute arachnid, of the order Acarina, of which there are many species; as, the cheese mite, sugar mite, harvest mite, etc. See Acarina. 2. Etym: [D. mijt; prob. the same word.]  A small coin formerly circulated in England, rated at about a third of a farthing.
