yearly
1. Happening, accruing, or coming every year; annual; as, a yearly income; a yearly feast. 2. Lasting a year; as, a yearly plant. 3. Accomplished in a year; as, the yearly circuit, or revolution, of the earth. Shak.\n\nAnnually; once a year to year; as, blessings yearly bestowed. Yearly will I do this rite. Shak.
