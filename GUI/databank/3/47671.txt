saline
1. Consisting of salt, or containing salt; as, saline particles; saline substances; a saline cathartic. 2. Of the quality of salt; salty; as, a saline taste.\n\nA salt spring; a place where salt water is collected in the earth.\n\n1. (Chem.)  A crude potash obtained from beet-root residues and other similar sources. [Written also salin.] 2. (Med. Chem.)  A metallic salt; esp., a salt of potassium, sodium, lithium, or magnesium, used in medicine.
