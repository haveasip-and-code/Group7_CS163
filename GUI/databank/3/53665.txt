secant
Cutting; divivding into two parts; as, a secant line.\n\n1. (Geom.)  A line that cuts another; especially, a straight line cutting a curve in two or more points. 2. (Trig.)  A right line drawn from the center of a circle through one end of a circular arc, and terminated by a tangent drawn from the other end; the number expressing the ratio line of this line to the radius of the circle. See Trigonometrical function, under Function.
