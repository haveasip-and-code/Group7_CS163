stria
1. A minute groove, or channel; a threadlike line, as of color; a narrow structural band or line; a striation; as, the striæ, or groovings, produced on a rock by a glacier passing over it; the striæ on the surface of a shell; a stria of nervous matter in the brain. 2. (Arch.)  A fillet between the flutes of columns, pilasters, or the like. Oxf. Gloss.
