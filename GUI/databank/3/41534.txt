sagamore
1. Etym: [Cf. Sachem.]  The head of a tribe among the American Indians; a chief; -- generally used as synonymous with sachem, but some writters distinguished between them, making the sachem a chief of the first rank, and a sagamore one of the second rank. \"Be it sagamore, sachem, or powwow.\" Longfellow. 2. A juice used in medicine. [Obs.] Johnson.
