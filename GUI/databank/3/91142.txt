initial
1. Of or pertaining to the beginning; marking the commencement; incipient; commencing; as, the initial symptoms of a disease. 2. Placed at the beginning; standing at the head, as of a list or series; as, the initial letters of a name.\n\nThe first letter of a word or a name.\n\nTo put an initial to; to mark with an initial of initials. [R.]
