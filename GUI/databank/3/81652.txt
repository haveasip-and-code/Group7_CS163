seaman
A merman; the male of the mermaid. [R.] \"Not to mention mermaids or seamen.\" Locke.\n\nOne whose occupation is to assist in the management of ships at sea; a mariner; a sailor; -- applied both to officers and common mariners, but especially to the latter. Opposed to landman, or landsman. Able seaman, a sailor who is practically conversant with all the duties of common seamanship. -- ordinary seaman. See Ordinary.
