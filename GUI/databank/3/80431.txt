allegoric
Belonging to, or consisting of, allegory; of the nature of an allegory; describing by resemblances; figurative. \"An allegoric tale.\" Falconer. \"An allegorical application.\" Pope. Allegorical being . . . that kind of language which says one thing, but means another. Max Miller. Al`le*gor\"ic*al*ly, adv. -- Al`le*gor\"ic*al*ness, n.
