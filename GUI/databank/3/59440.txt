admirable
1. Fitted to excite wonder; wonderful; marvelous. [Obs.] In man there is nothing admirable but his ignorance and weakness. Jer. Taylor. 2. Having qualities to excite wonder united with approbation; deserving the highest praise; most excellent; -- used of persons or things. \"An admirable machine.\" \"Admirable fortitude.\" Macaulay. Syn. -- Wonderful; marvelous; surprising; excellent; delightful; praiseworthy.
