afore
1. Before. [Obs.] If he have never drunk wine afore. Shak. 2. (Naut.)  In the fore part of a vessel.\n\n1. Before (in all its senses). [Archaic] 2. (Naut.)  Before; in front of; farther forward than; as, afore the windlass. Afore the mast, among the common sailors; -- a phrase used to distinguish the ship's crew from the officers.
