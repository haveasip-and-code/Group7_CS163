paternoster
1. The Lord's prayer, so called from the first two words of the Latin version. 2. (Arch.)  A beadlike ornament in moldings. 3. (Angling)  A line with a row of hooks and bead Paternoster pump, Paternoster wheel, a chain pump; a noria. -- Paternoster while, the space of time required for repeating a paternoster. Udall.
