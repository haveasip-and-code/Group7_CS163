synclinal
1. Inclined downward from opposite directions, so as to meet in a common point or line. 2. (Geol.)  Formed by strata dipping toward a common line or plane; as, a synclinal trough or valley; a synclinal fold; -- opposed to anticlinal. Note: A downward flexure in the case of folded rocks makes a synclinal axis, and the alternating upward flexure an anticlinal axis.\n\nA synclinal fold.
