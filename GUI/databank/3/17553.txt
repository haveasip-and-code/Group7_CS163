inmate
One who lives in the same house or apartment with another; a fellow lodger; esp.,one of the occupants of an asylum, hospital, or prison; by extension, one who occupies or lodges in any place or dwelling. So spake the enemy of mankind, inclos'd In serpent, inmate bad. Milton.\n\nAdmitted as a dweller; resident; internal. [R.] \"Inmate guests.\" Milton.
