kitchen
1. A cookroom; the room of a house appropriated to cookery. Cool was his kitchen, though his brains were hot. Dryden. A fat kitchen makes a lean will. Franklin. 2. A utensil for roasting meat; as, a tin kitchen. Kitchen garden. See under Garden. -- Kitchen lee, dirty soapsuds. [Obs.] \" A brazen tub of kitchen lee.\" Ford. -- Kitchen stuff, fat collected from pots and pans. Donne.\n\nTo furnish food to; to entertain with the fare of the kitchen. [Obs.] Shak.
