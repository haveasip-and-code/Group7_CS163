missive
1. Specially sent; intended or prepared to be sent; as, a letter missive. Ayliffe. 2. Missile. \"The missive weapons fly.\" Dryden. Letters missive, letters conveying the permission, comand, or advice of a superior authority, as a sovereign. They are addressed and sent to some certain person or persons, and are distinguished from letters patent, which are addressed to the public.\n\n1. That which is sent; a writing containing a message. 2. One who is sent; a messenger. [Obs.] Shak.
