nomancy
The art or practice of divining the destiny of persons by the letters which form their names. NO-MAN'S LAND No\"-man's` land`. 1. (Naut.)  A space amidships used to keep blocks, ropes, etc.; a space on a ship belonging to no one in particular to care for. 2. Fig.: An unclaimed space or time. That no-man's land of twilight. W. Black.
