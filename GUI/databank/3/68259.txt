winter-rig
To fallow or till in winter. [Prov. Eng.] WINTER'S BARK Win\"ter's bark`. (Bot.)  The aromatic bark of tree (Drimys, or Drymis, Winteri) of the Magnolia family, which is found in Southern Chili. It was first used as a cure for scurvy by its discoverer, Captain John Winter, vice admiral to sir Francis Drake, in 1577.
