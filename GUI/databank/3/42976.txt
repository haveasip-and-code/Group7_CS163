shortsighted
1. Not able to see far; nearsighted; myopic. See Myopic, and Myopia. 2. Fig.: Not able to look far into futurity; unable to understand things deep; of limited intellect. 3. Having little regard for the future; heedless. -- Short\"sight`ed*ly, adv. -- Short\"sight`ed*ness, n. Cunning is a kind of shortsightedness. Addison.
