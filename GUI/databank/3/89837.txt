maybe
Perhaps; possibly; peradventure. Maybe the amorous count solicits her. Shak. In a liberal and, maybe, somewhat reckless way. Tylor.\n\nPossible; probable, but not sure. [R.] Then add those maybe years thou hast to live. Driden.\n\nPossibility; uncertainty. [R.] What they offer is mere maybe and shift. Creech.
