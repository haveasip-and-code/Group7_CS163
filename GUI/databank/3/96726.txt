rabid
1. Furious; raging; extremely violent. The rabid flight Of winds that ruin ships. Chapman. 2. Extreme, unreasonable, or fanatical in opinion; excessively zealous; as, a rabid socialist. 3. Affected with the distemper called rabies; mad; as, a rabid dog or fox. 4. (Med.) Of or pertaining to rabies, or hydrophobia; as, rabid virus.
