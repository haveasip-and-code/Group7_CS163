supper
A meal taken at the close of the day; the evening meal. Note: Supper is much used in an obvious sense, either adjectively or as the first part of a compound; as, supper time or supper-time, supper bell, supper hour, etc.\n\nTo take supper; to sup. [R.]\n\nTo supply with supper. [R.] \"Kester was suppering the horses.\" Mrs. Gaskell.
