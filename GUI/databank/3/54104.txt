bookish
1. Given to reading; fond of study; better acquainted with books than with men; learned from books. \"A bookish man.\" Addison. \"Bookish skill.\" Bp. Hall. 2. Characterized by a method of expression generally found in books; formal; labored; pedantic; as, a bookish way of talking; bookish sentences. -- Book\"ish*ly, adv. -- Book\"ish*ness, n.
