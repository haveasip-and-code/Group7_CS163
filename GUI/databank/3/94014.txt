pontifical
1. Of or pertaining to a pontiff, or high priest; as, pontifical authority; hence, belonging to the pope; papal. 2. Of or pertaining to the building of bridges. [R.] Now had they brought the work by wondrous art Pontifical, a ridge of pendent rock Over the vexed abyss. Milton.\n\n1. A book containing the offices, or formulas, used by a pontiff. South. 2. pl.  The dress and ornaments of a pontiff. \"Dressed in full pontificals.\" Sir W. Scott.
