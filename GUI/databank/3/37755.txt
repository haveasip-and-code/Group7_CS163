spirited
1. Animated or possessed by a spirit. [Obs.] \"So talked the spirited, sly snake.\" Milton. 2. Animated; full of life or vigor; lively; full of spirit or fire; as, a spirited oration; a spirited answer. Note: Spirited is much used in composition; as in high-spirited, low- spirited, mean-spirited, etc. Syn. -- Lively; vivacious; animated; ardent; active; bold; courageous. -- Spir\"it*ed*ly, adv. -- Spir\"it*ed*ness, n.
