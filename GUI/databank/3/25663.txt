capitulary
1. A capitular. 2. The body of laws or statutes of a chapter, or of an ecclesiastical council. 3. A collection of laws or statutes, civil and ecclesiastical, esp. of the Frankish kings, in chapters or sections. Several of Charlemagne's capitularies. Hallam.\n\nRelating to the chapter of a cathedral; capitular. \"Capitulary acts.\" Warton.
