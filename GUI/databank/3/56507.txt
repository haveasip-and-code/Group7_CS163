saturation
1. The act of saturating, or the state of being saturating; complete penetration or impregnation. 2. (Chem.)  The act, process, or result of saturating a substance, or of combining it to its fullest extent. 3. (Optics)  Freedom from mixture or dilution with white; purity; -- said of colors. Note: The degree of saturation of a color is its relative purity, or freedom from admixture with white.
