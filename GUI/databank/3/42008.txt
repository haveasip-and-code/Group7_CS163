overwork
1. To work beyond the strength; to cause to labor too much or too long; to tire excessively; as, to overwork a horse. 2. To fill too full of work; to crowd with labor. My days with toil are overwrought. Longfellow. 3. To decorate all over.\n\nTo work too much, or beyond one's strength.\n\nWork in excess of the usual or stipulated time or quantity; extra work; also, excessive labor.
