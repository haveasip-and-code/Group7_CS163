scaly
1. Covered or abounding with scales; as, a scaly fish. \"Scaly crocodile.\" Milton. 2. Resembling scales, laminæ, or layers. 3. Mean; low; as, a scaly fellow. [Low] 4. (Bot.)  Composed of scales lying over each other; as, a scaly bulb; covered with scales; as, a scaly stem. Scaly ant-eater (Zoöl.), the pangolin.
