uprise
1. To rise; to get up; to appear from below the horizon. \"Uprose the sun.\" Cowley. Uprose the virgin with the morning light. Pope. 2. To have an upward direction or inclination. Uprose the mystic mountain range. Tennyson.\n\nThe act of rising; appearance above the horizon; rising. [R.] Did ever raven sing so like a lark, That gives sweet tidings of the sun's uprise Shak.
