consociate
An associate; an accomplice. [Archaic] \"Wicked consociates.\" Bp. Hall.\n\n1. To bring into alliance, confederacy, or relationship; to bring together; to join; to unite. [R.] Join pole to pole, consociate severed worlds. Mallet. 2. To unite in an ecclesiastical consociation. [U.S.]\n\n1. To be allied, confederated, or associated; to coalescence. [R.] Bentley. 2. To form an ecclesiastical consociation. [U.S.]
