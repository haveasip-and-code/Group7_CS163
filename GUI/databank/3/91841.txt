stipple
1. To engrave by means of dots, in distinction from engraving in lines. The interlaying of small pieces can not altogether avoid a broken, stippled, spotty effect. Milman. 2. To paint, as in water colors, by small, short touches which together produce an even or softly graded surface.\n\nA mode of execution which produces the effect by dots or small points instead of lines. 2. (Paint.)  A mode of execution in which a flat or even tint is produced by many small touches.
