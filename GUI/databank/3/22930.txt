fleshly
1. Of or pertaining to the flesh; corporeal. \"Fleshly bondage.\" Denham. 2. Animal; not Dryden. 3. Human; not celestial; not spiritual or divine. \"Fleshly wisdom.\" 2 Cor. i. 12. Much ostentation vain of fleshly arm And fragile arms. Milton. 4. Carnal; wordly; lascivious. Abstain from fleshly lusts, which war against the soul. 1 Pet. ii. 11.\n\nIn a fleshly manner; carnally; lasciviously. [Obs.] Chaucer.
