devilish
1. Resembling, characteristic of, or pertaining to, the devil; diabolical; wicked in the extreme. \"Devilish wickedness.\" Sir P. Sidney. This wisdom descendeth not from above, but is earthly, sensual, devilish. James iii. 15. 2. Extreme; excessive. [Colloq.] Dryden. Syn. -- Diabolical; infernal; hellish; satanic; wicked; malicious; detestable; destructive. -- Dev\"il*ish*ly, adv. -- Dev\"il*ish*ness, n.
