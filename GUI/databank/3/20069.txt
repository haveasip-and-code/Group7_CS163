A rick. [Obs.] B. Jonson.\n\nVapor; steam; smoke; fume. As hateful to me as the reek of a limekiln. Shak.\n\nTo emit vapor, usually that which is warm and moist; to be full of fumes; to steam; to smoke; to exhale. Few chimneys reeking you shall espy. Spenser. I found me laid In balmy sweat, which with his beams the sun Soon dried, and on the reeking moisture fed. Milton. The coffee rooms reeked with tobacco. Macualay.
