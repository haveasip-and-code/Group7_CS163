denominative
1. Conferring a denomination or name. 2. (Logic)  Connotative; as, a denominative name. 3. Possessing, or capable of possessing, a distinct denomination or designation; denominable. The least denominative part of time is a minute. Cocker. 4. (Gram.)  Derived from a substantive or an adjective; as, a denominative verb.\n\nA denominative name or term; denominative verb. Jer. Taylor. Harkness.
