ravenous
1. Devouring with rapacious eagerness; furiously voracious; hungry even to rage; as, a ravenous wolf or vulture. 2. Eager for prey or gratification; as, a ravenous appetite or desire. -- Rav\"en*ous*ly, adv. -- Rav\"en*ous*ness, n. RAVEN'S-DUCK Ra\"ven's-duck`, n. Etym: [Cf. G. ravenstuch.]  A fine quality of sailcloth. Ham. Nav. Encyc.
