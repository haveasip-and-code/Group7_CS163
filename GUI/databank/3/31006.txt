scream
To cry out with a shrill voice; to utter a sudden, sharp outcry, or shrill, loud cry, as in fright or extreme pain; to shriek; to screech. I heard the owl scream and the crickets cry. Shak. And scream thyself as none e'er screamed before. Pope.\n\nA sharp, shrill cry, uttered suddenly, as in terror or in pain; a shriek; a screech. \"Screams of horror.\" Pope.
