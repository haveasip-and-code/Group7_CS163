foliaceous
1. (Bot.)  Belonging to, or having the texture or nature of, a leaf; having leaves intermixed with flowers; as, a foliaceous spike. 2. (Min.)  Consisting of leaves or thin laminæ; having the form of a leaf or plate; as, foliaceous spar. 3. (Zoöl.)  Leaflike in form or mode of growth; as, a foliaceous coral.
