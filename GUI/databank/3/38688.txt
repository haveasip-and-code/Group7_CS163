despotism
1. The power, spirit, or principles of a despot; absolute control over others; tyrannical sway; tyranny. \"The despotism of vice.\" Byron. 2. A government which is directed by a despot; a despotic monarchy; absolutism; autocracy. Despotism . . . is the only form of government which may with safety to itself neglect the education of its infant poor. Bp. Horsley.
