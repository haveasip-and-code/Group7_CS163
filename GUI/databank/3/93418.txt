panegyric
An oration or eulogy in praise of some person or achievement; a formal or elaborate encomium; a laudatory discourse; laudation. See Synonym of Eulogy.\n\nContaining praise or eulogy; encomiastic; laudatory. \"Panegyric strains.\" Pope. -- Pan`e*gyr\"ic*al*ly, adv. Some of his odes are panegyrical. Dryden.
