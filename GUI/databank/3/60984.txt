blarney
Smooth, wheedling talk; flattery. [Colloq.] Blarney stone, a stone in Blarney castle, Ireland, said to make those who kiss it proficient in the use of blarney.\n\nTo influence by blarney; to wheedle with smooth talk; to make or accomplish by blarney. \"Blarneyed the landlord.\" Irving. Had blarneyed his way from Long Island. S. G. Goodrich.
