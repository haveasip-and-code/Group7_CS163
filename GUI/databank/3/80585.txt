quadruple
Fourfold; as, to make quadruple restitution; a quadruple alliance. Quadruple time (Mus.), that in which each measure is divided into four equal parts.\n\nfour times the sum or number; a fourfold amount; as, to receive to quadruple of the amount in damages.\n\nTo multiply by four; to increase fourfold; to double; to double twice. A. Smith.\n\nTo be multiplied by four; to increase fourfold; to become four times as much.
