legendary
Of or pertaining to a legend or to legends; consisting of legends; like a legend; fabulous. \"Legendary writers.\" Bp. Lloyd. Legendary stories of nurses and old women. Bourne.\n\n1. A book of legends; a tale or parrative. Read the Countess of Pembroke's \"Arcadia,\" a gallant legendary full of pleasurable accidents. James I. 2. One who relates legends. Bp. Lavington.
