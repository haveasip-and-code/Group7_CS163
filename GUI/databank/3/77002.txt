lustful
1. Full of lust; excited by lust Spenser. Tillotson. 2. Exciting lust; characterized by lust or sensuality. \" Lustful orgies.\" Milton. 3. Strong; lusty. [Obs.] \" Lustful health.\" Sackville. Syn. -- sensual; fleshly; carnal; inordinate; licentious; lewd; unchaste; impure; libidinous; lecherous. -- Lust\"ful*ly, adv. -- Lust\"ful*ness, n.
