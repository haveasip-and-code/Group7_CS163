realty
1. Royalty. [Obs.] Chaucer. 2. Loyalty; faithfulness. [R.] Milton.\n\n1. Realty. [Obs.] Dr. H. More. 2. (Law) (a) Immobility, or the fixed, permanent nature of real property; as, chattels which savor of the realty; -- so written in legal language for reality. (b) Real estate; a piece of real property. Blackstone.
