preparative
Tending to prepare or make ready; having the power of preparing, qualifying, or fitting; preparatory. Laborious quest of knowledge preparative to this work. South.\n\n1. That which has the power of preparing, or previously fitting for a purpose; that which prepares. \"A preparative unto sermons.\" Hooker. 2. That which is done in the way of preparation. \"Necessary preparatives for our voyage.\" Dryden.
