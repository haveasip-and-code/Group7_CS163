fitch
1. (Bot.)  A vetch. [Obs.] 2. pl. (Bot.)  A word found in the Authorized Version of the Bible, representing different Hebrew originals. In Isaiah xxviii. 25, 27, it means the black aromatic seeds of Nigella sativa, still used as a flavoring in the East. In Ezekiel iv. 9, the Revised Version now reads spelt.\n\nThe European polecat; also, its fur.
