simular
One who pretends to be what he is not; one who, or that which, simulates or counterfeits something; a pretender. [Obs.] Shak. Christ calleth the Pharisees hypocrites, that is to say, simulars, and painted sepulchers. Tyndale.\n\nFalse; specious; counterfeit. [R. & Obs.] \"Thou simular man of virtue.\" Shak.
