remonstrance
1. The act of remonstrating; as: (a) A pointing out; manifestation; proof; demonstration. [Obs.] You may marvel why I . . . would not rather Make rash remonstrance of my hidden power Than let him be so lost. Shak. (b) Earnest presentation of reason in opposition to something; protest; expostulation. 2. (R.C.Ch.)  Same as Monstrance.
