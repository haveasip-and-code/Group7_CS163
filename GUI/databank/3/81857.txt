shale
1. A shell or husk; a cod or pod. \"The green shales of a bean.\" Chapman. 2. Etym: [G. shale.] (Geol.)  A fine-grained sedimentary rock of a thin, laminated, and often friable, structure. Bituminous shale. See under Bituminous.\n\nTo take off the shell or coat of; to shell. Life, in its upper grades, was bursting its shell, or was shaling off its husk. I. Taylor.
