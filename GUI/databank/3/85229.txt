saunter
To wander or walk about idly and in a leisurely or lazy manner; to lounge; to stroll; to loiter. One could lie under elm trees in a lawn, or saunter in meadows by the side of a stream. Masson. Syn. -- To loiter; linger; stroll; wander.\n\nA sauntering, or a sauntering place. That wheel of fops, that saunter of the town. Young.
