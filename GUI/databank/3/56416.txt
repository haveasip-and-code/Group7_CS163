skit
To cast reflections on; to asperse. [Prov. Eng. & Scot.] Crose.\n\n1. A reflection; a jeer or gibe; a sally; a brief satire; a squib. Tooke. A similar vein satire upon the emptiness of writers is given in his \"Tritical Essay upon the Faculties of the Human Mind;\" but that is a mere skit compared with this strange performance. Leslie Stephen. 2. A wanton girl; a light wench. [Obs.]
