flexure
1. The act of flexing or bending; a turning or curving; flexion; hence, obsequious bowing or bending. Will it give place to flexure and low bending Shak. 2. A turn; a bend; a fold; a curve. Varying with the flexures of the valley through which it meandered. British Quart. Rev. 3. (Zo√∂l.)  The last joint, or bend, of the wing of a bird. 4. (Astron.) The flexure of a curve (Math.), the bending of a curve towards or from a straight line.
