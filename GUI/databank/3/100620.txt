woodman
1. A forest officer appointed to take care of the king's woods; a forester. [Eng.] 2. A sportsman; a hunter. [The duke] is a better woodman than thou takest him for. Shak. 3. One who cuts down trees; a woodcutter. Woodman, spare that tree. G. P. Morris. 4. One who dwells in the woods or forest; a bushman.
