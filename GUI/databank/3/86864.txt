conscript
Enrolled; written; registered. Conscript fathers (Rom. Antiq.), the senators of ancient Rome. When certain new senators were first enrolled with the \"fathers\" the body was called Patres et Conscripti; afterward all were called Patres conscripti.\n\nOne taken by lot, or compulsorily enrolled, to serve as a soldier or sailor.\n\nTo enroll, by compulsion, for military service.
