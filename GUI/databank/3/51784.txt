energetic
1. Having energy or energies; possessing a capacity for vigorous action or for exerting force; active. \"A Being eternally energetic.\" Grew. 2. Exhibiting energy; operating with force, vigor, and effect; forcible; powerful; efficacious; as, energetic measures; energetic laws. Syn. -- Forcible; powerful; efficacious; potent; vigorous; effective; strenuous. -- En`er*get\"ic*al*ly, adv. -- En`er*get\"ic*al*ness, n.
