following
1. One's followers, adherents, or dependents, collectively. Macaulay. 2. Vocation; business; profession.\n\n1. Next after; succeeding; ensuing; as, the assembly was held on the following day. 2. (Astron.)  (In the field of a telescope) In the direction from which stars are apparently moving (in consequence of the erth's rotation); as, a small star, north following or south following. In the direction toward which stars appear to move is called preceding. Note: The four principal directions in the field of a telescope are north, south, following, preceding.
