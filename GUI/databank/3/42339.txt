remorseless
Being without remorse; having no pity; hence, destitute of sensibility; cruel; insensible to distress; merciless. \"Remorseless adversaries.\" South. \"With remorseless cruelty.\" Milton. Syn. -- Unpitying; pitiless; relentless; unrelenting; implacable; merciless; unmerciful; savage; cruel. -- Re*morse\"less*ly, adv. -- Re*morse\"less*ness, n.
