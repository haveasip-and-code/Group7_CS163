whimper
To cry with a low, whining, broken voice; to whine; to complain; as, a child whimpers. Was there ever yet preacher but there were gainsayers that spurned, that winced, that whimpered against him Latimer.\n\nTo utter in alow, whining tone.\n\nA low, whining, broken cry; a low, whining sound, expressive of complaint or grief.
