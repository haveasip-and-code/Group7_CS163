listing
1. The act or process of one who lists (in any sense of the verb); as, the listing of a door; the listing of a stock at the Stock Exchange. 2. The selvedge of cloth; list. 3. (Carp.)  The sapwood cut from the edge of a board. 4. (Agric.)  The throwing up of the soil into ridges, -- a method adopted in the culture of beets and some garden crops. [Local, U. S.]
