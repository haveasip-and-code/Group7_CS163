elongate
1. To lengthen; to extend; to stretch; as, to elongate a line. 2. To remove further off. [Obs.] Sir T. Browne.\n\nTo depart to, or be at, a distance; esp., to recede apparently from the sun, as a planet in its orbit. [R.]\n\nDrawn out at length; elongated; as, an elongate leaf. \"An elongate form.\" Earle.
