crick
The creaking of a door, or a noise resembling it. [Obs.] Johnson.\n\n1. A painful, spasmodic affection of the muscles of some part of the body, as of the neck or back, rendering it difficult to move the part. To those also that, with a crick or cramp, have thei necks drawn backward. Holland. 2. Etym: [Cf. F. cric.]  A small jackscrew. Knight.
