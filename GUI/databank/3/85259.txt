surly
1. Arrogant; haughty. [Obs.] Cotgrave. 2. Gloomily morose; ill-natured, abrupt, and rude; severe; sour; crabbed; rough; sullen; gloomy; as, a surly groom; a surly dog; surly language; a surly look. \"That surly spirit, melancholy.\" Shak. 3. Rough; dark; tempestuous. Now softened into joy the surly storm. Thomson.
