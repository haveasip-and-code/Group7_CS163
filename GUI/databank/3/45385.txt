piling
1. The act of heaping up. 2. (Iron Manuf.)  The process of building up, heating, and working, fagots, or piles, to form bars, etc.\n\nA series of piles; piles considered collectively; as, the piling of a bridge. Pug piling, sheet piles connected together at the edges by dovetailed tongues and grooves. -- Sheet piling, a series of piles made of planks or half logs driven edge to edge, -- used to form the walls of cofferdams, etc.
