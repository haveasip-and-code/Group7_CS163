starboard
That side of a vessel which is one of the right hand of a person who stands on board facing the bow; -- opposed to Ant: larboard, or Ant: port.\n\nPertaining to the right-hand side of a ship; being or lying on the right side; as, the starboard quarter; starboard tack.\n\nTo put to the right, or starboard, side of a vessel; as, to starboard the helm.
