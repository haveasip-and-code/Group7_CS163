sol
1. The sun. 2. (Alchem.)  Gold; -- so called from its brilliancy, color, and value. Chaucer.\n\n(a) A syllable applied in solmization to the note G, or to the fifth tone of any diatonic scale. (b) The tone itself.\n\n1. A sou. 2. A silver and gold coin of Peru. The silver sol is the unit of value, and is worth about 68 cents.
