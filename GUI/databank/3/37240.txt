rand
1. A border; edge; margin. [Obs. or Prov. Eng.] 2. A long, fleshy piece, as of beef, cut from the flank or leg; a sort of steak. Beau. & Fl. 3. A thin inner sole for a shoe; also, a leveling slip of leather applied to the sole before attaching the heel.\n\nTo rant; to storm. [Obs.] I wept, . . . and raved, randed, and railed. J. Webster.
