emasculate
1. To deprive of virile or procreative power; to castrate power; to castrate; to geld. 2. To deprive of masculine vigor or spirit; to weaken; to render effeminate; to vitiate by unmanly softness. Luxury had not emasculated their minds. V. Knox.\n\nDeprived of virility or vigor; unmanned; weak. \"Emasculate slave.\" Hammond.
