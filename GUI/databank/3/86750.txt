prosper
To favor; to render successful. \"Prosper thou our handiwork.\" Bk. of Common Prayer. All things concur toprosper our design. Dryden.\n\n1. To be successful; to succeed; to be fortunate or prosperous; to thrive; to make gain. They, in their earthly Canaan placed, Long time shall dwell and prosper. Milton. 2. To grow; to increase. [Obs.] Black cherry trees prosper even to considerable timber. Evelyn.
