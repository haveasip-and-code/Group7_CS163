waterproof
Proof against penetration or permeation by water; impervious to water; as, a waterproof garment; a waterproof roof.\n\n1. A substance or preparation for rendering cloth, leather, etc., impervious to water. 2. Cloth made waterproof, or any article made of such cloth, or of other waterproof material, as rubber; esp., an outer garment made of such material.\n\nTo render impervious to water, as cloth, leather, etc.
