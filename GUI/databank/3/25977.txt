maund
A hand basket. [Obs.] Herrick.\n\nAn East Indian weight, varying in different localities from 25 to about 82 pounds avoirdupois.\n\n1. To beg. [Obs.] B. Jonson. Beau. & Fl. 2. To mutter; to mumble; to grumble; to speak indistinctly or disconnectedly; to talk incoherently. He was ever maundering by the how that he met a party of scarlet devils. Sir W. Scott.
