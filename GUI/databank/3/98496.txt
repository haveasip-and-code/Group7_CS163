unformed
1. Decomposed, or resolved into parts; having the form destroyed. 2. Not formed; not arranged into regular shape, order, or relations; shapeless; amorphous. 3. (Biol.)  Unorganized; without definite shape or structure; as, an unformed, or unorganized, ferment. Unformed stars (Astron.), stars not grouped into any constellation; informed stars. See Sporades.
