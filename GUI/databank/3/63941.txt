cater
A provider; a purveyor; a caterer. [Obs.] Chaucer.\n\n1. To provide food; to buy, procure, or prepare provisions. [He] providently caters for the sparrow. Shak. 2. By extension: To supply what is needed or desired, at theatrical or musical entertainments; -- followed by for or to.\n\nThe four of cards or dice.\n\nTo cut diagonally. [Obs.] Halliwell.
