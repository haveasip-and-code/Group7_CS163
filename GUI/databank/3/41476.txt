instruction
1. The act of instructing, teaching, or furnishing with knowledge; information. 2. That which instructs, or with which one is instructed; the intelligence or information imparted; as: (a) Precept; information; teachings. (b) Direction; order; command. \"If my instructions may be your guide.\" Shak. Syn. -- Education; teaching; indoctrination; information; advice; counsel. See Education.
