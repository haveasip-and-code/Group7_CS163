suffocate
Suffocated; choked. Shak.\n\n1. To choke or kill by stopping respiration; to stifle; to smother. Let not hemp his windpipe suffocate. Shak. 2. To destroy; to extinguish; as, to suffocate fire.\n\nTo become choked, stifled, or smothered. \"A swelling discontent is apt to suffocate and strangle without passage.\" collier.
