pomp
1. A procession distinguished by ostentation and splendor; a pageant. \"All the pomps of a Roman triumph.\" Addison. 2. Show of magnificence; parade; display; power. Syn. -- Display; parade; pageant; pageantry; splendor; state; magnificence; ostentation; grandeur; pride.\n\nTo make a pompons display; to conduct. [Obs.] B. Jonson.
