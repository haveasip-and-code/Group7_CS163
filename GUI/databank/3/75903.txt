perdu
1. One placed on watch, or in ambush. 2. A soldier sent on a forlorn hope. Shak.\n\n1. Lost to view; in concealment or ambush; close. He should lie perdue who is to walk the round. Fuller. 2. Accustomed to, or employed in, desperate enterprises; hence, reckless; hopeless. \"A perdue captain.\" Beau. & Fl.
