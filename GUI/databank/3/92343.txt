gleek
1. A jest or scoff; a trick or deception. [Obs.] Where's the Bastard's braves, and Charles his gleeks Shak. 2. Etym: [Cf. Glicke]  An enticing look or glance. [Obs.] A pretty gleek coming from Pallas' eye. Beau. & Fl.\n\nTo make sport; to gibe; to sneer; to spend time idly. [Obs.] Shak.\n\n1. A game at cards, once popular, played by three persons. [Obs.] Pepys. Evelyn. 2. Three of the same cards held in the same hand; -- hence, three of anything. [Obs.]
