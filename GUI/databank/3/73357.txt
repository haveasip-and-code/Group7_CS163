fizzle
1. To make a hissing sound. It is the easfizzling. B. Jonson. 2. To make a ridiculous failure in an undertaking. [Colloq. or Low] To fizzle out, to burn with a hissing noise and then go out, like wet gunpowder; hence, to fail completely and ridicuously; to prove a failure. [Colloq.]\n\nA failure or abortive effort. [Colloq.]
