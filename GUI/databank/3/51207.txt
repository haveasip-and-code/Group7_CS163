tye
1. A knot; a tie. [R.] See Tie. 2. (Naut.)  A chain or rope, one end of which passes through the mast, and is made fast to the center of a yard; the other end is attached to a tackle, by means of which the yard is hoisted or lowered. 3. (Mining)  A trough for washing ores. Knight.\n\nSee Tie, the proper orthography.
