interpretation
1. The act of interpreting; explanation of what is obscure; translation; version; construction; as, the interpretation of a foreign language, of a dream, or of an enigma. Look how we can, or sad or merrily, Interpretation will misquote our looks. Shak. 2. (Fine Arts)  An artist's way of expressing his thought or embodying his conception of nature. 5. (Math.)
