nominate
1. To mention by name; to name. [Obs.] To nominate them all, it is impossible. Shak. 2. To call; to entitle; to denominate. [Obs.] Spenser. 3. To set down in express terms; to state. [Obs.] Is it so noiminated in the bond Shak. 4. To name, or designate by name, for an office or place; to appoint; esp., to name as a candidate for an election, choice, or appointment; to propose by name, or offer the name of, as a candidate for an office or place.
