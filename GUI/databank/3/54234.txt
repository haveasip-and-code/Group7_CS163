cosmic
1. Pertaining to the universe, and having special reference to universal law or order, or to the one grand harmonious system of things; hence; harmonious; orderly. 2. Pertaining to the solar system as a whole, and not to the earth alone. 3. Characteristic of the cosmos or universe; inconceivably great; vast; as, cosmic speed. \"Cosmic ranges of time.\" Tyndall. 4. (Astron.)  Rising or setting with the sun; -- the opposite of acronycal.
