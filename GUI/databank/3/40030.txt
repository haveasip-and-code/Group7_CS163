insuperable
Incapable of being passed over or surmounted; insurmountable; as, insuperable difficulties. And middle natures, how they long to join, Yet never pass the insuperable line Pope. The difficulty is enhanced, or is . . . insuperable. I. Taylor. Syn. -- Impassable; insurmountable; unconquerable. -- In*su\"per*a*ble*ness, n. -- In*su\"per*a*bly, adv.
