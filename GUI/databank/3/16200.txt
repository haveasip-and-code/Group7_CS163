corkwood
1. The wood of the cork oak. [Obs.] 2.  Any one of several trees or shrubs having light or corky wood; esp.: (a) In the United States, the tree Leitneria floridana. (b) In the West Indies: (1) Either of the cotton trees Ochroma lagopus and Pariti tiliaceum.  (2)  The tree producing the aligator apple. (3)  The blolly.
