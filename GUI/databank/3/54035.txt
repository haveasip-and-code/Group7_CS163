boundless
Without bounds or confines; illimitable; vast; unlimited. \"The boundless sky.\" Bryant. \"The boundless ocean.\" Dryden. \"Boundless rapacity.\" \"Boundless prospect of gain.\" Macaulay. Syn. -- Unlimited; unconfined; immeasurable; illimitable; infinite. -- Bound\"less*ly, adv. -- Bound\"less*ness, n.
