lather
1. Foam or froth made by soap moistened with water. 2. Foam from profuse sweating, as of a horse.\n\nTo spread over with lather; as, to lather the face.\n\nTo form lather, or a froth like lather; to accumulate foam from profuse sweating, as a horse.\n\nTo beat severely with a thong, strap, or the like; to flog. [Low]
