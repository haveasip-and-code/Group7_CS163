blacken
1. To make or render black. While the long funerals blacken all the way. Pope 2. To make dark; to darken; to cloud. \"Blackened the whole heavens.\" South. 3. To defame; to sully, as reputation; to make infamous; as, vice blackens the character. Syn. -- To denigrate; defame; vilify; slander; calumniate; traduce; malign; asperse.\n\nTo grow black or dark.
