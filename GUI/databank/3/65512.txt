cockney
1. An effeminate person; a spoilt child. \"A young heir or cockney, that is his mother's darling.\" Nash (1592). This great lubber, the world, will prove a cockney. Shak. 2. A native or resident of the city of London; -- used contemptuosly. A cockney in a rural village was stared at as much as if he had entered a kraal of Hottentots. Macaulay.\n\nOf or relating to, or like, cockneys.
