looking
Having a certain look or appearance; -- often compounded with adjectives; as, good-looking, grand-looking, etc.\n\n1. The act of one who looks; a glance. 2. The manner in which one looks; appearance; countenance; face. [Obs.] All dreary was his cheer and his looking. Chaucer. Looking for, anticipation; expectation. \"A certain fearful looking for of judgment.\" Heb. x. 27.
