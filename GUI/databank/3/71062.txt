ophite
Of or pertaining to a serpent. [Obs.]\n\nA greenish spotted porphyry, being a diabase whose pyroxene has been altered to uralite; -- first found in the Pyreness. So called from the colored spots which give it a mottled appearance. -- O*phi\"ic, a.\n\nA mamber of a Gnostic serpent-worshiping sect of the second century.
