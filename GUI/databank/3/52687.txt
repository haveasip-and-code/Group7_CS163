spike
1. A sort of very large nail; also, a piece of pointed iron set with points upward or outward. 2. Anything resembling such a nail in shape. He wears on his head the corona radiata . . . ; the spikes that shoot out represent the rays of the sun. Addison. 3. An ear of corn or grain. 4. (Bot.)  A kind of flower cluster in which sessile flowers are arranged on an unbranched elongated axis.
