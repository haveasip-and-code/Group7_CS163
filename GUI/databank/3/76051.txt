commodious
Adapted to its use or purpose, or to wants and necessities; serviceable; spacious and convenient; roomy and comfortable; as, a commodious house. \"A commodious drab.\" Shak. \"Commodious gold.\" Pope. The haven was not commodious to winter in. Acts. xxvii. 12. Syn. -- Convenient; suitable; fit; proper; advantageous; serviceable; useful; spacious; comfortable.
