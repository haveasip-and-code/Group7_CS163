pamphlet
1. A writing; a book. Testament of love. Sir Thomas More in his pamphlet of Richard the Third. Ascham. 2. A small book consisting of a few sheets of printed paper, stitched together, often with a paper cover, but not bound; a short essay or written discussion, usually on a subject of current interest.\n\nTo write a pamphlet or pamphlets. [R.] Howell.
