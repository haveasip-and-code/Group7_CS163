swanpan
The Chinese abacus; a schwanpan. S. W. Williams. SWAN'S-DOWN; SWANS-DOWN Swan's\"-down`, or; Swans\"-down`, n. 1. The down, or fine, soft feathers, of the swan, used on various articles of dress. 2. A fine, soft, thick cloth of wool mixed with silk or cotton; a sort of twilled fustian, like moleskin. Swan's-down cotton. See Cotton flannel, under Cotton.
