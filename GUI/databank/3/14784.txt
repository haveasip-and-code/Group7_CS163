bream
1. (Zoöl)  A European fresh-water cyprinoid fish of the genus Abramis, little valued as food. Several species are known. 2. (Zoöl)  An American fresh-water fish, of various species of Pomotis and allied genera, which are also called sunfishes and pondfishes. See Pondfish. 3. (Zoöl)  A marine sparoid fish of the genus Pagellus, and allied genera. See Sea Bream.\n\nTo clean, as a ship's bottom of adherent shells, seaweed, etc., by the application of fire and scraping.
