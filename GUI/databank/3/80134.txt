interlocutory
1. Consisting of, or having the nature of, dialogue; conversational. Interlocutory discourses in the Holy Scriptures. Fiddes. 2. (Law)  Intermediate; not final or definitive; made or done during the progress of an action. Note: An order, sentence, decree, or judgment, given in an intermediate stage between the commencement and termination of a cause, is called interlocutory.\n\nInterpolated discussion or dialogue.
