yonder
At a distance, but within view. Yonder are two apple women scolding. Arbuthnot.\n\nBeing at a distance within view, or conceived of as within view; that or those there; yon. \"Yon flowery arbors, yonder alleys green.\" Milton. \"Yonder sea of light.\" Keble. Yonder men are too many for an embassage. Bacon.
