coma
A state of profound insensibility from which it is difficult or impossible to rouse a person. See Carus.\n\n1. (Astron.)  The envelope of a comet; a nebulous covering, which surrounds the nucleus or body of a comet. 2. (Bot.)  A tuft or bunch, -- as the assemblage of branches forming the head of a tree; or a cluster of brachts when empty and terminating the inflorescence of a plant; or a tuft of long hairs on certain seeds. Coma Berenices ( Etym: [L.] (Astron.), a small constellation north of Virgo; -- called also Berenice's Hair.
