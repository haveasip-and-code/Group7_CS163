dermoptera
1. (Zoöl.)  The division of insects which includes the earwigs (Forticulidæ). 2. (Zoöl.)  A group of lemuroid mammals having a parachutelike web of skin between the fore and hind legs, of which the colugo (Galeopithecus) is the type. See Colugo. 3. (Zoöl.)  An order of Mammalia; the Cheiroptera. [Written also Dermaptera, and Dermatoptera.]
