wizard
1. A wise man; a sage. [Obs.] See how from far upon the eastern road The star-led wizards [Magi] haste with odors sweet! Milton. 2. One devoted to the black art; a magician; a conjurer; a sorcerer; an enchanter. The wily wizard must be caught. Dryden.\n\n1. Enchanting; charming. Collins. 2. Haunted by wizards. Where Deva spreads her wizard stream. Milton.
