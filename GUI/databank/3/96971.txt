participate
Acting in common; participating. [R.] Shak.\n\nTj have a share in common with others; to take a part; to partake; -- followed by in, formely by of; as, to participate in a debate. Shak. So would he participateof their wants. Hayward. Mine may come when men With angels may participate. Milton.\n\n1. To partake of; to share in; to receive a part of. [R.] Fit to participate all rational delight. Milton. 2. To impart, or give, or share of. [Obs.] Drayton.
