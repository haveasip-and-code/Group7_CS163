incredulous
1. Not credulous; indisposed to admit or accept that which is related as true, skeptical; unbelieving. Bacon. A fantastical incredulous fool. Bp. Wilkins. 2. Indicating, or caused by, disbelief or incredulity. \"An incredulous smile.\" Longfellow. 3. Incredible; not easy to be believed. [R.] Shak.
