mareschal
A military officer of high rank; a marshal. [Obs.] MARE'S-NEST Mare's\"-nest`, n.  A supposed discovery which turns out to be a hoax; something grosaly absurd. MARE'S-TAIL Mare's\"-tail`, n. 1. A long streaky cloud, spreading out like a horse's tail, and believed to indicate rain; a cirrus cloud. See Cloud. Mackerel sky and mare's-tails Make tall ships carry low sails. Old Rhyme. 2. (Bot.)  An aquatic plant of the genus Hippuris (H.vulgaris), having narrow leaves in whorls.
