warish
To protect from the effects of; hence, to cure; to heal. [Obs.] My brother shall be warished hastily. Chaucer. Varro testifies that even at this day there be some who warish and cure the stinging of serpents with their spittle. Holland.\n\nTo be cured; to recover. [Obs.] Your daughter . . . shall warish and escape. Chaucer.
