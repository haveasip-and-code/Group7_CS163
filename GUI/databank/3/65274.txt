spew
1. To eject from the stomach; to vomit. 2. To cast forth with abhorrence or disgust; to eject. Because thou art lukewarm, and neither hot nor cold, I will spew thee out of my mouth. Rev. ii. 16.\n\n1. To vomit. Chaucer. 2. To eject seed, as wet land swollen with frost.\n\nThat which is vomited; vomit.
