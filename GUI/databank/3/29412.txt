springer
1. One who, or that which, springs; specifically, one who rouses game. 2. A young plant. [Obs.] Evelyn. 3. (Arch.) (a) The impost, or point at which an arch rests upon its support, and from which it seems to spring. Hence: (b) The bottom stone of an arch, which lies on the impost. The skew back is one form of springer. (c) The rib of a groined vault, as being the solid abutment for each section of vaulting. 4. (Zoöl.)  The grampus. 5. (Zoöl.)  A variety of the field spaniel. See Spaniel. 6. (Zoöl.)  A species of antelope; the sprinkbok.
