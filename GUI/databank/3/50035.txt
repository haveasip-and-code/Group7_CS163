trice
1. To pull; to haul; to drag; to pull away. [Obs.] Out of his seat I will him trice. Chaucer. 2. (Naut.)  To haul and tie up by means of a rope.\n\nA very short time; an instant; a moment; -- now used only in the phrase in a trice. \"With a trice.\" Turbervile. \" On a trice.\" Shak. A man shall make his fortune in a trice. Young.
