slouch
1. A hanging down of the head; a drooping attitude; a limp appearance; an ungainly, clownish gait; a sidewise depression or hanging down, as of a hat brim. 2. An awkward, heavy, clownish fellow. [Colloq.] Slouth hat, a soft, limp hat of unstiffened cloth or felt.\n\n1. To droop, as the head. 2. To walk in a clumsy, lazy manner. [Colloq.]\n\nTo cause to hang down; to depress at the side; as, to slouth the hat.
