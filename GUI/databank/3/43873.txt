loneliness
1. The condition of being lonely; solitude; seclusion. 2. The state of being unfrequented by human beings; as, the loneliness of a road. 3. Love of retirement; disposition to solitude. I see The mystery of your loneliness. Shak. 4. A feeling of depression resulting from being alone. Syn. -- Solitude; seclusion. See Solitude.
