muss
A scramble, as when small objects are thrown down, to be taken by those who can seize them; a confused struggle. Shak.\n\nA state of confusion or disorder; -- prob. variant of mess, but influenced by muss, a scramble. [Colloq. U.S.]\n\nTo disarrange, as clothing; to rumple. [Colloq. U.S.]\n\nA term of endearment. [Obs.] See Mouse. B. Jonson.
