rung
imp. & p. p. of Ring.\n\n1. (Shipbuilding)  A floor timber in a ship. 2. One of the rounds of a ladder. 3. One of the stakes of a cart; a spar; a heavy staff. 4. (Mach.)  One of the radial handles projecting from the rim of a steering wheel; also, one of the pins or trundles of a lantern wheel.
