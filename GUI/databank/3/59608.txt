unquestioned
1. Not called in question; not doubted. 2. Not interrogated; having no questions asked; not examined or examined into. Shak. She muttering prayers, as holy rites she meant, Through the divided crowd unquestioned went. Dryden. 3. Indisputable; not to be opposed or impugned. Their unquestioned pleasures must be served. B. Jonson.
