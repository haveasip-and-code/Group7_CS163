catchy
1. Apt or tending to catch the fancy or attention; catching; taking; as, catchy music. 2.  Tending to catch or insnare; entangling; -- usually used fig.; as, a catchy question. 3.  Consisting of, or occuring in, disconnected parts or snatches; changeable; as, a catchy wind. It [the fox's scent] is . . . flighty or catchy, if variable. Encyc. of Sport.
