chaser
1. One who or that which chases; a pursuer; a driver; a hunter. 2. (Naut.)  Same as Chase gun, esp. in terms bow chaser and stern chaser. See under Bow, Stern.\n\n1. One who chases or engraves. See 5th Chase, and Enchase. 2. (Mech.)  A tool with several points, used for cutting or finishing screw threads, either external or internal, on work revolving in a lathe.
