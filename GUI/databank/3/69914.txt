disinterest
Disinterested. [Obs.] The measures they shall walk by shall be disinterest and even. Jer. Taylor.\n\n1. What is contrary to interest or advantage; disadvantage. [Obs.] Glanvill. 2. Indifference to profit; want of regard to private advantage; disinterestedness. [Obs.] Johnson.\n\nTo divest of interest or interested motives. [Obs.] Feltham.
