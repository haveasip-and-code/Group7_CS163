drowsy
1. Inclined to drowse; heavy with sleepiness; lethargic; dozy. \"When I am drowsy.\" Shak. Dapples the drowsy east with spots of gray. Shak. To our age's drowsy blood Still shouts the inspiring sea. Lowell. 2. Disposing to sleep; lulling; soporific. The drowsy hours, dispensers of all good. Tennyson. 3. Dull; stupid. \" Drowsy reasoning.\" Atterbury. Syn. -- Sleepy; lethargic; dozy; somnolent; comatose; dull heavy; stupid.
