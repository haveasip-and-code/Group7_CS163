forehand
1. All that part of a horse which is before the rider. Johnson. 2. The chief or most important part. Shak. 3. Superiority; advantage; start; precedence. And, but for ceremony, such a wretch . . . Had the forehand and vantage of a king. Shak.\n\nDone beforehand; anticipative. And so extenuate the forehand sin. Shak.
