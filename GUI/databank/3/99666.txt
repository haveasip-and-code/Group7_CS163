confectioner
1. A compounder. [Obs.] Canidia Neapolitana was confectioner of unguents. Haywood. 2. One whose occupation it is to make or sell confections, candies, etc. CONFECTIONERS' SUGAR Con*fec\"tion*ers' sug`ar.  A highly refined sugar in impalpable powder, esp. suited to confectioners' uses.
