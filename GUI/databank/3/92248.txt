feat
1. An act; a deed; an exploit. The warlike feats I have done. Shak. 2. A striking act of strength, skill, or cunning; a trick; as, feats of horsemanship, or of dexterity.\n\nTo form; to fashion. [Obs.] To the more mature, A glass that feated them. Shak.\n\nDexterous in movements or service; skillful; neat; nice; pretty. [Archaic] Never master had a page . . . so feat. Shak. And look how well my garments sit upon me --Much feater than before. Shak.
