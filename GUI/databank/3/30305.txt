replication
1. An answer; a reply. Shak. Withouten any repplicacioun. Chaucer. 2. (Law Pleadings)  The reply of the plaintiff, in matters of fact, to the defendant's plea. 3. Return or repercussion, as of sound; echo. To hear the replication of your sounds. Shak. 4. A repetition; a copy. Farrar. Syn. -- Answer; response; reply; rejoinder.
