disinclination
The state of being disinclined; want of propensity, desire, or affection; slight aversion or dislike; indisposition. Disappointment gave him a disinclination to the fair sex. Arbuthnot. Having a disinclination to books or business. Guardian. Syn. -- Unwillingness; disaffection; alienation; dislike; indisposition; distaste; aversion; repugnance.
