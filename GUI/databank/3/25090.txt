lamentable
1. Mourning; sorrowful; expressing grief; as, a lamentable countenance. \"Lamentable eye.\" Spenser. 2. Fitted to awaken lament; to be lamented; sorrowful; pitiable; as, a lamentable misfortune, or error. \"Lamentable helplessness.\" Burke. 3. Miserable; pitiful; paltry; -- in a contemptuous or Bp. Stillingfleet. -- Lam\"en*ta*ble*ness, n. -- Lam\"en*ta*bly, adv.
