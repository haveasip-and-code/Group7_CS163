knowing
1. Skilful; well informed; intelligent; as, a knowing man; a knowing dog. The knowing and intelligent part of the world. South. 2. Artful; cunning; as, a knowing rascal. [Colloq.]\n\nKnowledge; hence, experience. \" In my knowing.\" Shak. This sore night Hath trifled former knowings. Shak.
