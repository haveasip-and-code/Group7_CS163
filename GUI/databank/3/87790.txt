listless
Having no desire or inclination; indifferent; heedless; spiritless. \" A listless unconcern.\" Thomson. Benumbed with cold, and listless of their gain. Dryden. I was listless, and desponding. Swift. Syn. -- Heedless; careless; indifferent; vacant; uninterested; languid; spiritless; supine; indolent. -- List\"less*ly, adv. -- List\"less*ness, n.
