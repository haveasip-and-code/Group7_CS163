courtly
1. Relating or belonging to a court. 2. Elegant; polite; courtlike; flattering. In courtly company or at my beads. Shak. 3. Disposed to favor the great; favoring the policy or party of the court; obsequious. Macualay.\n\nIn the manner of courts; politely; gracefully; elegantly. They can produce nothing so courtly writ. Dryden
