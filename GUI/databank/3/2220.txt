bosh
Figure; outline; show. [Obs.]\n\nEmpty talk; contemptible nonsense; trash; humbug. [Colloq.]\n\n1. One of the sloping sides of the lower part of a blast furnace; also, one of the hollow iron or brick sides of the bed of a puddling or boiling furnace. 2. pl.  The lower part of a blast furnace, which slopes inward, or the widest space at the top of this part. 3. In forging and smelting, a trough in which tools and ingots are cooled.
