sty
1. A pen or inclosure for swine. 2. A place of bestial debauchery. To roll with pleasure in a sensual sty. Milton.\n\nTo shut up in, or as in, a sty. Shak.\n\nTo soar; to ascend; to mount. See Stirrup. [Obs.] With bolder wing shall dare aloft to sty, To the last praises of this Faery Queene. Spenser.\n\nAn inflamed swelling or boil on the edge of the eyelid. [Written also stye.]
