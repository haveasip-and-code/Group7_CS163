gobble
1. To swallow or eat greedily or hastily; to gulp. Supper gobbled up in haste. Swift. 2. To utter (a sound) like a turkey cock. He . . . gobbles out a note of self-approbation. Goldsmith. To gobble up, to capture in a mass or in masses; to capture suddenly. [Slang]\n\n1. To eat greedily. 2. To make a noise like that of a turkey cock. Prior.\n\nA noise made in the throat. Ducks and geese . . . set up a discordant gobble. Mrs. Gore.
