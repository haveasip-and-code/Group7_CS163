perspicuous
1. Capable of being through; transparent; translucent; not opaque. [Obs.] Peacham. 2. Clear to the understanding; capable of being clearly understood; clear in thought or in expression; not obscure or ambiguous; as, a perspicuous writer; perspicuous statements. \"The purpose is perspicuous.\" Shak. -- Per*spic\"u*ous*ly, adv. -- Per*spic\"u*ous*ness, n.
