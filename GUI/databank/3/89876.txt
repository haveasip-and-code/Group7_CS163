quiver
Nimble; active. [Obs.] \" A little quiver fellow.\" Shak.\n\nTo shake or move with slight and tremulous motion; to tremble; to quake; to shudder; to shiver. The green leaves quiver with the cooling wind. Shak. And left the limbs still quivering on the ground. Addison.\n\nThe act or state of quivering; a tremor.\n\nA case or sheath for arrows to be carried on the person. Reside him hung his bow And quiver, with three-bolted thunder stored. Milton.
