literator
1. One who teaches the letters or elements of knowledge; a petty schoolmaster. Burke. 2. A person devoted to the study of literary trifles, esp. trifles belonging to the literature of a former age. That class of subjects which are interesting to the regular literator or black-letter \" bibliomane,\" simply because they have once been interesting. De Quincey. 3. A learned person; a literatus. Sir W. Hamilton.
