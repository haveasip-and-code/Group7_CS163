matriculate
To enroll; to enter in a register; specifically, to enter or admit to membership in a body or society, particularly in a college or university, by enrolling the name in a register. In discovering and matriculating the arms of commissaries from North America. Sir W. Scott.\n\nTo go though the process of admission to membership, as by examination and enrollment, in a society or college.\n\nMatriculated. Skelton. -- n.  One who is matriculated. Arbuthnot.
