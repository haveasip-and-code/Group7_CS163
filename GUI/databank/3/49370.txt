unravel
1. To disentangle; to disengage or separate the threads of; as, to unravel a stocking. 2. Hence, to clear from complication or difficulty; to unfold; to solve; as, to unravel a plot. 3. To separate the connected or united parts of; to throw into disorder; to confuse. \"Art shall be conjured for it, and nature all unraveled.\" Dryden.\n\nTo become unraveled, in any sense.
