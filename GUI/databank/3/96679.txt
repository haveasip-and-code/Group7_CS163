trine
Threefold; triple; as, trine dimensions, or length, breadth, and thickness.\n\n1. (Astrol.)  The aspect of planets distant from each other 120 degrees, or one third of the zodiac; trigon. In sextile, square, and trine. Milton. 2. A triad; trinity. [R.] A single trine of brazen tortoises. Mrs. Browning. Eternal One, Almighty Trine! Keble.\n\nTo put in the aspect of a trine. [R.] By fortune he [Saturn] was now to Venus trined. Dryden.
