coronary
1. Of or pertaining to a crown; ferming, or adapted to form, a crown or garland. \"Coronary thorns.\" Bp. Pearson. The catalogue of coronary plants is not large in Theophrastus. Sir T. Browne. 2. (Anat.)  Resembling, or situated like, a crown or circlet; as, the coronary arteries and veins of the heart.\n\nA small bone in the foot of a horse.
