muck
, abbreviation of Amuck. To run a muck. See Amuck.\n\n1. Dung in a moist state; manure. Bacon. 2. Vegetable mold mixed with earth, as found in low, damp places and swamps. 3. Anything filthy or vile. Spenser. 4. Money; -- in contempt. The fatal muck we quarreled for. Beau. & Fl. Muck bar, bar iron which has been through the rolls only once. -- Muck iron, crude puddled iron ready for the squeezer or rollers. Knight.\n\nLike muck; mucky; also, used in collecting or distributing muck; as, a muck fork.\n\nTo manure with muck.
