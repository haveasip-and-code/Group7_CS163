voiding
1. The act of one who, or that which, v Bp. Hall. 2. That which is voided; that which is ejected or evacuated; a remnant; a fragment. [R.] Rowe. Voiding knife, a knife used for gathering up fragments of food to put them into a voider.\n\nReceiving what is ejected or voided. \"How in our voiding lobby hast thou stood\" Shak.
