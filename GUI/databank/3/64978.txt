reprise
1. A taking by way of retaliation. [Obs.] Dryden. 2. pl. (Law)  Deductions and duties paid yearly out of a manor and lands, as rent charge, rent seck, pensions, annuities, and the like. [Written also reprizes.] Burrill. 3. A ship recaptured from an enemy or from a pirate.\n\n1. To take again; to retake. [Obs.] Spenser. 2. To recompense; to pay. [Obs.]
