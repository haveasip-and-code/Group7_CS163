estuary
1. A place where water boils up; a spring that wells forth. [Obs.] Boyle. 2. A passage, as the mouth of a river or lake, where the tide meets the current; an arm of the sea; a frith. it to the sea was often by long and wide estuaries. Dana.\n\nBelonging to, or formed in, an estuary; as, estuary strata. Lyell.
