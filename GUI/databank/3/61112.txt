intonate
To thunder. [Obs.] Bailey.\n\n1. (Mus.)  To sound the tones of the musical scale; to practice the sol- fa. 2. To modulate the voice in a musical, sonorous, and measured manner, as in reading the liturgy; to intone.\n\nTo utter in a musical or sonorous manner; to chant; as, to intonate the liturgy.
