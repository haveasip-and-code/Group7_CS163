halter
One who halts or limps\n\nA strong strap or cord. Especially: (a) A rope or strap, with or without a headstall, for leading or tying a horse. (b) A rope for hanging malefactors; a noose. Shak. No man e'er felt the halter draw With good opinion of the law. Trumbull.\n\nTo tie by the neck with a rope, strap, or halter; to put a halter on; to subject to a hangman's halter. \"A haltered neck.\" Shak.
