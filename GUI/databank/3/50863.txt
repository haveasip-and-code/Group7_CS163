apologize
1. To make an apology or defense. Dr. H. More. 2. To make an apology or excuse; to make acknowledgment of some fault or offense, with expression of regret for it, by way of amends; -- with for; as, my correspondent apologized for not answering my letter. To apologize for his insolent language. Froude.\n\nTo defend. [Obs.] The Christians . . . were apologized by Plinie. Dr. G. Benson.
