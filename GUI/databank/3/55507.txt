anamorphosis
1. (Persp.)  A distorted or monstrous projection or representation of an image on a plane or curved surface, which, when viewed from a certain point, or as reflected from a curved mirror or through a polyhedron, appears regular and in proportion; a deformation of an image. 2. (Biol.)  Same as Anamorphism, 2. 3. (Bot.)  A morbid or monstrous development, or change of form, or degeneration.
