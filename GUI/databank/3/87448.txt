harbinger
1. One who provides lodgings; especially, the officer of the English royal household who formerly preceded the court when traveling, to provide and prepare lodgings. Fuller. 2. A forerunner; a precursor; a messenger. I knew by these harbingers who were coming. Landor.\n\nTo usher in; to be a harbinger of. \"Thus did the star of religious freedom harbinger the day.\" Bancroft.
