wail
To choose; to select. [Obs.] \"Wailed wine and meats.\" Henryson.\n\nTo lament; to bewail; to grieve over; as, to wail one's death. Shak.\n\nTo express sorrow audibly; to make mournful outcry; to weep. Therefore I will wail and howl. Micah i. 8.\n\nLoud weeping; violent lamentation; wailing. \"The wail of the forest.\" Longfellow.
