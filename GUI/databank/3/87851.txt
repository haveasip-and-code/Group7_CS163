nominal
1. Of or pertaining to a name or names; having to do with the literal meaning of a word; verbal; as, a nominal definition. Bp. Pearson. 2. Existing in name only; not real; as, a nominal difference. \"Nominal attendance on lectures.\" Macaulay.\n\n1. A nominalist. [Obs.] Camden. 2. (Gram.)  A verb formed from a noun. 3. A name; an appellation. A is the nominal of the sixth note in the natural diatonic scale. Moore (Encyc. of Music. )
