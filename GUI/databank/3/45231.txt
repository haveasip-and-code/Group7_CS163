holder
One who is employed in the hold of a vessel.\n\n1. One who, or that which, holds. 2. One who holds land, etc., under another; a tenant. 3. (Com.)  The payee of a bill of exchange or a promissory note, or the one who owns or holds it. Note: Holder is much used as the second part of a compound; as, shareholder, officeholder, stockholder,etc.
