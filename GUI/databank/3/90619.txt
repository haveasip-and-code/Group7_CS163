puisne
1. Later in age, time, etc.; subsequent. [Obs.] \" A puisne date to eternity.\" Sir M. Hale. 2. Puny; petty; unskilled. [Obs.] 3. (Law)  Younger or inferior in rank; junior; associate; as, a chief justice and three puisne justices of the Court of Common Pleas; the puisne barons of the Court of Exchequer. Blackstone.\n\nOne who is younger, or of inferior rank; a junior; esp., a judge of inferior rank. It were not a work for puisnes and novices. Bp. Hall.
