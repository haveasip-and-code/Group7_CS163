stope
A horizontal working forming one of a series, the working faces of which present the appearance of a flight of steps.\n\n(a) To excavate in the form of stopes. (b) To fill in with rubbish, as a space from which the ore has been worked out.\n\nStepped; gone; advanced. [Obs.] A poor widow, somedeal stope in age. Chaucer.
