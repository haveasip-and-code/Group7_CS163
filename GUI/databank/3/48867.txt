vinculum
1. A bond of union; a tie. 2. (Math.)  A straight, horizontal mark placed over two or more members of a compound quantity, which are to be subjected to the same operation, as in the expression x2 + y2 - x + y. 3. (Anat.)  A band or bundle of fibers; a frænum. 4. (Zoöl.)  A commissure uniting the two main tendons in the foot of certain birds.
