coagulate
Coagulated. [Obs.] Shak.\n\nTo cause (a liquid) to change into a curdlike or semisolid state, not by evaporation but by some kind of chemical reaction; to curdle; as, rennet coagulates milk; heat coagulates the white of an egg.\n\nTo undergo coagulation. Boyle. Syn. -- To thicken; concrete; curdle; clot; congeal.
