corny
Strong, stiff, or hard, like a horn; resembling horn. Up stood the cornu reed. Milton.\n\n1. Producing corn or grain; furnished with grains of corn. [R.] \"The corny ear.\" Prior. 2. Containing corn; tasting well of malt. [R.] A draught of moist and corny ale. Chaucer. 3. Tipsy. [Vulgar, Eng.] Forby.
