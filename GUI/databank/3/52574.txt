cambrian
1. (Geog.)  Of or pertaining to Cambria or Wales. 2. (Geol.)  Of or pertaining to the lowest subdivision of the rocks of the Silurian or Molluscan age; -- sometimes described as inferior to the Silurian. It is named from its development in Cambria or Wales. See the Diagram under Geology.\n\n1. A native of Cambria or Wales. 2. (Geol.)  The Cambrian formation.
