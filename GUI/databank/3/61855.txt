transalpine
Being on the farther side of the Alps in regard to Rome, that is, on the north or west side of the Alps; of or pertaining to the region or the people beyond the Alps; as, transalpine Gaul; -- opposed to cisalpine. \" Transalpine garbs.\" Beau. & Fl.\n\nA native or inhabitant of a country beyond the Alps, that is, out of Italy.
