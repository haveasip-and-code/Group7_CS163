faithless
1. Not believing; not giving credit. Be not faithless, but believing. John xx. 27. 2. Not believing on God or religion; specifically, not believing in the Christian religion. Shak. 3. Not observant of promises or covenants. 4. Not true to allegiance, duty, or vows; perfidious; trecherous; disloyal; not of true fidelity; inconstant, as a husband or a wife. A most unnatural and faithless service. Shak. 5. Serving to disappoint or deceive; delusive; unsatisfying. \"Yonder faithless phantom.\" Goldsmith. -- Faith\"less*ly, adv.Faith\"less*ness, n.
