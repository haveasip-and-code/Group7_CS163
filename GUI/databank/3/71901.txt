ordinal
1. Indicating order or succession; as, the ordinal numbers, first, second, third, etc. 2. Of or pertaining to an order.\n\n1. A word or number denoting order or succession. 2. (Ch. of Eng.)  The book of forms for making, ordaining, and consecrating bishops, priests, and deacons. 3. (R. C. Ch.)  A book containing the rubrics of the Mass. [Written also ordinale.]
