metropolitan
1. Of or pertaining to the capital or principal city of a country; as, metropolitan luxury. 2. (Eccl.)  Of, pertaining to, or designating, a metropolitan or the presiding bishop of a country or province, his office, or his dignity; as, metropolitan authority. \"Bishops metropolitan.\" Sir T. More.\n\n1. The superior or presiding bishop of a country or province. 2. (Lat. Church.)  An archbishop. 3. (Gr. Church)  A bishop whose see is civil metropolis. His rank is intermediate between that of an archbishop and a patriarch. Hook.
