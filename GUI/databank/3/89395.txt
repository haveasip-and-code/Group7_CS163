badge
1. A distinctive mark, token, sign, or cognizance, worn on the person; as, the badge of a society; the badge of a policeman. \"Tax gatherers, recognized by their official badges. \" Prescott. 2. Something characteristic; a mark; a token. Sweet mercy is nobility's true badge. Shak. 3. (Naut.)  A carved ornament on the stern of a vessel, containing a window or the representation of one.\n\nTo mark or distinguish with a badge.
