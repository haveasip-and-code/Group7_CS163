inhospitable
1. Not hospitable; not disposed to show hospitality to strangers or guests; as, an inhospitable person or people. Have you no touch of pity, that the poor Stand starved at your inhospitable door Cowper. 2. Affording no shelter or sustenance; barren; desert; bleak; cheerless; wild. \"Inhospitable wastes.\" Blair. -- In*hos\"pi*ta*ble*mess, n. -- In*hos\"pi*ta*bly, adv.
