parage
1. (Old Eng. Law)  Equality of condition, blood, or dignity; also, equality in the partition of an inheritance. Spelman. 2. (Feudal Law)  Equality of condition between persons holding unequal portions of a fee. Burrill. 3. Kindred; family; birth. [Obs.] Ld. Berners. We claim to be of high parage. Chaucer.
