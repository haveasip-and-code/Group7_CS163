ill-natured
1. Of habitual bad temper; peevish; fractious; cross; crabbed; surly; as, an ill-natured person. 2. Dictated by, or indicating, ill nature; spiteful. \"The ill-natured task refuse.\" Addison. 3. Intractable; not yielding to culture. [R.] \"Ill-natured land.\" J. Philips. -- Ill`-na\"tured*ly, adv. -- Ill`-na\"tured*ness, n.
