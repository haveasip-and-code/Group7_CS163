depositary
1. One with whom anything is lodged in the trust; one who receives a deposit; -- the correlative of depositor. I . . . made you my guardians, my depositaries. Shak. The depositaries of power, who are mere delegates of the people.J.S. Mill. 2. A storehouse; a depository. Bp. Hurd. 3. (Law)  One to whom goods are bailed, to be kept for the bailor without a recompense. Kent.
