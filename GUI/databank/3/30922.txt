doctrinal
1. Pertaining to, or containing, doctrine or something taught and to be believed; as, a doctrinal observation. \"Doctrinal clauses.\" Macaulay. 2. Pertaining to, or having to do with, teaching. The word of God serveth no otherwise than in the nature of a doctrinal instrument. Hooker.\n\nA matter of doctrine; also, a system of doctrines. T. Goodwin. Sir T. Elyot.
