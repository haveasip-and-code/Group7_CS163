reem
The Hebrew name of a horned wild animal, probably the Urus. Note: In King James's Version it is called unicorn; in the Revised Version,wild ox. Job xxxix. 9.\n\nTo open (the seams of a vessel's planking) for the purpose of calking them. Reeming iron (Naut.), an iron chisel for reeming the seams of planks in calking ships.
