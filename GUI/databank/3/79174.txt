piecemeal
1. In pieces; in parts or fragments. \"On which it piecemeal brake.\" Chapman. The beasts will tear thee piecemeal. Tennyson. 2. Piece by piece; by little and little in succession. Piecemeal they win, this acre first, than that. Pope.\n\nMade up of parts or pieces; single; separate. \"These piecemeal guilts.\" Gov. of Tongue.\n\nA fragment; a scrap. R. Vaughan.
