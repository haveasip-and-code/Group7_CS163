regal
Of or pertaining to a king; kingly; royal; as, regal authority, pomp, or sway. \"The regal title.\" Shak. He made a scorn of his regal oath. Milton. Syn. -- Kingly; royal. See Kingly.\n\nA small portable organ, played with one hand, the bellows being worked with the other, -- used in the sixteenth and seventeenth centuries.
