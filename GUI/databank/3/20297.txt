tetragonal
1. (Geom.)  Of or pertaining to a tetragon; having four angles or sides; thus, the square, the parallelogram, the rhombus, and the trapezium are tetragonal fingers. 2. (Bot.)  Having four prominent longitudinal angles. 3. (Crystallog.)  Designating, or belonging to, a certain system of crystallization; dimetric. See Tetragonal system, under Crystallization.
