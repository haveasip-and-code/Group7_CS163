drudge
To perform menial work; to labor in mean or unpleasant offices with toil and fatigue. He gradually rose in the estimation of the booksellers for whom he drudged. Macaulay.\n\nTo consume laboriously; -- with away. Rise to our toils and drudge away the day. Otway.\n\nOne who drudges; one who works hard in servile employment; a mental servant. Milton.
