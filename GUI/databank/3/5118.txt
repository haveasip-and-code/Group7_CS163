barrow
1. A support having handles, and with or without a wheel, on which heavy or bulky things can be transported by hand. See Handbarrow, and Wheelbarrow. 2. (Salt Works)  A wicker case, in which salt is put to drain.\n\nA hog, esp. a male hog castrated. Holland.\n\n1. A large mound of earth or stones over the remains of the dead; a tumulus. 2. (Mining)  A heap of rubbish, attle, etc.
