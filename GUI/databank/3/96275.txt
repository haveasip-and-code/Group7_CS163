trapezoid
1. (Geom.)  A plane four-sided figure, having two sides parallel to each other. 2. (Anat.)  A bone of the carpus at the base of the second metacarpal, or index finger.\n\n1. Having the form of a trapezoid; trapezoidal; as, the trapezoid ligament which connects the coracoid process and the clavicle. 2. (Anat.)  Of or pertaining to the trapezoid ligament; as, the trapezoid line.
