justle
To run or strike against each other; to encounter; to clash; to jostle. Shak. The chariots shall rage in the streets; they shall justle one against another in the broad ways. Nahum ii. 4.\n\nTo push; to drive; to force by running against; to jostle. We justled one another out, and disputed the post for a great while. Addison.\n\nAn encounter or shock; a jostle.
