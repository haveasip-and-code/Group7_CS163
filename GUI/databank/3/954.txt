alight
1. To spring down, get down, or descend, as from on horseback or from a carriage; to dismount. 2. To descend and settle, lodge, rest, or stop; as, a flying bird alights on a tree; snow alights on a roof. 3. To come or chance (upon). [R.]\n\nLighted; lighted up; in a flame. \"The lamps were alight.\" Dickens.
