icy
1. Pertaining to, resembling, or abounding in, ice; cold; frosty. \"Icy chains.\" Shak. \"Icy region.\" Boyle. \"Icy seas.\" Pope. 2. Characterized by coldness, as of manner, influence, etc.; chilling; frigid; cold. Icy was the deportment with which Philip received these demonstrations of affection. Motley.
