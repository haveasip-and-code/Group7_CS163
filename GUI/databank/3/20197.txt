bouge
1. To swell out. [Obs.] 2. To bilge. [Obs.] \"Their ship bouged.\" Hakluyt.\n\nTo stave in; to bilge. [Obs.] Holland.\n\nBouche (see Bouche, 2); food and drink; provisions. [Obs.] [They] made room for a bombardman that brought bouge for a country lady or two, that fainted . . . with fasting. B. Jonson .
