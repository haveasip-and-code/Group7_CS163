nill
Not to will; to refuse; to reject. [Obs.] Certes, said he, I nill thine offered grace. Spenser.\n\nTo be unwilling; to refuse to act. The actions of the will are \"velle\" and \"nolle,\" to will and nill. Burton. Will he, nill he, whether he wills it or not.\n\n1. Shining sparks thrown off from melted brass. 2. Scales of hot iron from the forge. Knight.
