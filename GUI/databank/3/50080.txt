shrine
1. A case, box, or receptacle, especially one in which are deposited sacred relics, as the bones of a saint. 2. Any sacred place, as an altar, tromb, or the like. Too weak the sacred shrine guard. Byron. 3. A place or object hallowed from its history or associations; as, a shrine of art.\n\nTo enshrine; to place reverently, as in a shrine. \"Shrined in his sanctuary.\" Milton.
