huge
Very large; enormous; immense; excessive; -- used esp. of material bulk, but often of qualities, extent, etc.; as, a huge ox; a huge space; a huge difference. \"The huge confusion.\" Chapman. \"A huge filly.\" Jer. Taylor. -- Huge\"ly, adv. -- Huge\"ness, n. Doth it not flow as hugely as the sea. Shak. Syn. -- Enormous; gigantic; colossal; immense; prodigious; vast.
