sandal
Same as Sendal. Sails of silk and ropes of sandal. Longfellow.\n\nSandalwood. \"Fans of sandal.\" Tennyson.\n\n(a) A kind of shoe consisting of a sole strapped to the foot; a protection for the foot, covering its lower surface, but not its upper. (b) A kind of slipper. (c) An overshoe with parallel openings across the instep.
