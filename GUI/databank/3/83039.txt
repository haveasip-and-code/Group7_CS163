imperative
1. Expressive of command; containing positive command; authoritatively or absolutely directive; commanding; authoritative; as, imperative orders. The suit of kings are imperative. Bp. Hall. 2. Not to be avoided or evaded; obligatory; binding; compulsory; as, an imperative duty or order. 3. (Gram.)  Expressive of commund, entreaty, advice, or exhortation; as, the imperative mood.\n\nThe imperative mood; also, a verb in the imperative mood.
