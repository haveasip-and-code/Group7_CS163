sprit
To throw out with force from a narrow orifice; to eject; to spurt out. [Obs.] Sir T. Browne.\n\nTo sprout; to bud; to germinate, as barley steeped for malt.\n\nA shoot; a sprout. [Obs.] Mortimer.\n\nA small boom, pole, or spar, which crosses the sail of a boat diagonally from the mast to the upper aftmost corner, which it is used to extend and elevate.
