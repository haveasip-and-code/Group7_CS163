turkoman
Same as Turcoman. TURK'S-HEAD Turk's\"-head`, n. 1. (Naut.) A knot of turbanlike form  worked on a rope with a piece of small line. 2. (a) The melon cactus. [West Indies] (b) Any of several species of Echinocactus. [California] 3.  A long-handled, round-headed broom for sweeping ceilings, etc. [Colloq. or Dial.]
