losing
Given to flattery or deceit; flattering; cozening. [Obs.] Amongst the many simoniacal that swarmed in the land, Herbert, Bishop of Thetford, must not be forgotten; nick-named Losing, that is, the Fratterer. Fuller.\n\nCausing or incurring loss; as, a losing game or business. Who strive sit out losing hands are lost. Herbert.
