lithic
1. Of or pertaining to stone; as, lithic architecture. 2. (Med.)  Pertaining to the formation of uric-acid concretions (stone) in the bladder and other parts of the body; as, lithic diathesis. LIthic acid (Old Med. Chem.), uric acid. See Uric acid, under Uric.\n\nA medicine which tends to prevent stone in the bladder.\n\nPertaining to or denoting lithium or some of its compounds. Frankland.
