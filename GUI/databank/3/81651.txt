shamble
1. (Mining)  One of a succession of niches or platforms, one above another, to hold ore which is thrown successively from platform to platform, and thus raised to a higher level. 2. pl.  A place where butcher's meat is sold. As summer flies are in the shambles. Shak. 3. pl.  A place for slaughtering animals for meat. To make a shambles of the parliament house. Shak.\n\nTo walk awkwardly and unsteadily, as if the knees were weak; to shuffle along.
