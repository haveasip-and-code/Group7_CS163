bedlam
1. A place appropriated to the confinement and care of the insane; a madhouse. Abp. Tillotson. 2. An insane person; a lunatic; a madman. [Obs.] Let's get the bedlam to lead him. Shak. 3. Any place where uproar and confusion prevail.\n\nBelonging to, or fit for, a madhouse. \"The bedlam, brainsick duchess.\" Shak.
