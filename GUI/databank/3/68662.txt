huswife
1. A female housekeeper; a woman who manages domestic affairs; a thirfty woman. \"The bounteous huswife Nature.\" Shak. The huswife is she that do labor doth fall. Tusser. 2. A worthless woman; a hussy. [Obs.] Shak. 3. Etym: [See Hussy a bag.]  A case for sewing materials. See Housewife. Cowper.\n\nTo manage with frugality; -- said of a woman. Dryden.
