audacious
1. Daring; spirited; adventurous. As in a cloudy chair, ascending rides Audacious. Milton. 2. Contemning the restraints of law, religion, or decorum; bold in wickedness; presumptuous; impudent; insolent. \" Audacious traitor.\" Shak. \" Such audacious neighborhood.\" Milton. 3. Committed with, or proceedings from, daring effrontery or contempt of law, morality, or decorum. \"Audacious cruelty.\" \"Audacious prate.\" Shak.
