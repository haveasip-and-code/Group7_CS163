tope
A moundlike Buddhist sepulcher, or memorial monument. often erected over a Buddhish relic.\n\nA grove or clumb of trees; as, a toddy tope. [India] Whitworth.\n\n1. (Zoöl.)  A small shark or dogfish (Galeorhinus, or Galeus, galeus), native of Europe, but found also on the coasts of California and Tasmania; -- called also toper, oil shark, miller's dog, and penny dog. 2. (Zoöl.)  The wren. [Prov. Eng.]\n\nTo drink hard or frequently; to drink strong or spiritous liquors to excess. If you tope in form, and treat. Dryden.
