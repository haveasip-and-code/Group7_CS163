cuddy
1. An ass; esp., one driven by a huckster or greengrocer. [Scot.] 2. Hence: A blockhead; a lout. Hood. 3. (Mech.)  A lever mounted on a tripod for lifting stones, leveling up railroad ties, etc. Knight.\n\nA small cabin: also, the galley or kitchen of a vessel.\n\nThe coalfish (Pollachius carbonarius). [Written also cudden.]
