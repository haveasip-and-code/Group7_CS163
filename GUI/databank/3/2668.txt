pied
imp. & p. p. of Pi, or Pie, v.\n\nVariegated with spots of different colors; party-colored; spotted; piebald. \"Pied coats.\" Burton. \"Meadows trim with daisies pied.\" Milton. Pied antelope (Zoöl.), the bontebok. -- Pied-billed grebe (Zoöl.), the dabchick. -- Pied blackbird (Zoöl.), any Asiatic thrush of the genus Turdulus. -- Pied finch (Zoöl.) (a) The chaffinch. (b) The snow bunting. [Prov. Eng.] -- Pied flycatcher (Zoöl.), a common European flycatcher (Ficedula atricapilla). The male is black and white.
