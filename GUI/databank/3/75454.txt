topping
1. Rising above; surpassing. 2. Hence, assuming superiority; proud. The great and flourishing condition of some of the topping sinners of the world. South. 3. Fine; gallant. [Slang] Johnson.\n\n1. The act of one who tops; the act of cutting off the top. 2. (Naut.)  The act of raising one extremity of a spar higher than the other. 3. pl.  That which comes from hemp in the process of hatcheling. Topping lift (Naut.), a large, strong tackle employed to raise or top the end of a gaff, or of a boom.
