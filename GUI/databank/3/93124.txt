creak
To make a prolonged sharp grating or ssqueaking sound, as by the friction of hard substances; as, shoes creak. The creaking locusts with my voice conspire. Dryden. Doors upon their hinges creaked. Tennyson.\n\nTo produce a creaking sound with. Creaking my shoes on the plain masonry. Shak.\n\nThew sound produced by anuthing that creaks; a creaking. Roget.
