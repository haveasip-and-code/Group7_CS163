recreant
1. Crying for mercy, as a combatant in the trial by battle; yielding; cowardly; mean-spirited; craven. \"This recreant knight.\" Spenser. 2. Apostate; false; unfaithful. Who, for so many benefits received, Turned recreant to God, ingrate and false. Milton.\n\nOne who yields in combat, and begs for mercy; a mean-spirited, cowardly wretch. Blackstone. You are all recreants and dastards! Shak.
