enumerate
To count; to tell by numbers; to count over, or tell off one after another; to number; to reckon up; to mention one by one; to name over; to make a special and separate account of; to recount; as, to enumerate the stars in a constellation. Enumerating the services he had done. Ludlow. Syn. -- To reckon; compute; calculate; count; estimate; relate; rehearse; recapitulate; detail.
