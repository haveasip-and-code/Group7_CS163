thorough
Through. [Obs.] Spenser. Shak.\n\n1. Passing through; as, thorough lights in a house. [Obs.] 2. Passing through or to the end; hence, complete; perfect; as, a thorough reformation; thorough work; a thorough translator; a thorough poet.\n\n1. Thoroughly. [Obs. or Colloq.] Chaucer. 2. Through. [Obs.] Shak.\n\nA furrow between two ridges, to drain off the surface water. [Prov. Eng.] Halliwell.
