aspic
1. The venomous asp. [Chiefly poetic] Shak. Tennyson. 2. A piece of ordnance carrying a 12 pound shot. [Obs.]\n\nA European species of lavender (Lavandula spica), which produces a volatile oil. See Spike.\n\nA savory meat jelly containing portions of fowl, game, fish, hard boiled eggs, etc. Thackeray.
