intensive
1. Stretched; admitting of intension, or increase of degree; that can be intensified. Sir M. Hale. 2. Characterized by persistence; intent; unremitted; assiduous; intense. [Obs.] Sir H. Wotton. 3. (Gram.)  Serving to give force or emphasis; as, an intensive verb or preposition.\n\nThat which intensifies or emphasizes; an intensive verb or word.
