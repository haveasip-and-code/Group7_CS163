cantle
1. A corner or edge of anything; a piece; a fragment; a part. \"In one cantle of his law.\" Milton. Cuts me from the best of all my land A huge half moon, a monstrous cantle out. Shak. 2. The upwardly projecting rear part of saddle, opposite to the pommel. [Written also cante.]\n\nTo cut in pieces; to cut out from. [Obs.] [Written also cantile.]
