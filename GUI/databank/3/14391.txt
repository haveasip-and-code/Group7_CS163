cupola
1. (Arch.)  A roof having a rounded form, hemispherical or nearly so; also, a celing having the same form. When on a large scale it is usually called dome. 2. A small structure standing on the top of a dome; a lantern. 3. A furnace for melting iron or other metals in large quantity, -- used chiefly in foundries and steel works. 4. A revoling shot-proof turret for heavy ordnance. 5. (Anat.)  The top of the spire of the cochlea of the ear.
