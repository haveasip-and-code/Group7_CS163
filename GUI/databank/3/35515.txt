drummer
1. One whose office is to best the drum, as in military exercises and marching. 2. One who solicits custom; a commercial traveler. [Colloq. U.S.] Bartlett. 3. (Zoöl.)  A fish that makes a sound when caught; as: (a) The squeteague. (b) A California sculpin. 4. (Zoöl.)  A large West Indian cockroach (Blatta gigantea) which drums on woodwork, as a sexual call.
