swaddle
Anything used to swaddle with, as a cloth or band; a swaddling band. They put me in bed in all my swaddles. Addison.\n\n1. To bind as with a bandage; to bind or warp tightly with clothes; to swathe; -- used esp. of infants; as, to swaddle a baby. They swaddled me up in my nightgown with long pieces of linen. Addison. 2. To beat; to cudgel. [Obs.] Hudibras.
