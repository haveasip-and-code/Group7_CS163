footman
1. A soldier who marches and fights on foot; a foot soldier. 2. A man in waiting; a male servant whose duties are to attend the door, the carriage, the table, etc. 3. Formerly, a servant who ran in front of his master's carriage; a runner. Prior. 4. A metallic stand with four feet, for keeping anything warm before a fire. 5. (Zoöl.)  A moth of the family Lithosidæ; -- so called from its livery- like colors.
