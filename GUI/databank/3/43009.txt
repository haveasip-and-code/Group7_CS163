ruling
1. Predominant; chief; reigning; controlling; as, a ruling passion; a ruling sovereign. 2. Used in marking or engraving lines; as, a ruling machine or pen. Syn. -- Predominant; chief; controlling; directing; guilding; governing; prevailing; prevalent.\n\n1. The act of one who rules; ruled lines. 2. (Law)  A decision or rule of a judge or a court, especially an oral decision, as in excluding evidence.
