finery
1. Fineness; beauty. [Obs.] Don't choose your place of study by the finery of the prospects. I. Watts. 2. Ornament; decoration; especially, excecially decoration; showy clothes; jewels. Her mistress' cast-off finery. F. W. Robertson. 3. Etym: [Cf. Refinery.] (Iron Works)  A charcoal hearth or furnace for the conversion of cast iron into wrought iron, or into iron suitable for puddling.
