unappealable
1. Not appealable; that can not be carried to a higher tribunal by appeal; as, an unappealable suit or action. 2. Not to be appealed from; -- said of a judge or a judgment that can not be overruled. The infallible, unappealable Judge [God]. South. We submitted to a galling yet unappealable necessity. Shelley. -- Un`ap*peal\"a*bly, adv.
