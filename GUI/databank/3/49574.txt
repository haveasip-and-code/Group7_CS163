abjure
1. To renounce upon oath; to forswear; to disavow; as, to abjure allegiance to a prince. To abjure the realm, is to swear to abandon it forever. 2. To renounce or reject with solemnity; to recant; to abandon forever; to reject; repudiate; as, to abjure errors. \"Magic I here abjure.\" Shak. Syn. -- See Renounce.\n\nTo renounce on oath. Bp. Burnet.
