jagger
One who carries about a small load; a peddler. See 2d Jag. [Scot.] Sir W. Scott.\n\nOne who, or that which, jags; specifically: (a) jagging iron used for crimping pies, cakes, etc. (b) A toothed chisel. See Jag, v. t. Jagger spring, a spring beneath a seat, and resting on cleats or blocks in the body of a vehicle. Knight.
