serial
1. Of or pertaining to a series; consisting of a series; appearing in successive parts or numbers; as, a serial work or publication. \"Classification . . . may be more or less serial.\" H. Spencer. 2. (Bot.)  Of or pertaining to rows. Gray. Serial homology. (Biol.) See under Homology. -- Serial symmetry. (Biol.) See under Symmetry.\n\nA publication appearing in a series or succession of part; a tale, or other writing, published in successive numbers of a periodical.
