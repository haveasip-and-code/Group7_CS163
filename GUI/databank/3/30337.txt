ache
A name given to several species of plants; as, smallage, wild celery, parsley. [Obs.] Holland.\n\nContinued pain, as distinguished from sudden twinges, or spasmodic pain. \"Such an ache in my bones.\" Shak. Note: Often used in composition, as, a headache, an earache, a toothache.\n\nTo suffer pain; to have, or be in, pain, or in continued pain; to be distressed. \"My old bones ache.\" Shak. The sins that in your conscience ache. Keble.
