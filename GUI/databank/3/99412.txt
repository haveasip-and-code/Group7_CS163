pilgrimage
1. The journey of a pilgrim; a long journey; especially, a journey to a shrine or other sacred place. Fig., the journey of human life. Shak. The days of the years of my pilgrimage. Gen. xlvii. 9. 2. A tedious and wearisome time. In prison hast thou spent a pilgrimage. Shak. Syn. -- Journey; tour; excursion. See Journey.
