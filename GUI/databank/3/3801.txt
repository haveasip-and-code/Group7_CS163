cobble
A fishing boat. See Coble.\n\n1. A cobblestone. \"Their slings held cobbles round.\" Fairfax. 2. pl.  Cob coal. See under Cob.\n\n1. To make or mend coarsely; to patch; to botch; as, to cobble shoes. Shak. \"A cobbled saddle.\" Thackeray. 2. To make clumsily. \"Cobbled rhymes.\" Dryden. 3. To pave with cobblestones.
