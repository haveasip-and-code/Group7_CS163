anodyne
Serving to assuage pain; soothing. The anodyne draught of oblivion. Burke. Note: \"The word [in a medical sense] in chiefly applied to the different preparations of opium, belladonna, hyoscyamus, and lettuce.\" Am. Cyc.\n\nAny medicine which allays pain, as an opiate or narcotic; anything that soothes disturbed feelings.
