dorian
1. Of or pertaining to the ancient Greeks of Doris; Doric; as, a Dorian fashion. 2. (Mus.)  Same as Doric, 3. \"Dorian mood.\" Milton. Dorian mode (Mus.), the first of the authentic church modes or tones, from D to D, resembling our D minor scale, but with the B natural. Grove.\n\nA native or inhabitant of Doris in Greece.
