ceremonious
1. Consisting of outward forms and rites; ceremonial. Note: [In this sense ceremonial is now preferred.] The ceremonious part of His worship. South. 2. According to prescribed or customary rules and forms; devoted to forms and ceremonies; formally respectful; punctilious. \"Ceremonious phrases.\" Addison. Too ceremonious and traditional. Shak. Syn. -- Formal; precise; exact. See Formal.
