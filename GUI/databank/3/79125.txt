lentous
Viscid; viscous; tenacious. Spawn of a lentous and transparent body. Sir T. Browne. L'ENVOI; L'ENVOY L'en`voi\", or; L'en`voy\" (, n. Etym: [F. le the + envei a sending. See Envoy.] 1. One or more detached verses at the end of a literary composition, serving to convey the moral, or to address the poem to a particular person; -- orig. employed in old French poetry. Shak. 2. A conclusion; a result. Massinger.
