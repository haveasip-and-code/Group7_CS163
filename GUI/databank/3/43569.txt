spright
1. Spirit; mind; soul; state of mind; mood. [Obs.] \"The high heroic spright.\" Spenser. Wondrous great grief groweth in my spright. Spenser. 2. A supernatural being; a spirit; a shade; an apparition; a ghost. Forth he called, out of deep darkness dread, Legions of sprights. Spenser. To thee, O Father, Son, and Sacred Spright. Fairfax. 3. A kind of short arrow. [Obs.] Bacon.\n\nTo haunt, as a spright. [Obs.] Shak.
