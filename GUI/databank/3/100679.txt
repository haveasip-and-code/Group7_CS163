pernicious
Quick; swift (to burn). [R.] Milton.\n\nHaving the quality of injuring or killing; destructive; very mischievous; baleful; malicious; wicked. Let this pernicious hour Stand aye accursed in the calendar. Shak. Pernicious to his health. Prescott. Syn. -- Destructive; ruinous; deadly; noxious; injurious; baneful; deleterious; hurtful; mischievous. -- Per*ni\"cious*ly, adv., -- Per*ni\"cious*ness, n.
