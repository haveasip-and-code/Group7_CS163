shameless
1. Destitute of shame; wanting modesty; brazen-faced; insensible to disgrace. \"Such shameless bards we have.\" Pope. Shame enough to shame thee, wert thou not shameless. Shak. 2. Indicating want of modesty, or sensibility to disgrace; indecent; as, a shameless picture or poem. Syn. -- Impudent; unblushing; audacious; immodest; indecent; indelicate. -- Shame\"less*ly, adv. -- Shame\"less*ness, n.
