sneeze
To emit air, chiefly through the nose, audibly and violently, by a kind of involuntary convulsive force, occasioned by irritation of the inner membrane of the nose. Not to be sneezed at, not to be despised or contemned; not to be treated lightly. [Colloq.] \"He had to do with old women who were not to be sneezed at.\" Prof. Wilson.\n\nA sudden and violent ejection of air with an audible sound, chiefly through the nose.
