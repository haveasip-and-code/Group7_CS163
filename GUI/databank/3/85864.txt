embrasure
An embrace. [Obs.] \"Our locked embrasures.\"\" Shak.\n\n1. (Arch.)  A splay of a door or window. Apart, in the twilight gloom of a window's embrasure, Sat the lovers. Longfellow. 2. (Fort.)  An aperture with slant sides in a wall or parapet, through which cannon are pointed and discharged; a crenelle. See Illust. of Casemate.
