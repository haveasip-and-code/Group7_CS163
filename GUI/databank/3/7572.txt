satirical
1. Of or pertaining to satire; of the nature of satire; as, a satiric style. 2. Censorious; severe in language; sarcastic; insulting. \"Satirical rogue.\" Shak. Syn. -- Cutting; caustic; poignant; sarcastic; ironical; bitter; reproachful; abusive. -- Sa*tir\"ic*al*ly, adv. -- Sa*tir\"ic*al*ness, n.
