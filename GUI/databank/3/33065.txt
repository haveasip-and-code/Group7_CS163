treasonable
Pertaining to treason; consisting of treason; involving the crime of treason, or partaking of its guilt. Most men's heads had been intoxicated with imaginations of plots and treasonable practices. Clarendon. Syn. -- Treacherous; traitorous; perfidious; insidious. -- Trea\"son*a*ble*ness, n. -- Trea\"son*a*bly, adv.
