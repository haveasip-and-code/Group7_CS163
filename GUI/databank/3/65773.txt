taper
1. A small wax candle; a small lighted wax candle; hence, a small light. Get me a taper in my study, Lucius. Shak. 2. A tapering form; gradual diminution of thickness in an elongated object; as, the taper of a spire.\n\nRegularly narrowed toward the point; becoming small toward one end; conical; pyramidical; as, taper fingers.\n\nTo become gradually smaller toward one end; as, a sugar loaf tapers toward one end.\n\nTo make or cause to taper.
