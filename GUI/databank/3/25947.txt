abscission
1. The act or process of cutting off. \"Not to be cured without the abscission of a member.\" Jer. Taylor. 2. The state of being cut off. Sir T. Browne. 3. (Rhet.)  A figure of speech employed when a speaker having begun to say a thing stops abruptly: thus, \"He is a man of so much honor and candor, and of such generosity -- but I need say no more.\"
