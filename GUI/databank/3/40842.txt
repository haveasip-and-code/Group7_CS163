honorably
1. In an honorable manner; in a manner showing, or consistent with, honor. The reverend abbot . . . honorably received him. Shak. Why did I not more honorably starve Dryden. 2. Decently; becomingly. [Obs.] \"Do this message honorably.\" Shak. Syn. -- Magnanimously; generously; nobly; worthily; justly; equitably; fairly; reputably.
