rath
1. A hill or mound. [Ireland] Spencer. 2. A kind of ancient fortification found in Ireland.\n\nComing before others, or before the usual time; early. [Obs. or Poetic] Bring the rathe primrose that forsaken dies. Milton.\n\nEarly; soon; betimes. [Obs. or Poetic] Why rise ye up so rathe Chaucer. Too rathe cut off by practice criminal. Spencer.
