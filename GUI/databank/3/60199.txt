individually
1. In an individual manner or relation; as individuals; separately; each by itself. \"Individually or collectively.\" Burke. How should that subsist solitarily by itself which hath no substance, but individually the very same whereby others subsist with it Hooker. 2. In an inseparable manner; inseparably; incommunicably; indivisibly; as, individuallyhe same. [Omniscience], an attribute individually proper to the Godhead. Hakewill.
