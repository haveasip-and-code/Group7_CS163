portage
(a) A sailor's wages when in port. (b) The amount of a sailor's wages for a voyage.\n\nA porthole. [Obs.] Shak.\n\n1. The act of carrying or transporting. 2. The price of carriage; porterage. Bp. Fell. 3. Capacity for carrying; tonnage. [Obs.] Hakluyt. 4. A carry between navigable waters. See 3d Carry.\n\nTo carry (goods, boats, etc.) overland between navigable waters.
