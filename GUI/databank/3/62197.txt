belonging
1. That which belongs to one; that which pertains to one; hence, goods or effects. \"Thyself and thy belongings.\" Shak. 2. That which is connected with a principal or greater thing; an appendage; an appurtenance. 3. Family; relations; household. [Colloq.] Few persons of her ladyship's belongings stopped, before they did her bidding, to ask her reasons. Thackeray.
