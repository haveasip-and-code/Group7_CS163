undress
1. To divest of clothes; to strip. 2. To divest of ornaments to disrobe. 3. (Med.)  To take the dressing, or covering, from; as, to undress a wound.\n\n1. A loose, negligent dress; ordinary dress, as distinguished from full dress. 2. (Mil. & Naval)  An authorized habitual dress of officers and soldiers, but not full-dress uniform. Undress parade (Mil.), a substitute for dress parade, allowed in bad weather, the companies forming without arms, and the ceremony being shortened.
