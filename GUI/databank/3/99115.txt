shelving
Sloping gradually; inclining; as, a shelving shore. Shak. \"Shelving arches.\" Addison.\n\n1. The act of fitting up shelves; as, the job of shelving a closet. 2. The act of laying on a shelf, or on the shelf; putting off or aside; as, the shelving of a claim. 3. Material for shelves; shelves, collectively.
