cocket
Pert; saucy. [Obs.] Halliwell.\n\n1. (Eng. Law)  A customhouse seal; a certified document given to a shopper as a warrant that his goods have been duly enstered and have paid duty. 2. An office in a customhouse where goods intended for export are entered. [Eng.] 3. A measure for bread. [Obs.] Blount.
