columbine
Of or pertaining to a dove; dovelike; dove-colored. \"Columbine innocency.\" Bacon.\n\n1. (Bot.)  A plant of several species of the genus Aquilegia; as, A. vulgaris, or the common garden columbine; A. Canadensis, the wild red columbine of North America. 2. The mistress or sweetheart of Harlequin in pantomimes. Brewer.
