crows

The wrinkles that appear, as the effect of age or dissipation, under and around the outer corners of the eyes. Tennyson. 2. (Mil.)  A caltrop. [Written also crowfoot.] 3. (Arch.)  Same as Bird's-mouth. [U.S.]
