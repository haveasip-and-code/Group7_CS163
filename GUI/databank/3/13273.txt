punt
To play at basset, baccara, faro. or omber; to gamble. She heard . . . of his punting at gaming tables. Thackeray.\n\nAct of playing at basset, baccara, faro, etc.\n\nA flat-bottomed boat with square ends. It is adapted for use in shallow waters.\n\n1. To propel, as a boat in shallow water, by pushing with a pole against the bottom; to push or propel (anything) with exertion. Livingstone. 2. (Football)  To kick (the ball) before it touches the ground, when let fall from the hands.\n\nThe act of punting the ball.
