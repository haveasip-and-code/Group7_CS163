concede
1. To yield or suffer; to surrender; to grant; as. to concede the point in question. Boyle. 2. To grant, as a right or privilege; to make concession of. 3. To admit to be true; to acknowledge. We concede that their citizens were those who lived under different forms. Burke. Syn. -- To grant; allow; admit; yield; surrender.\n\nTo yield or make concession. I wished you to concede to America, at a time when she prayed concession at our feet. Burke.
