agglutinate
To unite, or cause to adhere, as with glue or other viscous substance; to unite by causing an adhesion of substances.\n\n1. United with glue or as with glue; cemented together. 2. (physiol.)  Consisting of root words combined but not materially altered as to form or meaning; as, agglutinate forms, languages, etc. See Agglutination, 2.
