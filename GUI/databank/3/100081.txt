rede
1. To advise or counsel. [Obs. or Scot.] I rede that our host here shall begin. Chaucer. 2. To interpret; to explain. [Obs.] My sweven [dream] rede aright. Chaucer.\n\n1. Advice; counsel; suggestion. [Obs. or Scot.] Burns. There was none other remedy ne reed. Chaucer. 2. A word or phrase; a motto; a proverb; a wise saw. [Obs.] \"This rede is rife.\" Spenser.
