ankylostomiasis
A disease due to the presence of the parasites Agchylostoma duodenale, Uncinaria (subgenus Necator) americana, or allied nematodes, in the small intestine. When present in large numbers they produce a severe anæmia by sucking the blood from the intestinal walls.  Called also miner's anæmia, tunnel disease, brickmaker's anæmia, Egyptian chlorosis.
