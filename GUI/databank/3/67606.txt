wiper
1. One who, or that which, wipes. 2. Something used for wiping, as a towel or rag. 3. (Mach.)  A piece generally projecting from a rotating or swinging piece, as an axle or rock shaft, for the purpose of raising stampers, lifting rods, or the like, and leaving them to fall by their own weight; a kind of cam. 4. (Firearms)  A rod, or an attachment for a rod, for holding a rag with which to wipe out the bore of the barrel.
