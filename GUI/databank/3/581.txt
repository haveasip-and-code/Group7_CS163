loaf
Any thick lump, mass, or cake; especially, a large regularly shaped or molded mass, as of bread, sugar, or cake. Bacon. Loaf sugar, refined sugar that has been formed into a conical loaf in a mold.\n\nTo spend time in idleness; to lounge or loiter about. \" Loafing vagabonds.\" W. Black.\n\nTo spend in idleness; -- with away; as, to loaf time away.
