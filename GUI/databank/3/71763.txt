diacaustic
Pertaining to, or possessing the properties of, a species of caustic curves formed by refraction. See Caustic surface, under Caustic.\n\n1. (Med.)  That which burns by refraction, as a double convex lens, or the sun's rays concentrated by such a lens, sometimes used as a cautery. 2. (Math.)  A curved formed by the consecutive intersections of rays of light refracted through a lens.
