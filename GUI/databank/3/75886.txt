ditty
1. A saying or utterance; especially, one that is short and frequently repeated; a theme. O, too high ditty for my simple rhyme. Spenser. 2. A song; a lay; a little poem intended to be sung. \"Religious, martial, or civil ditties.\" Milton.ditties sing. Sandys.\n\nTo sing; to warble a little tune. Beasts fain would sing; birds ditty to their notes. Herbert.
