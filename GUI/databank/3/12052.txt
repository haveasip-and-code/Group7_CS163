demonstrative
1. Having the nature of demonstration; tending to demonstrate; making evident; exhibiting clearly or conclusively. \"Demonstrative figures.\" Dryden. An argument necessary and demonstrative. Hooker. 2. Expressing, or apt to express, much; displaying feeling or sentiment; as, her nature was demonstrative. 3. Consisting of eulogy or of invective. \"Demonstrative eloquence.\" Blair. Demonstrative pronoun (Gram.), a pronoun distinctly designating that to which it refers.\n\nA demonstrative pronoun; as, \"this\" and \"that\" are demonstratives.
