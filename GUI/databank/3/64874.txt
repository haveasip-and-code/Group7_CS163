grizzly
Somewhat gray; grizzled. Old squirrels that turn grizzly. Bacon. Grizzly bear (Zoöl.), a large and ferocious bear (Ursus horribilis) of Western North America and the Rocky Mountains. It is remarkable for the great length of its claws.\n\n1. (Zoöl.)  A grizzly bear. See under Grizzly, a. 2. pl.  In hydraulic mining, gratings used to catch and throw out large stones from the sluices. [Local, U. S.] Raymond.
