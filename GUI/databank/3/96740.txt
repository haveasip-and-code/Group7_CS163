indurate
1. Hardened; not soft; indurated. Tyndale. 2. Without sensibility; unfeeling; obdurate.\n\n1. To make hard; as, extreme heat indurates clay; some fossils are indurated by exposure to the air. 2. To make unfeeling; to deprive of sensibility; to render obdurate.\n\nTo grow hard; to harden, or become hard; as, clay indurates by drying, and by heat.
