decompound
1. To compound or mix with that is already compound; to compound a second time. 2. To reduce to constituent parts; to decompose. It divides and decompounds objects into . . . parts. Hazlitt.\n\n1. Compound of what is already compounded; compounded a second time. 2. (Bot.)  Several times compounded or divided, as a leaf or stem; decomposite.\n\nA decomposite.
