matin
A French mastiff.\n\n1. Morning. [Obs.] Shak. 2. pl. Etym: [F. matines. See Etymol. above.]  Morning worship or service; morning prayers or songs. The winged choristers began To chirp their matins. Cleveland. 3. Time of morning service; the first canonical hour in the Roman Catholic Church.\n\nOf or pertaining to the morning, or to matins; used in the morning; matutinal.
