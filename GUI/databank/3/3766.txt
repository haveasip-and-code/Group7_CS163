sorrowful
1. Full of sorrow; exhibiting sorrow; sad; dejected; distressed. \"This sorrowful prisoner.\" Chaucer. My soul is exceeding sorrowful, even unto death. Matt. xxvi. 38. 2. Producing sorrow; exciting grief; mournful; lamentable; grievous; as, a sorrowful accident. Syn. -- Sad; mournful; dismal; disconsolate; drear; dreary; grievous; lamentable; doleful; distressing. -- Sor\"row*ful*ly, adv. -- Sor\"row*ful*ness, n.
