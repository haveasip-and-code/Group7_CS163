correspondent
Suitable; adapted; fit; corresponding; congruous; conformable; in accord or agreement; obedient; willing. Action correspondent or repugnant unto the law. Hooker. As fast the correspondent passions rise. Thomson. I will be correspondent to command. Shak.\n\n1. One with whom intercourse is carried on by letter. Macualay. 2. One who communicates information, etc., by letter or telegram to a newspaper or periodical. 3. (Com.)  One who carries on commercial intercourse by letter or telegram with a person or firm at a distance.
