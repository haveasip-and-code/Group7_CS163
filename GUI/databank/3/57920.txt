barracuda
1. (Zoöl.)  A voracious pikelike, marine fish, of the genus Sphyræna, sometimes used as food. Note: That of Europe and our Atlantic coast is Sphyræna spet (or S. vulgaris); a southern species is S. picuda; the Californian is S. argentea. 2. (Zoöl.)  A large edible fresh-water fish of Australia and New Zealand (Thyrsites atun).
