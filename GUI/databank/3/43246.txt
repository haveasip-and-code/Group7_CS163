spinner
1. One who, or that which, spins one skilled in spinning; a spinning machine. 2. A spider. \"Long-legged spinners.\" Shak. 3. (Zoöl.)  A goatsucker; -- so called from the peculiar noise it makes when darting through the air. 4. (Zoöl.)  A spinneret. Ring spinner, a machine for spinning, in which the twist, given to the yarn by a revolving bobbin, is regulated by the drag of a small metal loop which slides around a ring encircling the bobbin, instead of by a throstle.
