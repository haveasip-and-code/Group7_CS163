sometime
1. At a past time indefinitely referred to; once; formerly. Did they not sometime cry \"All hail\" to me Shak. 2. At a time undefined; once in a while; now and then; sometimes. Sometime we see a cloud that's dragonish, A vapor sometime like a bear or lion. Shak. 3. At one time or other hereafter; as, I will do it sometime. \"Sometime he reckon shall.\" Chaucer.\n\nHaving been formerly; former; late; whilom. Our sometime sister, now our queen. Shak. Ion, our sometime darling, whom we prized. Talfourd.
