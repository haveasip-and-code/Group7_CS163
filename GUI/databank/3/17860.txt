corduroy
1. A sort of cotton velveteen, having the surface raised in ridges. 2. pl.  Trousers or breeches of corduroy. Corduroy road, a roadway formed of logs laid side by side across it, as in marshy places; -- so called from its rough or ribbed surface, resembling corduroy. [U.S.]\n\nTo form of logs laid side by side. \"Roads were corduroyed.\" Gemn. W.T. Sherman.
