redintegration
1. Restoration to a whole or sound state; renewal; renovation. Dr. H. More. 2. (Chem.)  Restoration of a mixed body or matter to its former nature and state. [Achaic.] Coxe. 3. (Psychology)  The law that objects which have been previously combined as part of a single mental state tend to recall or suggest one another; -- adopted by many philosophers to explain the phenomena of the association of ideas.
