double-quick
Of, or performed in, the fastest time or step in marching, next to the run; as, a double-quick step or march.\n\nDouble-quick time, step, or march. Note: Double-quick time requires 165 steps, each 33 inches in length, to be taken in one minute. The number of steps may be increased up to 180 per minute.\n\nTo move, or cause to move, in double-quick time.
