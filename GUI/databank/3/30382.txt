sozzle
1. To splash or wet carelessly; as, to sozzle the feet in water. [Local, U.S.] Bartlett. 2. To heap up in confusion. [Prov. Eng.] Forby.\n\n1. One who spills water or other liquids carelessly; specifically, a sluttish woman. [Local, U.S.] 2. A mass, or heap, confusedly mingled. [Prov. Eng.]
