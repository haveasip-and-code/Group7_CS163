aaronic
Pertaining to Aaron, the first high priest of the Jews. AARON'S ROD Aar\"on's rod`. Etym: [See Exodus vii. 9 and Numbers xvii. 8] 1. (Arch.)  A rod with one serpent twined around it, thus differing from the caduceus of Mercury, which has two. 2. (Bot.)  A plant with a tall flowering stem; esp. the great mullein, or hag-taper, and the golden-rod.
