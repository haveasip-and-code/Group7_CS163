vale
A tract of low ground, or of land between hills; a valley. \" Make me a cottage in the vale.\" Tennyson. Beyond this vale of tears there is a life above. Montgomery. In those fair vales, by nature formed to please. Harte. Note: Vale is more commonly used in poetry, and valley in prose and common discourse. Syn. -- Valley; dingle; dell; dale.\n\nSee 2d Vail, 3.
