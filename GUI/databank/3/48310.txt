barque
1. Formerly, any small sailing vessel, as a pinnace, fishing smack, etc.; also, a rowing boat; a barge. Now applied poetically to a sailing vessel or boat of any kind. Byron. 2. (Naut.)  A three-masted vessel, having her foremast and mainmast squarerigged, and her mizzenmast schooner-rigged.\n\nSame as 3d Bark, n.
