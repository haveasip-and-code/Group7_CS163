stench
To stanch. [Obs.] Harvey.\n\n1. A smell; an odor. [Obs.] Clouds of savory stench involve the sky. Dryden. 2. An ill smell; an offensive odor; a stink. Cowper. Stench trap, a contrivance to prevent stench or foul air from rising from the openings of sewers, drains, etc.\n\nTo cause to emit a disagreeable odor; to cause to stink. [Obs.] Young.
