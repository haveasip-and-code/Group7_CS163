clink
To cause to give out a slight, sharp, tinkling, sound, as by striking metallic or other sonorous bodies together. And let me the canakin clink. Shak.\n\n1. To give out a slight, sharp, thinkling sound. \"The clinking latch.\" Tennyson. 2. To rhyme. [Humorous]. Cowper.\n\nA slight, sharp, tinkling sound, made by the collision of sonorous bodies. \"Clink and fall of swords.\" Shak.
