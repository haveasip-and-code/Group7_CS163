venter

(c) A belly, or protuberant part; a broad surface; as, the venter of a muscle; the venter, or anterior surface, of the scapula. 2. (Zo√∂l.)  The lower part of the abdomen in insects. 3. (Rom. & O. E. Law)  A pregnant woman; a mother; as, A has a son B by one venter, and a daughter C by another venter; children by different venters.
