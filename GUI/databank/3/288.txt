bullock
1. A young bull, or any male of the ox kind. Take thy father's young bullock, even the second bullock of seven years old. Judges vi. 25. 2. An ox, steer, or stag.\n\nTo bully. [Obs.] She shan't think to bullock and domineer over me. Foote. BULLOCK'S-EYE Bul\"lock's-eye`, n.  See Bull's-eye, 3.
