ejaculate
1. To throw out suddenly and swiftly, as if a dart; to dart; to eject. [Archaic or Technical] Its active rays ejaculated thence. Blackmore. 2. To throw out, as an exclamation; to utter by a brief and sudden impulse; as, to ejaculate a prayer.\n\nTo utter ejaculations; to make short and hasty exclamations. [R.] \"Ejaculating to himself.\" Sir W. Scott.
