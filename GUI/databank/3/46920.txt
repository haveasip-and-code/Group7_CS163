wheeze
To breathe hard, and with an audible piping or whistling sound, as persons affected with asthma. \"Wheezing lungs.\" Shak.\n\n1. A piping or whistling sound caused by difficult respiration. 2. (Phon.)  An ordinary whisper exaggerated so as to produce the hoarse sound known as the \"stage whisper.\" It is a forcible whisper with some admixture of tone.
