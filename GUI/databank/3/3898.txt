painting
1. The act or employment of laying on, or adorning with, paints or colors. 2. (Fine Arts)  The work of the painter; also, any work of art in which objects are represented in color on a flat surface; a colored representation of any object or scene; a picture. 3. Color laid on; paint. [R.] Shak. 4. A depicting by words; vivid representation in words. Syn. -- See Picture.
