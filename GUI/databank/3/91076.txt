crafty
1. Relating to, or characterized by, craft or skill; dexterous. [Obs.] \"Crafty work.\" Piers Plowman. 2. Possessing dexterity; skilled; skillful. A noble crafty man of trees. Wyclif. 3. Skillful at deceiving others; characterized by craft; cunning; wily. \"A pair of crafty knaves.\" Shak. With anxious care and crafty wiles. J. Baillie. Syn. -- Skillful; dexterous; cunning; artful; wily; Cunning.
