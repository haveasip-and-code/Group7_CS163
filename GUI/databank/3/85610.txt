jockey
1. A professional rider of horses in races. Addison. 2. A dealer in horses; a horse trader. Macaulay. 3. A cheat; one given to sharp practice in trade.\n\n1. \" To jostle by riding against one.\" Johnson. 2. To play the jockey toward; to cheat; to trick; to impose upon in trade; as, to jockey a customer.\n\nTo play or act the jockey; to cheat.
