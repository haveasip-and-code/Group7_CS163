stately
Evincing state or dignity; lofty; majestic; grand; as, statelymanners; a stately gait. \"The stately homes of England!\" Mrs. Hemans. \"Filled with stately temples.\" Prescott. Here is a stately style indeed! Shak. Syn. -- Lofty; dignified; majestic; grand; august; magnificent.\n\nMajestically; loftily. Milton.
