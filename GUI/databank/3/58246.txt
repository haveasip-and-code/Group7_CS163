formed
1. (Astron.)  Arranged, as stars in a constellation; as, formed stars. [R.] 2. (Biol.)  Having structure; capable of growth and development; organized; as, the formed or organized ferments. See Ferment, n. Formed material (Biol.), a term employed by Beale to denote the lifeless matter of a cell, that which is physiologically dead, in distinction from the truly germinal or living matter.
