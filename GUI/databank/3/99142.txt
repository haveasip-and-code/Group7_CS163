unhappy
1. Not happy or fortunate; unfortunate; unlucky; as, affairs have taken an unhappy turn. 2. In a degree miserable or wretched; not happy; sad; sorrowful; as, children render their parents unhappy by misconduct. 3. Marked by infelicity; evil; calamitous; as, an unhappy day. \"The unhappy morn.\" Milton. 4. Mischievous; wanton; wicked. [Obs.] Shak. -- Un*hap\"pi*ly, adv. -- Un*hap\"pi*ness, n.
