pricker
1. One who, or that which, pricks; a pointed instrument; a sharp point; a prickle. 2. One who spurs forward; a light horseman. The prickers, who rode foremost, . . . halted. Sir W. Scott. 3. A priming wire; a priming needle, -- used in blasting and gunnery. Knight. 4. (Naut.)  A small marline spike having generally a wooden handle, -- used in sailmaking. R. H. Dana, Ir.
