uplift
To lift or raise aloft; to raise; to elevate; as, to uplift the arm; to uplift a rock. Cowper. Satan, talking to his nearest mate, With head uplift above the wave, and eyes That sparkling blazed. Milton.\n\nA raising or upheaval of strata so as to disturb their regularity and uniformity, and to occasion folds, dislocations, and the like.
