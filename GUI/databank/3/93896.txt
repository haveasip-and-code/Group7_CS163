solute
1. Loose; free; liberal; as, a solute interpretation. [Obs.] Bacon. 2. Relaxed; hence; merry; cheerful. [R.] A brow solute, and ever-laughing eye. Young. 3. Soluble; as, a solute salt. [Obs.] 4. (Bot.)  Not adhering; loose; -- opposed to adnate; as, a solute stipule.\n\n1. To dissolve; to resolve. [Obs.] 2. To absolve; as, to solute sin. [Obs.] Bale.
