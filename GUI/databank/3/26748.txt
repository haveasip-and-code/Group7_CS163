thwack
1. To strike with something flat or heavy; to bang, or thrash: to thump. \"A distant thwacking sound.\" W. Irving. 2. To fill to overflow. [Obs.] Stanyhurst.\n\nA heavy blow with something flat or heavy; a thump. With many a stiff thwack, many a bang, Hard crab tree and old iron rang. Hudibras.
