comical
1. Relating to comedy. They deny it to be tragical because its catastrphe is a wedding, which hath ever been accounted comical. Gay. 2. Exciting mirth; droll; laughable; as, a comical story. \"Comical adventures.\" Dryden. Syn. -- Humorous; laughable; funny. See Droll. -- Com\"ic*al*ly, adv. -- Com\"ic*al\"ness, n.
