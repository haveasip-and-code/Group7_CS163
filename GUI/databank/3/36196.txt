heavenly
1. Pertaining to, resembling, or inhabiting heaven; celestial; not earthly; as, heavenly regions; heavenly music. As is the heavenly, such are they also that are heavenly. 1 Cor. xv. 48. 2. Appropriate to heaven in character or happiness; perfect; pure; supremely blessed; as, a heavenly race; the heavenly, throng. The love of heaven makes one heavenly. Sir P. Sidney.\n\n1. In a manner resembling that of heaven. \"She was heavenly true.\" Shak. 2. By the influence or agency of heaven. Out heavenly guided soul shall climb. Milton.
