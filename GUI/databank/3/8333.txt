downy
1. Covered with down, or with pubescence or soft hairs. \"A downy feather.\" Shak. Plants that . . . have downy or velvet rind upon their leaves. Bacon. 2. Made of, or resembling, down. Hence, figuratively: Soft; placid; soothing; quiet. \"A downy shower.\" Keble. \"Downy pillow.\" Pope. Time steals on with downy feet. Young. 3. Cunning; wary. [Slang, Eng.] Latham.
