embargo
An edict or order of the government prohibiting the departure of ships of commerce from some or all of the ports within its dominions; a prohibition to sail. Note: If the embargo is laid on an enemy's ships, it is called a hostile embargo; if on the ships belonging to citizens of the embargoing state, it is called a civil embargo.\n\nTo lay an embargo on and thus detain; to prohibit from leaving port; -- said of ships, also of commerce and goods.
