queer
1. At variance with what is usual or normal; differing in some odd way from what is ordinary; odd; singular; strange; whimsical; as, a queer story or act. \" A queer look.\" W. Irving. 2. Mysterious; suspicious; questionable; as, a queer transaction. [Colloq.]\n\nCounterfeit money. [Slang] To shove the queer, to put counterfeit money in circulation. [Slang]
