outcast
Cast out; degraded. \"Outcast, rejected.\" Longfellow.\n\n1. One who is cast out or expelled; an exile; one driven from home, society, or country; hence, often, a degraded person; a vagabond. The Lord . . . gathereth together the outcasts of Israel. Ps. cxlvii. 2. 2. A quarrel; a contention. [Scot.] Jamieson.
