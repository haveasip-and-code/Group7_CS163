hue
1. Color or shade of color; tint; dye. \"Flowers of all hue.\" Milton. Hues of the rich unfolding morn. Keble. 2. (Painting)  A predominant shade in a composition of primary colors; a primary color modified by combination with others.\n\nA shouting or vociferation. Hue and cry (Law), a loud outcry with which felons were anciently pursued, and which all who heard it were obliged to take up, joining in the pursuit till the malefactor was taken; in later usage, a written proclamation issued on the escape of a felon from prison, requiring all persons to aid in retaking him. Burrill.
