overturn
1. To turn or throw from a basis, foundation, or position; to overset; as, to overturn a carriage or a building. 2. To subvert; to destroy; to overthrow. 3. To overpower; to conquer. Milton. Syn. -- To demolish; overthrow. See Demolish.\n\nThe act off overturning, or the state of being overturned or subverted; overthrow; as, an overturn of parties.
