exclaim
To cry out from earnestness or passion; to utter with vehemence; to call out or declare loudly; to protest vehemently; to vociferate; to shout; as, to exclaim against oppression with wonder or astonishment; \"The field is won!\" he exclaimed.\n\nOutcry; clamor. [Archaic] Cursing cries and deep exclaims. Shak.
