sooty
1. Of or pertaining to soot; producing soot; soiled by soot. \"Fire of sooty coal.\" Milton. 2. Having a dark brown or black color like soot; fuliginous; dusky; dark. \"The grisly legions that troop under the sooty flag of Acheron.\" Milton. Sooty albatross (Zoöl.), an albatross (Phoebetria fuliginosa) found chiefly in the Pacific Ocean; -- called also nellie. -- Sooty tern (Zoöl.), a tern (Sterna fuliginosa) found chiefly in tropical seas.\n\nTo black or foul with soot. [R.] Sootied with noisome smoke. Chapman.
