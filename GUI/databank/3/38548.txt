adventitious
1. Added extrinsically; not essentially inherent; accidental or causal; additional; supervenient; foreign. To things of great dimensions, if we annex an adventitious idea of terror, they become without comparison greater. Burke. 2. (Nat. Hist.)  Out of the proper or usual place; as, adventitious buds or roots. 3. (Bot.)  Accidentally or sparingly spontaneous in a country or district; not fully naturalized; adventive; -- applied to foreign plants. 4. (Med.)  Acquired, as diseases; accidental. -- Ad`ven*ti\"tious*ly, adv. -- Ad`ven*ti\"tious*ness, n.
