calculation
1. The act or process, or the result, of calculating; computation; reckoning, estimate. \"The calculation of eclipses.\" Nichol. The mountain is not so his calculation makes it. Boyle. 2. An expectation based on cirumstances. The lazy gossips of the port, Abborrent of a calculation crost, Began to chafe as at a personal wrong. Tennyson.
