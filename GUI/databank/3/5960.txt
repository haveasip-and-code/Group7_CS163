vassal
1. (Feud. Law)  The grantee of a fief, feud, or fee; one who holds land of superior, and who vows fidelity and homage to him; a feudatory; a feudal tenant. Burrill. 2. A subject; a dependent; a servant; a slave. \"The vassals of his anger.\" Milton. Rear vassal, the vassal of a vassal; an arriere vassal.\n\nResembling a vassal; slavish; servile. The sun and every vassal star. Keble.\n\nTo treat as a vassal; to subject to control; to enslave. [Obs.] Beau. & Fl.
