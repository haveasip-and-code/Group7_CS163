three-piled
1. Having the quality of three-pile; best; most costly. [R.] Thou art good velvet; thou 'rt three-piled piece. Shak. 2. Fig.: Extravagant; exaggerated; high-flown. \"Three-piled hyperboles.\" Shak. 3. Accustomed to wearing three-pile; hence, of high rank, or wealth. [Obs.] \"Three-piled people.\" Beau. & Fl.
