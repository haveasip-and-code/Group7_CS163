somber
1. Dull; dusky; somewhat dark; gloomy; as, a somber forest; a somber house. 2. Melancholy; sad; grave; depressing; as, a somber person; somber reflections. The dinner was silent and somber; happily it was also short. Beaconsfield.\n\nTo make somber, or dark; to make shady. [R.]\n\nGloom; obscurity; duskiness; somberness. [Obs.]
