clump
1. An unshaped piece or mass of wood or other substance. 2. A cluster; a group; a thicket. A clump of shrubby trees. Hawthorne. 3. The compressed clay of coal strata. Brande & C.\n\nTo arrange in a clump or clumps; to cluster; to group. Blackmore.\n\nTo tread clumsily; to clamp. [Prov. Eng.] Halliwell.
