pleasing
Giving pleasure or satisfaction; causing agreeable emotion; agreeable; delightful; as, a pleasing prospect; pleasing manners. \"Pleasing harmony.\" Shak. \"Pleasing features.\" Macaulay. -- Pleas\"ing*ly, adv. -- Pleas\"ing*ness, n. Syn. -- Gratifying; delightful; agreeable. See Pleasant.\n\nAn object of pleasure. [Obs.] Chaucer.
