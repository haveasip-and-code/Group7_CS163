(Sugar Manuf.)  A charge of sirup in the pans. 5. A beehive; a skep.\n\n1. To leap lightly; to move in leaps and hounds; -- commonly implying a sportive spirit. Fig.: To leave matters unnoticed, as in reading, speaking, or writing; to pass by, or overlook, portions of a thing; -- often followed by over.\n\n1. To leap lightly over; as, to skip the rope. 2.
