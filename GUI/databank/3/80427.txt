fraise
A large and thick pancake, with slices of bacon in it. [Obs.] Johnson.\n\n1. (Fort.)  A defense consisting of pointed stakes driven into the ramparts in a horizontal or inclined position. 2. (Mech.)  A fluted reamer for enlarging holes in stone; a small milling cutter.\n\nTo protect, as a line of troops, against an onset of cavalry, by opposing bayonets raised obliquely forward. Wilhelm.
