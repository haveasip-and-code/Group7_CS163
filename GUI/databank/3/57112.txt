langued
Tongued; having the tongue visible. Lions . . . represented as armed and langued gules. Cussans. LANGUE D'OC Langue` d'oc\". Etym: [F., language of oc yes.]  The dialect, closely akin to French, formerly spoken south of the Loire (in which the word for \"yes\" was oc); Provencal. LANGUE D'OIL Langue` d'oïl\". Etym: [F., language of oïl yes.]  The dialect formerly spoken north of the Loire (in which the word for \"yes\" was oïl, F. oui).
