princely
1. Of or relating to a prince; regal; royal; of highest rank or authority; as, princely birth, character, fortune, etc. 2. Suitable for, or becoming to, a prince; grand; august; munificent; magnificent; as, princely virtues; a princely fortune. \"Most princely gifts.\" Shak.\n\nIn a princely manner. My appetite was not princely got. Shak.
