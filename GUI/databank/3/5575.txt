discoursive
1. Reasoning; characterized by reasoning; passing from premises to consequences; discursive. Milton. 2. Containing dialogue or conversation; interlocutory. The epic is everywhere interlaced with dialogue or discoursive scenes. Dryden. 3. Inclined to converse; conversable; communicative; as, a discoursive man. [R.]\n\nThe state or quality of being discoursive or able to reason. [R.] Feltham.
