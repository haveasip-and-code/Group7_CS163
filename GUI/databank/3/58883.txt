congregate
Collected; compact; close. [R.] Bacon.\n\nTo collect into an assembly or assemblage; to assemble; to bring into one place, or into a united body; to gather together; to mass; to compact. Any multitude of Christian men congregated may be termed by the name of a church. Hooker. Cold congregates all bodies. Coleridge. The great receptacle Of congregated waters he called Seas. Milton.\n\nTo come together; to assemble; to meet. Even there where merchants most do congregate. Shak.
