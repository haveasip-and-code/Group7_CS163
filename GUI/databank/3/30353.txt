crutched
1. Supported upon crutches. 2. Etym: [See Crouch, v. t., and Crouched, a. ]  Marked with the sign of the cross; crouched. Crutched friar (Eccl.), one of a religious order, so called because its members bore the sign of the cross on their staves and habits; -- called also crossed friar and crouched friar.
