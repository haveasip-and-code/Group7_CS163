faultless
Without fault; not defective or imperfect; free from blemish; free from incorrectness, vice, or offense; perfect; as, a faultless poem. Whoever thinks a faultless piece to see, Thinks what ne'er was, nor is, nor e'er shall be. Pope. Syn. -- Blameless; spotless; perfect. See Blameless. -- Fault\"less*ly, adv.-Fault\"less*ness, n.
