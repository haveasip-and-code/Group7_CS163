one-sided
1. Having one side only, or one side prominent; hence, limited to one side; partial; unjust; unfair; as, a one-sided view or statement. \"Unguarded and one-sided language.\" T. Arnold. 2. (Bot.)  Growing on one side of a stem; as, one-sided flowers. -- One`-sid\"ed-ly, adv. -- One`-sid\"ed*ness, n.
