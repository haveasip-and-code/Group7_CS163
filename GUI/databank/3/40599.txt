fusil
1. Capable of being melted or rendered fluid by heat; fusible. [R.] \"A kind of fusil marble\" Woodward. 2. Running or flowing, as a liquid. [R.] \"A fusil sea.\" J. Philips. 3. Formed by melting and pouring into a mold; cast; founded. [Obs.] Milton.\n\nA light kind of flintlock musket, formerly in use.\n\nA bearing of a rhomboidal figure; -- named from its shape, which resembles that of a spindle. Note: It differs from a lozenge in being longer in proportion to its width.
