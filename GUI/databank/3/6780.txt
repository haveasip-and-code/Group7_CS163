posset
A beverage composed of hot milk curdled by some strong infusion, as by wine, etc., -- much in favor formerly. \"I have drugged their posset.\" Shak.\n\n1. To curdle; to turn, as milk; to coagulate; as, to posset the blood. [Obs.] Shak. 2. To treat with possets; to pamper. [R.] \"She was cosseted and posseted.\" O. W. Holmes.
