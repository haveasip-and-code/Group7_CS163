sheen
Bright; glittering; radiant; fair; showy; sheeny. [R., except in poetry.] This holy maiden, that is so bright and sheen. Chaucer. Up rose each warrier bold and brave, Glistening in filed steel and armor sheen. Fairfax.\n\nTo shine; to glisten. [Poetic] This town, That, sheening far, celestial seems to be. Byron.\n\nBrightness; splendor; glitter. \"Throned in celestial sheen.\" Milton.
