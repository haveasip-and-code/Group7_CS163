inhabitant
1. One who dwells or resides permanently in a place, as distinguished from a transient lodger or visitor; as, an inhabitant of a house, a town, a city, county, or state. \"Frail inhabitants of earth.\" Cowper. In this place, they report that they saw inhabitants which were very fair and fat people. Abp. Abbot. 2. (Law)  One who has a legal settlement in a town, city, or parish; a permanent resident.
