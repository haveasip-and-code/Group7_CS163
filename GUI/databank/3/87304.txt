breakfast
1. The first meal in the day, or that which is eaten at the first meal. A sorry breakfast for my lord protector. Shak. 2. A meal after fasting, or food in general. The wolves will get a breakfast by my death. Dryden.\n\nTo break one's fast in the morning; too eat the first meal in the day. First, sir, I read, and then I breakfast. Prior.\n\nTo furnish with breakfast. Milton.
