pestiferous
1. Pest-bearing; pestilential; noxious to health; malignant; infectious; contagious; as, pestiferous bodies. \"Poor, pestiferous creatures begging alms.\" Evelyn. \"Unwholesome and pestiferous occupations.\" Burke. 2. Noxious to peace, to morals, or to society; vicious; hurtful; destructive; as, a pestiferous demagogue. Pestiferous reports of men very nobly held. Shak.
