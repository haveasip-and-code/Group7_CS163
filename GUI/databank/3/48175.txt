quadrate
1. Having four equal sides, the opposite sides parallel, and four right angles; square. Figures, some round, some triangle, some quadrate. Foxe. 2. Produced by multiplying a number by itself; square. \" Quadrate and cubical numbers.\" Sir T. Browne. 3. Square; even; balanced; equal; exact. [Archaic] \" A quadrate, solid, wise man.\" Howell. 4. Squared; suited; correspondent. [Archaic] \" A generical description quadrate to both.\" Harvey. Quadrate bone (Anat.), a bone between the base of the lower jaw and the skull in most vertebrates below the mammals. In reptiles and birds it articulates the lower jaw with the skull; in mammals it is represented by the malleus or incus.\n\n1. (Geom.)
