lenient
1. Relaxing; emollient; softening; assuasive; -- some \"Lenient of grief.\" Milton. Of relax the fibers, are lenient, balsamic. Arbuthnot. Time, that on all things lays his lenient hand. Pope. 2. Mild; clement; merciful; not rigorous or severe; as, a lenient disposition; a lenient judge or sentence.\n\nA lenitive; an emollient.
