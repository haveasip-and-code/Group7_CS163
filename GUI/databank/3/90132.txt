eruptive
1. Breaking out or bursting forth. The sudden glance Appears far south eruptive through the cloud. Thomson. 2. (Med.)  Attended with eruption or efflorescence, or producing it; as, an eruptive fever. 3. (Geol.)  Produced by eruption; as, eruptive rocks, such as the igneous or volcanic.\n\nAn eruptive rock.
