bunsen cell
A zinc-carbon cell in which the zinc (amalgamated) is surrounded by dilute sulphuric acid, and the carbon by nitric acid or a chromic acid mixture, the two plates being separated by a porous cup. BUNSEN'S BATTERY; BUNSEN'S BURNER Bun\"sen's bat\"ter*y, Bun\"sen's burn`er.  See under Battery, and Burner.
