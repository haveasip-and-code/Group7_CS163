butcherly
Like a butcher; without compunction; savage; bloody; inhuman; fell. \"The victim of a butcherly murder.\" D. Webster. What stratagems, how fell, how butcherly, This deadly quarrel daily doth beget! Shak. BUTCHER'S BROOM Butch\"er's broom`. (Bot.)  A genus of plants (Ruscus); esp. R. aculeatus, which has large red berries and leaflike branches. See Cladophyll.
