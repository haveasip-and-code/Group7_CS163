battel
A single combat; as, trial by battel. See Wager of battel, under Wager.\n\nProvisions ordered from the buttery; also, the charges for them; -- only in the pl., except when used adjectively. [Univ. of Oxford, Eng.]\n\nTo be supplied with provisions from the buttery. [Univ. of Oxford, Eng.]\n\nTo make fertile. [Obs.] \"To battel barren land.\" Ray.\n\nFertile; fruitful; productive. [Obs.] A battel soil for grain, for pasture good. Fairfax.
