monseigneur
My lord; -- a title in France of a person of high birth or rank; as, Monseigneur the Prince, or Monseigneur the Archibishop. It was given, specifically, to the dauphin, before the Revolution of 1789. (Abbrev. Mgr.) MONSEL'S SALT Mon\"sel's salt`. (Med.)  A basic sulphate of iron; -- so named from Monsel, a Frenchman. MONSEL'S SOLUTION Mon\"sel's so*lu\"tion. Etym: [See Monsel's salt.] (Med.)  An aqueous solution of Monsel's salt, having valuable styptic properties.
