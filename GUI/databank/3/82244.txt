forehanded
1. Early; timely; seasonable. \"Forehanded care.\" Jer. Taylor. 2. Beforehand with one's needs, or having resources in advance of one's necessities; in easy circumstances; as, a forehanded farmer. [U.S.] 3. Formed in the forehand or fore parts. A substantial, true-bred beast, bravely forehanded. Dryden.
