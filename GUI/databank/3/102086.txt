benediction
1. The act of blessing. 2. A blessing; an expression of blessing, prayer, or kind wishes in favor of any person or thing; a solemn or affectionate invocation of happiness. So saying, he arose; whom Adam thus Followed with benediction. Milton. Homeward serenely she walked with God's benediction upon her. Longfellow. Specifically:  The short prayer which closes public worship; as, to give the benediction. 3. (Eccl.)  The form of instituting an abbot, answering to the consecration of a bishop. Ayliffe. 4. (R. C. Ch.)  A solemn rite by which bells, banners, candles, etc., are blessed with holy water, and formally dedicated to God.
