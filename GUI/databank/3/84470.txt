fob
A little pocket for a watch. Fob chain, a short watch chain worn a watch carried in the fob.\n\n1. To beat; to maul. [Obs.] 2. To cheat; to trick; to impose on. Shak. To fob off, to shift off by an artifice; to put aside; to delude with a trick.\"A conspiracy of bishops could prostrate and fob off the right of the people.\" Milton.
