adherent
1. Sticking; clinging; adhering. Pope. 2. Attached as an attribute or circumstance. 3. (Bot.)  Congenitally united with an organ of another kind, as calyx with ovary, or stamens with petals.\n\n1. One who adheres; one who adheres; one who follows a leader, party, or profession; a follower, or partisan; a believer in a particular faith or church. 2. That which adheres; an appendage. [R.] Milton. Syn. -- Follower; partisan; upholder; disciple; supporter; dependent; ally; backer.
