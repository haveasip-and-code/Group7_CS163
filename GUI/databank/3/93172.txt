rounding
Round or nearly round; becoming round; roundish.\n\n1. (Naut.)  Small rope, or strands of rope, or spun yarn, wound round a rope to keep it from chafing; -- called also service. 2. (Phonetics)  Modifying a speech sound by contraction of the lip opening; labializing; labialization. See Guide to Pronunciation, ยง 11.
