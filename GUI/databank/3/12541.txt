sententious
1. Abounding with sentences, axioms, and maxims; full of meaning; terse and energetic in expression; pithy; as, a sententious style or discourse; sententious truth. How he apes his sire, Ambitiously sententious! Addison. 2. Comprising or representing sentences; sentential. [Obs.] \"Sententious marks.\" Grew. --- Sen*ten\"tious*ly, adv. -- Sen*ten\"tious*ness, n.
