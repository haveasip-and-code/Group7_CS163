crowfoot
1. (Bot.)  The genus Ranunculus, of many species; some are common weeds, others are flowering plants of considerable beauty. 2. (Naut.)  A number of small cords rove through a long block, or euphroe, to suspend an awning by. 3. (Mil.)  A caltrop. [Written also crow's-foot.] 4. (Well Boring)  A tool with a side claw for recovering broken rods, etc. Raymond.
