skipjack
1. An upstart. [Obs.] Ford. 2. (Zoöl.)  An elater; a snap bug, or snapping beetle. 3. (Zoöl.)  A name given to several kinds of a fish, as the common bluefish, the alewife, the bonito, the butterfish, the cutlass fish, the jurel, the leather jacket, the runner, the saurel, the saury, the threadfish, etc. 4. (Naut.)  A shallow sailboat with a rectilinear or V-shaped cross section.
