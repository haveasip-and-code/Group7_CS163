brant
A species of wild goose (Branta bernicla) -- called also brent and brand goose. The name is also applied to other related species.\n\nSteep. [Prov. Eng.]\n\n1. Steep; high. [Obs.] Grapes grow on the brant rocks so wonderfully that ye will marvel how any man dare climb up to them. Ascham. 2. Smooth; unwrinkled. [Scot.] Your bonnie brow was brent. Burns.
