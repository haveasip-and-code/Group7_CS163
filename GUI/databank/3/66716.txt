dandie
One of a breed of small terriers; -- called also Dandie Dinmont.\n\n1.  In Scott's \"Guy Mannering\", a Border farmer of eccentric but fine character, who owns two terriers claimed to be the progenitors of the Dandie Dinmont terriers. 2.  One of a breed of terriers with short legs, long body, and rough coat, originating in the country about the English and Scotch border.
