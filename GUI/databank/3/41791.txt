spicy
1. Flavored with, or containing, spice or spices; fragrant; aromatic; as, spicy breezes. \"The spicy nut-brown ale.\" Milton. Led by new stars, and borne by spicy gales. Pope. 2. Producing, or abounding with, spices. In hot Ceylon spicy forests grew. Dryden. 3. Fig.: Piquant; racy; as, a spicy debate. Syn. -- Aromatic; fragrant; smart; pungent; pointed; keen. See Racy.
