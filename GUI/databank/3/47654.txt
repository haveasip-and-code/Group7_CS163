lieberkuehn
A concave metallic mirror attached to the object-glass end of a microscope, to throw down light on opaque objects; a reflector. LIEBERKUHN'S GLANDS; LIEBERKUEHN'S GLANDS Lie\"ber*kühn's glands`. Etym: [See Lieberkühn.] (Anat.)  The simple tubular glands of the small intestines; -- called also crypts of Lieberkühn.
