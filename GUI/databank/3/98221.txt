handfast
1. Hold; grasp; custody; power of confining or keeping. [Obs.] Shak. 2. Contract; specifically, espousal. [Obs.]\n\nFast by contract; betrothed by joining hands. [Obs.] Bale.\n\nTo pledge; to bind; to betroth by joining hands, in order to cohabitation, before the celebration of marriage. [Obs.]\n\nStrong; steadfast.[R.] Carlyle.
