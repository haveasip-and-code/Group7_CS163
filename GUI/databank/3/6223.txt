windward
The point or side from which the wind blows; as, to ply to the windward; -- opposed to Ant: leeward. To lay an anchor to the windward, a figurative expression, signifying to adopt precautionary or anticipatory measures for success or security.\n\nSituated toward the point from which the wind blows; as, the Windward Islands.\n\nToward the wind; in the direction from which the wind blows.
