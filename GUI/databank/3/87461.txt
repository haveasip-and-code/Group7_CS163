tattle
1. To prate; to talk idly; to use many words with little meaning; to chat. The tattling quality of age, which is always narrative. Dryden. 2. To tell tales; to communicate secrets; to be a talebearer; as, a tattling girl.\n\nIdle talk or chat; trifling talk; prate. [They] told the tattle of the day. Swift.
