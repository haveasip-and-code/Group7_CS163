Herring-shaped. HARE'S-EAR Hare's\"-ear`, n. (Bot.)  An umbelliferous plant (Bupleurum rotundifolium ); -- so named from the shape of its leaves. Dr. Prior. HARE'S-FOOT FERN Hare's\"-foot` fern`. (Bot.)  A species of fern (Davallia Canariensis) with a soft, gray, hairy rootstock; -- whence the name. HARE'S-TAIL Hare's\"-tail` (-tal`), n. (Bot.)  A kind of grass (Eriophorum vaginatum). See Cotton grass, under Cotton. Hare's-tail grass (Bot.), a species of grass (Lagurus ovatus) whose head resembles a hare's tail.
