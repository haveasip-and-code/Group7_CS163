elongation
1. The act of lengthening, or the state of being lengthened; protraction; extension. \"Elongation of the fibers.\" Arbuthnot. 2. That which lengthens out; continuation. May not the mountains of Westmoreland and Cumberland be considered as elongations of these two chains Pinkerton. 3. Removal to a distance; withdrawal; a being at a distance; distance. The distant points in the celestial expanse appear to the eye in so small a degree of elongation from one another, as bears no proportion to what is real. Glanvill. 4. (Astron.)  The angular distance of a planet from the sun; as, the elongation of Venus or Mercury.
