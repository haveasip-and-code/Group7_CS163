puke
To eject the contests of the stomach; to vomit; to spew. The infant Mewling and puking in the nurse's arms. Shak.\n\nTo eject from the stomach; to vomit up.\n\nA medicine that causes vomiting; an emetic; a vomit.\n\nOf a color supposed to be between black and russet. Shak. Note: This color has by some been regarded as the same with puce; but Nares questions the identity.
