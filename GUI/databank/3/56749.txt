gab
The hook on the end of an eccentric rod opposite the strap. See. Illust. of Eccentric.\n\nThe mouth; hence, idle prate; chatter; unmeaning talk; loquaciousness. [Colloq.] Gift of gab, facility of expression. [Colloq.]\n\n1. To deceive; to lie. [Obs.] Chaucer. 2. To talk idly; to prate; to chatter. Holinshed.
