rat-tail
Like a rat's tale in form; as, a rat-tail file, which is round, slender, and tapering. See Illust. of File.\n\n1. (Far.) pl.  An excrescence growing from the pastern to the middle of the shank of a horse. 2. (Zoöl.) (a) The California chimæra. See Chimæra. (b) Any fish of the genus Macrurus. See Grenadier, 2.
