retrace
1. To trace back, as a line. Then if the line of Turnus you retrace, He springs from Inachus of Argive race. Driden. 2. To go back, in or over (a previous course); to go over again in a reverse direction; as, to retrace one's steps; to retrace one's proceedings. 3. To trace over again, or renew the outline of, as a drawing; to draw again.
