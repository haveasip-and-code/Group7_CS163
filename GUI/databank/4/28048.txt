cooch (or coochie)
Of or pertaining to a woman's sexual orifices or region. Not usually used in a derogatory or derisive fashion. Rather, the meaning has subtle references to pleasure and often of respect or reverence. ; Plural - coochies - ; Hist. - derivation of 20's and 30's Entertainers known as 'Hoochie -[Coochie] Girls.'
