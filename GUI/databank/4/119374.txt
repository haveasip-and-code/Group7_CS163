Aimetti
Meaning: Origianally used to denote someone who is good at first-person shooter video games. Eventually adapted to be used as a synonym for good. Became especially popular because of the implications that someone is using hacks when called 'aimetti' beacause the first three letters are AIM, which is a type of Counter-Strike hack. ; Origin: Named after professional Counter-Strike player Dan Aimetti. At the time the word started being used, he played for team zEx.
