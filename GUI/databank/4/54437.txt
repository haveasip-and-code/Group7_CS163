Arts of Boabom
Various systems that comprise Boabom. There are three basic courses, which are: ; -Seamm-Jasani (or Gentle Boabom), which is a form of Active Relaxation ; -Osseous Boabom (or Magnetic self-defense) ; -Yaanbao (or Boabom with Implements). ; All these systems are taught at the Mmulargan School.
