cowboy killer
A drink / game at bars. Isn't very popular. Requires two slices of lemon, a shot of tequila, and a rubber band. ; 1) Rubber band is placed firmly around forehead. ; 2) Lemon slices are squeezed against band-wearer's eyelids. ; 3) Band-wearer fumbles blindly for shot of tequila. ; 4) Drink tequila. ; 5) Stretch rubber band away from forehead, then let go at distance of at least four inches.
