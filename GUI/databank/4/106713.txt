HONaluha
Prounced: Ha-NAA-Lu-Ya ; Synonym: Hallelujah ; Type: Slang ; Function: interjection & noun ; Interj: Used to express Joy or praise. Often used or associated in meaning with its Hebrew counterpart Hallelujah. While the development history of this word roots back to the coast of Hawaii, it is still used within sporadically Midwest (Ohio/Illinois). ; Noun: Music.
