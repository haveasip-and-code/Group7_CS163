gothmo
gothmo is a mix of [goth] and [emo] styles, coined by Colin, the assistant manager of hot topic in the rockaway mall in northern new jersey. gothmo incorporated pinstripe button down shirts with t-shirts over it. belt buckles which can be considered goth and emo at the same time. its the sensitive dark side. in essence, take an emo boy/girl and add black. its brooding and cool without being scary or too cry in the corner-ey. its been around for a while, just not named. edgy emo-ness.
