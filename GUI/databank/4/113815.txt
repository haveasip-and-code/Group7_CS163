motaba
1.) The fictional disease, based loosely off Ebola, which appeared in the movie Outbreak. It was later referenced in an episode of Clerks: the Animated Series. ; 2.) Any 'bug' which afflicts masses of people living in close proximity--typically college dorms, offices, etc--with no explainable cause or origin. ; 3.) A 'bug' which is more psychological than physical, and spreads rapidly amongst people with little to no physical contact.
