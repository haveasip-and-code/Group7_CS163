Ghosting
Two more definitions. ; 1. (verb) Ghosting : When you have a TV with a crappy rabbit ear antenna and the signal is a bit off and you see some 'ghosting' on the screen. ; 2. (verb) A Computer geek term refering to the Software Ghost used to backup computer hard drives and make images. Especially useful when backing up a drive that will not boot properly. Also used to make images for distribution.
