mahooter
noun, ma-who-der, mahooter, Also spelled mahooder, a word to be used when the real word for the item cannot be remembered, ; or when the word for an object is not known and you're trying to pretend it was forgotten, ; synonyms are [thing-a-ma-jig] [thing-a-ma-bob] [doo-hickey] ; word was created by Mark Romani.
