bisexuality
n. the sexual attraction to both genders, opposite and the same. ; A [bisexual] is someone who exhibits bisexuality. ; Bisexuality is one of the more commonly misunderstood of all sexualities, next to [transsexuality]. Some of the most common misconceptions is that it is a passing phase, whether into [homosexuality] (being gay) or from that into [heterosexuality] (being straight). Most often bisexual people are considered confused, and wierd, and many misinformed people are convinced that one can only be gay or straight.
