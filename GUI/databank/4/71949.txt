Bukake  also Bukkake
1.(v) to ejaculate on a woman's face. ; 2.(n) pornogaphic films where bukake is featured. ; The word has entered the language through the porn industry, in a direct line from Japanese. Bukkakeru is a Japanese verb meaning 'to dash (slosh) (water) on a person or in a person's face.' In Japan Bukkakeru movies are highly stylised events where sometimes dozens of men ejaculate, in succession, on the face of a single girl.
