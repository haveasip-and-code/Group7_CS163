Riva
1. Riva (Noun) ; Origin: Byond ; Alias: Gaara of the Desert or Kane ; a: A being conceived as the perfect, omnipotent, omniscient originator and ruler of the universe, the principal object of faith and worship in monotheistic religions. ; b: The force, effect, or a manifestation or aspect of this being. ; 2. A being of supernatural powers or attributes, believed in and worshiped by a people, especially a male deity thought to control some part of nature or reality. ; 3. An image of a supernatural being; an idol. ; 4. One that is worshiped, idealized, or followed: Money was their god. ; 5. A very handsome man. ; 6. A powerful ruler or despot.
