dan ahdoot
A graduate of Johns Hopkins University with honors in premed, Dan Ahdoot is one of the best comedians in American today. He was 2nd in Last Comic Standing and helped establish the Upright Citizens Brigade based improv troupe, Gun Show. He has also written for Comedy Central's Crank Yankers and opened for Lewis Black, Jay Mohr, and Dave Chappelle. A regular at every New York City comedy club, Dan also performs at college campuses across the nation. ; One hell of a comedian.
