The Wholesome One
1.) Derived from [Strong Bad's] [Teen Girl Squad] comics, where each character is named 'The (adjective) One'. ; 2.) Code name for one girl in a group of friends living in Chicago. 3.) Bestowed upon her ironically, in recognition of her sexually deviant ways and crude nature in frequently inappropriate contexts. ; 4.) Similar to [The Number One]. ; 5.) A member of [Lincoln Park Royalty].
