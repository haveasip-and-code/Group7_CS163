Rubber Soul
The sixth studio album by [the Beatles], released in Winter 1965. Rubber Soul is considered the turning point in the band's career, the Brit-Pop was now being intermingled with Dylanesque folk, distortion guitar, and minor psychedelia. Tracks are as follows: ; 1. Drive My Car ; 2. Norwegian Wood (This Bird Has Flown) ; 3. You Won't See Me ; 4. Nowhere Man ; 5. Think for Yourself ; 6. Word ; 7. Michelle ; 8. What Goes On ; 9. Girl ; 10. I'm Looking Through You ; 11. In My Life ; 12. Wait ; 13. If I Needed Someone ; 14. Run for Your Life
