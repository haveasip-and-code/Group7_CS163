Va' fa' un culo
Literally: Go do a ass ; Va' means 'you go' and comes from the Italian word 'Andare' which means 'to go' ; Fa' means 'you do' comes from the Italian word 'Fare' which means 'to do, or to make' ; un, is the indefinite article 'a' in masculine form. ; culo, means behind, or can be slang for bottom, or 'ass'
