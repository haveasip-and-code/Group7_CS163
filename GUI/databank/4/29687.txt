Funkathon
A funkathon is the naughties version of a house 'disco' which lasts longer than 24 hours. ; Funkathons differ from house parties in the following ways: ; *Much more alcohol ; *The attendance of many many people you barely know ; *Very loud and non-stop music ; *Smokey perfumed atmosphere ; *Sex in odd places ; *More than 24 hours of non-stop partying
