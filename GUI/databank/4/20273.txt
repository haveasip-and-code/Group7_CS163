Xel'Naga
A non-playable race in the Blizzard game [Starcraft]. Heard of only in the storyline, though never portrayed, they are supposedly the creators of the [Protoss] and the [Zerg]. The Xel'Naga creatures roamed the universe in travelling 'worldships', and breathed life into several species, in an attempt to create the perfect lifeform. ; The [Protoss] presented the purity of Form. ; The [Zerg] presented the purity of Essence. ; Though the Xel'Naga are now extinct, if the Protoss and Zerg would ever merge into one species, they would represent the Purity of Being.
