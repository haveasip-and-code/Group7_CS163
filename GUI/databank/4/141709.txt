Peveus
(noun) derived from the greek 'Perveos' meaning 'pokeable'. Other derivatives from 'Perveos' include 'impervious' which has the alternate meaning of 'unpokeable'. ; To describe someone as being 'peveus' you are similarly describing them as being 'pink-shirted' in nature, able to be poked and/or prodded, possibly perverse, a TinTin fan, downloader and watcher of anime and with a hatred for large dark birds such as ravens or 'crows'.
