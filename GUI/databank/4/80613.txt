the first and the third
1) A waste of time. 2) an unsatisfactory gain from a considerable investment. 3) Sexual assault during the course of public higher education. 4) The worst of a bad choice. ; A derivation of a longer trueism: 'You only go to a large state college to get raped, alcohol poisoning, and a substandard education.' The first and the third (of that list) became synonymous with any situation involving getting the lesser two of any three unappealing options.
