A Japanese Rock/Rap/Metal group that excels in all of those areas. The lead singer, Yuri, has a wonderfully depthful voice; the rapper, Motsu, is an excellent complement to her style; and their composer, T-Kimura, is one who loves to dabble in every genre he can. ; They are best known for their unique niche in music, and wonderfully over the top style-but most importantly, their quality music.
