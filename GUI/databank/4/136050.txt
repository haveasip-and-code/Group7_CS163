Schle
Hebrew Slang: ; Pronounced S'-ch-le. ; 'Ch' as a spanish Xavier, 'Le' as the french prefix. ; 1. When lotsa people drink from the same bottle, may it be a plastic Coke bottle or a glass Beer bottle, the bottom 2 inches of drink left in said bottle, containing only 2% of the original drink, the rest reserved for mostly oral bodily fluids coming from more than one mouth, is commonly referred to as Schle. ; Usually with an eerie taste to it. ; 2. Also a reference to people from the contaminated, crummy shallow waters of the [Gene Pool].
