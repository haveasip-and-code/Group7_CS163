The Blues
1. A state of depression which becomes the norm after you've spent your whole life being a slave or a sharecropper or another shitty occupation. ; 2. The result of somebody with the aforementioned problem picking up an instrument and singing about it. ; 3. A specific chord progression that originated in the 19th Century in the American South from black slaves with the problem mentioned in (1) and the talent mentioned in (2).
