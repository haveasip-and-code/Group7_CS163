Inter
The second football team in the city of Milan. ; Founded in 1908, the Internazionale Football Club has won 13 'Scudetto', 3 'Coppa Italia', 2 Champions Cups, 3 UEFA Cups and 2 Intercontinental Cups. ; Inter is still one of the most famous football teams in the world (expecially in Japan)even though in the last 15 years the 'neroazzurri' won only one UEFA Cup.
