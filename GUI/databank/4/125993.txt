Amy Brenneman
Creator, executive producer, and star of 'Judging Amy' on CBS for 6 very successful seasons. Her breakout role was playing Janice Licarsi on the first season of 'NYPD Blue' in which she became famous for appearing in the FIRST ever risque sex scene on T.V. Married to director Brad Silberling. Has two children: Charlotte Tucker and Bodhi Russell.
