heterosexual
A person who is attracted to the opposite gender. Heterosexuality is the most common sexual orientation in the world (but by no means 'normal'. It is simply more common than some variations of human sexuality). ; Only tiny minorities are exclusivly heterosexual or exlusively homosexual (somewhere between 2 to 5% each); most are somewhere in between those two extremes. ; Slang terms for heterosexuials include [straight].
