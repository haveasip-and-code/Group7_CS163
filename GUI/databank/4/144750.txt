Morgan Hill
is a city located in the southern part of [Santa Clara County], California. Founded on 10 November 1906, the city was named after a local ranch owner, Hiram Morgan Hill. ; Commonly known as 'Mo Hill' ; Contrary to popular belief Morgan Hill doesn't have the most fast food restaurants per capita. ; Is the mushroom capitol of the world. ; As of the census(GR2) of 2000, there are 33,556 people, 10,846 households, and 8,633 families residing in the city.
