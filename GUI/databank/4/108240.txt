m4gery
A rifle, of the general configuration of the Colt M4(tm), that is not actually made by Colt. ; An M4 is generally considered to be of the M16 family and having the following features: A short gas system, a barrel weighted in front of the front sight post with a cut out to facilitate the mounting of an M203 grenade launcher, an adjustable stock and feed ramps that extend from the barrel extension into the upper receiver.
