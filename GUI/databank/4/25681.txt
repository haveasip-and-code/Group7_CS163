Dieseldyke
A lady (often quite large,) that prefers the (very) close company of other ladies, known as [femmes]. Regularly seen wearing a lumberjack shirt, boots and dungarees/bib & braces overalls. ; Origin: Due to the normal extreme mass of these 'ladies,' it has often been assumed that locomotion could only be facilitated with the assistance of a powerful Diesel engine. ; See also [Clamjouster], [Mingeeta], [Plaid-clad Whale] and [Rugmuncher]
