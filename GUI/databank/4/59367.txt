Phoenix Nights
A two series comedy written by Peter Kay, Neil Fitzmaurice and Dave Spikey about a social club owner and the happenings in his club and the staff and the rivalry between the owner, Brian Potter and Den Perry, an owner of a nearby club. ; Absolutely fantastic and very funny indeed, there are some fantastic catch phrases to some out of that show! ; Since the show finished there has been a spin off series, also shown on channel 4, following the two doormen, Max and Paddy. ; Peter Kay shows he can play numerous characters successfully, many people dont realise he also plays Max.
