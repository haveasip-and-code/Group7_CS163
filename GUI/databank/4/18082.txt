Type of [Bicycle] ridden by balancing on a small, hard piece of rubber known as a saddle. This type of bicycle causes several problems which include the following: ; Wrist pain ; Shoulder pain ; Neck pain ; Back pain ; Saddle sores ; The above problems are not present on a [recumbent] bicycle. ; Also known as a Diamond-Frame (DF) bicycle, a UCI bike, an Upright or a wedgie bike.
