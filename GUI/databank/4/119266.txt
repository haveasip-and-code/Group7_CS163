Koopa
1. Nintendo characters; A race of turtle-like reptiles that come in all shapes and sizes. Most of them are Bowser's servants, but there are also a lot of good Koopas. Bowser's Koopas have many various soldier ranks, most common of which is 'Koopa Troopas' (which is another word for 'troopers', if I'm not mistaken). ; 2. Koopa is also the name of their king.
