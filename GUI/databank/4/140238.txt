beat boy
Beat Boy means Break Boy, B-Boy, or Bronx Boy. ; Back in the days of original Hip Hop the original Beat Boys would dress as fly as possible, and part of the flava of a Beat Boy would be how he could do all of his moves and never get dirty. ; Beat Boys would go to a jam, dance with some females, socialize, and when the right break beats came on, they'd get down, and after gettin down, they would get back to partying.
