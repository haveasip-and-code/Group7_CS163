byob
BYOB is an acronym with several similar yet related expansions. The acronym is most often associated with a social gathering (a party) at which the host will not be providing alcoholic beverages; the guests are invited to bring a beverage of their choice for their own consumption. ; Common expansions of the acronym include: ; Bring Your Own Bottle ; Bring Your Own Booze ; Bring Your Own Beer ; Bring Your Own Beverage
