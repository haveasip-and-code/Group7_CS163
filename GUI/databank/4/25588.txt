Guatemalan bake sale
1. (n) - a bake sale held exclusively in Guatemala, usually featuring traditionaly Guatemalan baked goods ; 2. (v) - to utterly destroy an opponent in verbal, physical, or electronic battle in a quick or excessively voilent manner ; derived from the vigor with which Guatemalan women push their goods onto prospective consumers, and the aftermath that can result from over-zealous advertisment of said baked goods.
