couchsurf
To sleep at someone elses' home (esp. on the individual's couch) for an indefinite period of time. An act perpetrated mostly by college students, [slacker]s, [junkie]s, [skater]s, snowboarders, [punk]s, [raver]s, [clubber]s, goths, [hippie]s, cult members, or any other individual in need of a place to stay and willing to mooch said place from a friend, relative, or complete stranger willing to oblige them.
