roiderize
Used on online forums, refers to when a person gets upset at something and goes through and edits their posts, removing everything they're saying and replacing it with a period, leaving shells of the conversation for people to guess at. Sometimes is done when the person is publicly humiliated, but more often is done when a person simply wants to erase their existance from the forum and have nothing to do with it any longer. ; Named after a person who did it and removed over 1000 posts in a single thread.
