Vampire Hunter D
By Hideuki Kikuchi. An anime starring D, a [dhampire]/[dhampile] who hunts vampires for a living. Apparantly his left hand can talk. ; D originated where that Kikuchi wanted to write a horror story based off folklore and Western movies, with a character suffering from the duality of good and evil. His first mental image of him was inspired by the gunfighter video of Phil Collins.
