THotD
Ancronym (the first T is commonly ignored, intended to have each letter spelt out 'H-O-T-D' and not pronounced as 'Hot Dee') for The House of the Dead a now classic Arcade Shooter (traditional aim your gun at a screen and fire and aim off the screen to reload and nothing else type game) about two agents from a paranormal defense organization on a mission to thwart the plans of a madman making animatronic creatures under the disguise of zombies.
