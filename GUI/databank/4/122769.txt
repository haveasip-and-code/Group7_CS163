DDRsteal-a-dew
Noun: An event where someone makes a visit to the home of a freind/relative/acquaintence for a short videogame session, and then use that as an excuse to steal a canned beverage from the fridge of said friend/relative/acquaintance. ; Etymology: derived from the words 'DDR'([Dance Dance Revolution]: a popular rhythm/music game developed by KONAMI) 'steal'(the act of maliciously taking something that does not belong to you) and 'Dew'(shortened variation of [Mountain Dew], a [Pepsi] product)
