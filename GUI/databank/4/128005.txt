Starbursts
The only candy that he who is without sin would eat. ; The original Starbursts flavors are strawberry(the precious), cherry, orange, and lemon. ; The California flavors are red-cherry, strawberry-watermelon, raspberry, and plum. ; There are also sour versions of cherry, green-apple, blue-raspberry, and watermelon. Eating the sours will result in 'that face'. ; The Tropical flavors are tropical punch, strawberry-banana, kiwi-banana, and mango-melon.
