prima donna
From [Italian] words meaning 'first [woman]' or 'first [lady]', depending on your preference, the prima donna is the leading lady in a performance, and especially in [opera]. Can also be called a [diva]. ; Is now often used to describe someone [arrogant], [vain], or just plain bitchy. Diva and prima donna have become synonomous with a show-off or a [bitch], regardless of whether or not the person in question is male or female.
