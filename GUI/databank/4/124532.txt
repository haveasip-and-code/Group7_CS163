Giff
n. Giff, also giffey and giffer. ; Vulgar slang ; A person who is socially inept or who is a social outcast usually has several identity and physical problems. ; Usually unclean, possibly has a personality disorder. ; Origin ; Jonathan Mark Schultz invented the word on the 28th August 2005 at 12 O clock. He started using the word as an alternative to tramp. He created the word to describe his stepfather and frequently used it for other people he disliked.
