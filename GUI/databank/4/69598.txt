RZA
From Brooklyn, RZA formed the Wu-Tang Clan. After releasing several albums, the Wu went their seperate ways, RZA being the most successful. He has released many solo albums under different alias' and produced film score for blockbusters such as Quentin Tarantino's 'Kill Bill' and Jim Jarmuch's 'Ghost Dog'.
