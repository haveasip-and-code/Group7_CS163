Papariffic
Adj. ; Describing something as good enough for the Pope; Describing something as to have Papal status or Abilities; Describing something as being the best around. ; Originating from the Ozy and Millie cartoon strip on 5th April, 1999 (www.ozyandmillie.org/1999/om19990405.html) ; which was commenting on the social [taboo] becomming a thing of the past.
