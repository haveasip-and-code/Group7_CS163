ruined
1.Derived from the act of reckoning the term 'ruined' most commonly is uttered through the lips of a close friend who has decicrated you in an epic struggle of some sort i.e. a video game. ; 2.An insult often hurled at elderly, mentally challenged, or just people with poor motor skills when they fall or get hurt in some manner that is to the view hilarious. ; 3.Often coupled with such phrases as 'Face' and 'Pwned' followed by open palm thrusts or pointing respectively.
