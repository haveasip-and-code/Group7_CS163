Fox Headed
1. Someone who disdains all other news sources other than talk radio and Fox News. This person typically needs all news to be provided in a way that is congruent with their reactionary political philosophies. Any news presented in a way that conflicts with their view of the world is 'liberal'. Short of rape or murder, Fox Headed people will support conservatives no matter what. ; 2. One who has a hard time with any sort of cognitive dissonance with respect to news events.
