Tatsu-maki-sempuu-kyaku
The 'Hurricane Kick' from the world-renowned Street Fighter games. The Ansatuken-users (more commonly known as the shotos to uneducated people) preform this move. [ryu], [Ken], and [Akuma] can preform this technique.
