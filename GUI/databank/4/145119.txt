poonut butter
Poonut butter is a delicious treat, similar in consistency to Gnutella. Here is the receipe for Poonut Butter: ; 1. Eat only bran muffins for a whole day ; 2. Eat only bran muffins for the next day ; 3. Eat only bran muffins for the next day ; 4. On the 4th day, begin harvesting your poop. It will be in small 'poonuts' from all the bran. Put 1 pound of poonuts into the food processor along with a 1/2 cup of olive oil. Blend until smooth. Serve poonut butter on bread or baked in a pie.
