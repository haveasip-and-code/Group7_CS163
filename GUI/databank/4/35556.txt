Huehnerficker
A German swearword meaning [wanker],[tosser],[arsehole], [motherfucker], [knobhead] or any other similarly derogatory term used to describe a person. ; (literal meaning = chickenfucker) ; A most versatile word, which can be used to great effect in a wide variety of contexts. Particularly useful when negotiating the removal of a towel which has been placed on a sunbed at an ungodly hour for the purposes of unlawfully establishing ownership rights to said sunbed.
