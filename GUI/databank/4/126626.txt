maneech
root words: neech, nage, ownage ; Used to describe a situation where the sheer awesomeness of the circumstance is so intensely overwhelming that it cannot be accurately described with authentic english words. It is commonly seen in the form of all capial letters. It is also not rare to see the 'a' and the 'e' in the word repeated excessively. Word is commonly followed by the root word neech to intensify the effect.
