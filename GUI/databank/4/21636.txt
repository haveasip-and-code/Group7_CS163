Sootfaces
A special [avatar] as a result of [Gaia Online]'s 'April Fools' Day 2004' YGS box event. ; Your [avatar]'s face became covered with [soot], and it remained for the entire day. ; A Sootface will revert to its previous condition after saving changes to the [avatar], but some users liked the look and decided to keep it. ; This status was intended to be temporary, and therefore CANNOT be recovered after it is gone. ; ...See also [Invisibob], as they are directly related to the Sootface.
