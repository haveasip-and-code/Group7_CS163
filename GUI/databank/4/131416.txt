Taco Bell Challenge
The act of purchasing the 'Grande Meal' consisting of 10 tacos, to be made up of 5 hard shell and 5 soft shell tacos, and consuming every last shred of each taco within a half hour. The rules are simple: ; 1) Eat EVERYTHING. This includes every shred of lettuce. ; 2) Choice of Border Sauce and drinks is left to the Challenger.
