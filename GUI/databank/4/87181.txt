that's what she said
1. Used to add sexual innuendo to a conversation. ; 2. When a male wants to share with his friends that he was with a girl (whether her was or not), he will respond to a phrase that sets up 'that's what she said...' as funny or sexual. ; 3. To imply that what is being talked about was actually a sexual statement and that a girl agreed to said statement in the recent past. ; 4. Something people say after a phrase is said by someone else to add emphasis. ; 5. A phrase said by one male to another after a story about her was told to imply that he was with the first male's girl after him.
