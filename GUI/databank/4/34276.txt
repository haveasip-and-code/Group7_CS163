decomplexification
1. The process used to simplify a useful system or process that was once simple, but because of its use over time has become complex, unwieldy, or unusable. ; 2. The process used to make something easier out of something worthwhile that is not easy to use. ; Synonym: simplify ; Origin: Don Morgan created this humorous, oxymoron-like, but useful word around 1994 while serving in the U.S. Army.
