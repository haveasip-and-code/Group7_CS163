Neo-Conservative
American conservative group that experienced after Ronald Reagan. [Neo-Cons] typically beleive in Supply side economics, a free market, and social conservatism. (IE: anti-abortion and anti-gay marriage) It is (as of the writing of this definition) the largest group of republicans, including people such as George W. Bush, Tom Delay, and Dick Cheney. ; Other powerful factions of the Republican party are the Evangelical Protestants, and the Libertarians.
