Pushpa
An odd specim who also goes by the names: Diamond Trim P, Trick Magnet Isaac Rockefeller and P. Sometimes she even thinks she is a doctor who goes by the name 'Dr.P'. She is related to a [Krupa] at times, who is the light of her world (some might say she's a 'bundle of hope'). ; She may be seen running around making obscene noises such as 'hoodie' or TOUCHFAT. ; Be on your guard when the touchfat call is heard...
