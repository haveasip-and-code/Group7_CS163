Vengeance
1. A punishment inflicted in retaliation for injury or offense. Often disproportionate to the injury done. ; 2. [Revenge] or [retribution] exacted with extreme prejudice, enthusiasm, and passion. ; 3. Retaliation by extreme measures, or carried to the extreme. ; 4. (-with a VENGEANCE) with great force or [vehemence]; to an extreme or excessive degree.
