Evil Atheist Conspiracy
The Evil Atheist Conspiracy, or EAC, is a [satire] of the views that many believers hold regarding [atheist]s. According to the story, the EAC plans to conquer the world, persecute [Christian]s, and abolish everything good. Comments on the EAC frequently involve black helicopters, mind-control rays, and members' duty to deny the existence of the EAC.
