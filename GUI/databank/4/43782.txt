walking on eggshells
To be in a delicate situation; to be on the edge of danger or ruin. ; An 'eggshell' is the thin, white outer coating of an egg. The shell is very thin and breaks easily. So if you are 'walking on eggshells' you are in a situation where you could break something (or get in trouble or ruin everything) very easily. ; Synonym: [walking on thin ice]
