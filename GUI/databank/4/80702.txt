ding-ding
1 n. small [penis], refers to the tendency of small [penis]es to bounce around easily even when erect. Possible derivative of '[dinky]', or referring to the '[dinger]' of a bell, which swings back and forth, causing the bell to ring. Generally a derogatory comment. ; 2. adj. Used to describe someone who is [insane] or '[not quite all there]' mentally.
