Lost in Translation
An amazing film written and directed by Sofia Coppola and starring Scarlett Johansson and Bill Murray. ; It's about two people who are visiting Japan and who feel lost in life. Their chance meeting leads to a touching and intimate friendship and the film follows their attemts to find themselves in a strangely familiar and beautiful yet alien and bewildering environment. ; The film is shot beautifully and the music combines with amazing shots of Tokyo to produce an incredibly moving film.
