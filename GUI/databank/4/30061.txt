skunk
1. ; Any of several small, mostly carnivorous New World mammals of the genus Mephitis and related genera, having a bushy tail and black fur with white markings and ejecting a foul-smelling oily liquid from glands near the anus when frightened or in danger. Also called polecat. ; 2. Slang. ; 1. A person regarded as obnoxious or despicable. ; 2. A person whose company is avoided.
