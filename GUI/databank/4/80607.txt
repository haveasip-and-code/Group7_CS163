gungrave
a great anime series and an underated game. Following the story of a ressurected guy called beyond the grave(or brandon) who needs revenge on the syndicate 'Millenion' who he used to work for. His old friend harry macdowell became the boss of millenion after getting rid of the old boss 'big daddy' who was close to brandon. And after brandon refused to kill big daddy macdowell shot him so brandon is after harry as well.
