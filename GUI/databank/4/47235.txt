Kikyou
A priestess from the anime/manga [InuYasha]. Kikyou was the one who sealed InuYasha to the tree of ages and then died and was reincarnated as [Kagome]. Kikyou then was brought back and know lives of the souls of the dead and a hate for InuYasha. Kikyou is a very misunderstood character and is one of the most hated.
