E-Klepto
Electronic Kleptomaniac. ; A person with an obsessive impulse to steal over the internet regardless of economic need. ; E-Kleptos are an increasingly large portion of internet users. As P2P networks continue to expand more and more electronic kleptomaniacs are emerging. Given the choice of paying for software, music, movies and other forms of electronic media or not paying for it, many are choosing to not pay.
