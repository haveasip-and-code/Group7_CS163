eah
a locution meant to express an indifferent attitude or ambivalence. A phrase often adopted by 'fence-sitters' or people who are otherwise hesitant to take a position on some matter. ; Note: Can also be spelled 'eeaahh' or 'eeeaaahhh' (the extra letters indicating greater indifference). In extreme cases of indifference, one can add a 'bl' in front of 'eah', forming the phrase 'bleah' (although this phrase is less common). ; Note 2: the pitch in which this phrase is uttered can also indicate a greater or lesser degree of ambivalance (higher pitch indicating more ambivalence; lower pitch indicating less ambivalence)
