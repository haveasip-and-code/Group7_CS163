godpuppet
One whose body is controlled by their brain, whose brain is controlled by the soul, whose soul is guided by god. Assummes souls influence neither physical objects; like cars or watches, nor other's brains. ; [god]- A being conceived as the perfect, omnipotent, omniscient originator and ruler of the universe, the principal object of faith and worship in monotheistic religions.(Middle English, from Old English. See gheu) [puppet]- One whose behavior is determined by the will of others.(Middle English poppet, doll, possibly from Anglo-Norman poppe, doll)
