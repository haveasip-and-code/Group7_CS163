dubsciple
Dubsciple - dub-ci-ple, n. (eng. 2005): ; 1. one who adheres to the movement of dub ; 2. one who spreads the philosophy of dub ; - see also 'Dub': a derivation of roots reggae noted for it's general lack of lyrics and driving heartbeat like bass lines; pioneered by trench town (kingston, Jamaica slums) dj's/producers in the 1960s.
