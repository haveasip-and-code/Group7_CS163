manic depresion
Manic depression is a [bipolar] mental disorder with varying periods of high highs and low lows. Sufferers of manic depression have both depresion and anxiety. Sufferers also sometimes have mood swings (happy one minute and depressed or scared another minute). see [anxiety] and also [depression]. ; Those with manic depression often will have anxiety attacks for a period of time followed by a period of depression.
