PoK
PoK is an acronym used by the players of the [MMORPG] [Everquest], in order to easily refer to the Plane of Knowledge. This is a [zone] that was introduced to the [game] in the [expansion set], Everquest: Planes of Power, which coincidentally enough, users of Everquest often refer to as [PoP]. ; The Plane of Knowledge is a central area to the [game], in which people may access distant places all throughout the [game] [world], via clickable stone [portals] placed throughout the [zone]. Thus, it is spoken of a lot while playing.
