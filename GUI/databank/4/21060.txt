FTTP
Fiber to the Premises. Industry slang for running fiber optic cable to a neighborhood, campus, or business, but not all the way through the building or all the way to your home. ; A stopgap measure deployed while telecom and internet companies spend billions to rewire their networks and give you higher speed. ; Any type of fiber will allow much higher internet speeds and allow for things like HDTV video chatting with friends, large online gaming, etc.
