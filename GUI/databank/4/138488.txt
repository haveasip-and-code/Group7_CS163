Prant
A shotty, useless piece of chalk that is not suitable for writing on the blackboard because it is too tiny to grasp. ; Prants can also be used as a fun pre-class game called pranting. Object of the game is to make use out of prants, and throw them onto the chalk ledge. Five point for not using the blackboard as a backboard, and three for using it. Each person gets five throws in a round. Highest score at the end of five rounds wins.
