Cazar
The name of a species of alien found in the [Ratchet and Clank] series, occasionally refered to as [Magical Cat Thing]s. They stand between five and seven feet (1.5 - 2 meters) tall, have brown fur, and hail from the planet Marcadia. Notable characters include Captain Sasha and her father the Galactic President. ; The name was only coined after much pressure from fans for Insomniac Games to create one.
