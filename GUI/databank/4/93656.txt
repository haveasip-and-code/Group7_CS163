Biomerge
1. The Process through which a digimon Synthesizes Proteins to take on a physical form in the Human World. In the original Version of the Digimon Anime this was known as being 'Realized.' ; 2. The Process through which a human and a Digimon become one Digimon (a Mega Level). In the original Version of the Digimon Anime this was known as 'Matrix Evolution.'
