Stackin' Loot
1. To be making money, generally a lot of it. ; 2. To be on the clock at work. ; 3. Working in general ; words are commonly placed in between stackin' and loot. the words are reversable as well. for example. ; stackin' mad loot ; stackin' crazy loot ; stackin' some loot ; stackin' that loot ; loot stackin'
