Grats Mobile
1. A figurative vehicle one 'rides on' after doing something '1337'. This word is often preceded by the phrase 'take a ride on (the)'. 'Grats Mobile' is also used frequently in a sarcastic manner to poke fun at someone who claims to have accomplished something great, but in actually has accomplished nothing noteworthy. ; 2. A vehicle that is so dilapidated and/or fugly that it is funny.
