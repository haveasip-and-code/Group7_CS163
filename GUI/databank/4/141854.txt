Glen Rock
A town in Bergen County, New Jersey. The average household income for residents of Glen Rock is $104,192 (year 2000). The average home value was $316,900 in the year 2000. It is a predominantly white town. ; Glen Rock is named after a large glacial stone that was deposited there during the Ice Age. ; $421,092 is total 'richness factor' (home values plus income).
