Knackeragua
- Any working class area of an Irish city (e.g. Mayfield in Cork, Ballybeg in Waterford, west Tallaght or Ballymun in Dublin). Can also be used to refer to the entire city of Limerick. ; - Derives from [knacker] + 'Nicaragua' and originates from the early '80s when that country was much in the news for being something of a troublespot. ; - Such an area will be populated by [skangers], [norries], [skobes], [chavs] and of course the ludakrisly laughable [wiggaz]. ; - The polar opposite of [D4]
