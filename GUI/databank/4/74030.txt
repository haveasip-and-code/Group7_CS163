Phayder
One who 'Phaydes' ; -or- ; A 1337 spelling of Fader ; Phayder itself comes from the game Unreal Tournament. Released in 2000, the game earned Game Of The Year status, and was very popular for a time. The Phayder portion of the game was from a team of terrorists called the Necris. their male operatives were Phayders.
