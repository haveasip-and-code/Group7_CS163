Trillville
A rap group under the BME Click from Atlanta, GA. Signed by Lil' Jon in the late 90's, the three members of Trillville (Don P, Dirty Mouth, and Lil' Atlanta aka Lil' LA) put out their first major album in 2004. The group claims that the name 'Trillville' represents anybody that keeps it true and real, wherever they are from.
