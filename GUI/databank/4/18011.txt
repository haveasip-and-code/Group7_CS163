diva
Also known as a [prima donna] ('first lady'), and especially in an [opera]. Refers to the leading lady of the production. The [male] counterpart is the divo. Plural, including male and female singers, is 'divi' (DEE-vee), or, if just female singers, 'dive' (DEE-vay). ; Also refers to a [brat]ty, demanding, self-centered person, regardless of whether they're a performer or not.
