Baby Boy
A term used to describe a male that has no prospects in life whatsoever. A man who drifts through life aimlessly without any goals in mind. See: [Scrub]. ; Popularized by John Singleton's movie of the same name, which featured [Snoop Dogg] and Tyrese. ; Some characteristics (but not limited to) are: ; 1) Still lives with his mama, ; 2) Has no job/money, ; 3) Has a kid (or children) from more than one girl.
