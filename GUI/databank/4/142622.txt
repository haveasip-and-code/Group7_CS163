double-ugly
Refers to someone so very, very ugly that he or she can only be discribed as double-ugly. Similar to [butt-ugly] but twice as repulsive; hence the plural. ; One of the first instances of 'double-ugly' in print was in a classic 'Tom and Jerry' comic book from the 1950s. ; Alternative spellings include: [double ugly], [doubly-ugly]. Using a dash is proper when spelling compound adverbs and adjectives.
