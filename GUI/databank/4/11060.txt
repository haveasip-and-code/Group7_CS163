cinderella complex
When a woman (often submissive and needy) spends all her time searching for her 'prince charming' to come rescue her; brainwashed by rosy romantic images and ideals. ; Women with this personality trait often lose good guys because they feel the guy they are presently with doesn't fit their ideal of the perfect man. ; She dreams of living a fairy tale lifestyle where she meets this prince and they fall madly in love and live happily ever after in a magical fantasy where all of her needs and desires are taken care of.
