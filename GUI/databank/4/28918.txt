Ankou
An celtic legend over a fairy that was despised by his peers and ended up becoming a gatherer of dead. ; The base stone where the [grimreaper] father time and other legends were inspired. ; The legends said to not mention of his name, for he might be listening. ; Traveled in an old cart accompanied by two skeletons that took the [souls] of the dead. ; In some countries there was the habit of sacrificing someone for every new cemetary build, so that the person would become an 'Ankou' of the place. ; An Ankou is a guardian of dead.
