the bitch
1. The person in a group who gets assigned undesirable tasks, gets no respect, etc. ; 2. Slang for the effeminate one in a homosexual relationship. ; 3. A variant on seven-card stud [poker] in which the player who is dealt the queen of spades (the Bitch) face-down (if there is one) splits the pot with the winner. If the Bitch is dealt face up, the entire hand is scrapped and redealt from the beginning, keeping the pot as it was when the Bitch appeared.
