ahotha hatsolaat
'Your limping sister' - phrase similar by meaning to 'you mother f..' or 'you son of a b...' but considered as least severe. ; Another version of it is 'In'al ahotha hatsolaat' - where 'in'al' is result of arabic language influence and add the whole phrase more power and significance. ; Sometimes used as an answer in case a person don't want to reveal a real one and prefer to stop the questions.
