Gamer Sense
N.- Gaming- The ability of longtime [gamers] to judge when something is too easy or too pat, especially once they've put several hours under their belt on a certain game. While not necessarily psychic in origin, it can seem that way sometimes, especially when said gamer save seconds before an instant-death trap. See also [precognition]. ; From [Spider Sense], the near-precognition that [Spider-Man] displays on a regular basis to evade attacks.
