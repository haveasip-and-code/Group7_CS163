nonline
After all of our regular websites that used to be good started going downhill (theonion.com, somethingawful.com, etc) my friends and I started going 'on strike' against the internet. Of course it's impossible to live life without the internet completely these days, so we began to say we were nonline, meaning that we were able to shop and research on the internet, and yet not regularly visiting once amusing websites that many people frequent
