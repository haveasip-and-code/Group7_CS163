moon-sugar
-=noun=- ; 1) A slang term for cocaine. ; 2) From the game [Morrowind]: a substance native to southern Elsweyr, harvested from the sugarcane groves of the Tenmar Forest. It is used as a seasoning in foods, a source of communion with the holy moons, a dangerously addictive drug, and an ingredient in another drug called [skooma]. Considered to be 'crystalized moonlight' by the [khajiit] and regularly used in their cooking. Moon-sugar is invariably sweet and makes even the most bitter foods succulently savory.
