Masse!
A german word used in various situations to express that something is different than usual. Direct translation of it would be [mass] and the usage isnt that different. It can also be used similar to [wicked]. ; Usually it is used without much sentence surrounding it, as it is an expression of surprise, awe, amazement, shock etc. ; Most of the times, the 'e' is stretched. ; If anything, german verbs like 'verteilt' (distributed) or 'gekriegt' (got) are added. ; It can also be used if there's lots of sth around. ; In rare cases, it has been used as replacement for a 'Hello' or 'Hi'.
