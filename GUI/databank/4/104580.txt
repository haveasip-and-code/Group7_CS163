Krahny
(KRON-nee) ; adj, generally used of persons ; 1. having a cruel and sarcastic sense of humor ; 2. with very little patience or tolerance remaining ; 3. (of acts) educational but painful ; 4. mentally irregular. See also: insane, weird, zealous, maniacal, bonkers ; { Etymology. NOTE: word does NOT originate from the Krahns of Liberia. From M. Krahn (Low German), a Physics teacher who posseses these characteristics. }
