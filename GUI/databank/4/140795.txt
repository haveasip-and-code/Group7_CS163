evaculate
verb: to expel semen that has been ejaculated into one's bodily orifice by another person, e.g. by defecating after anal sex, or by douching after vaginal sex. ; etymology: hybridization of 'evacuate' (as in, 'evacuate one's bowels,' i.e. to [shit]; 'evacuate one's bladder,' i.e. to [piss]) and 'ejaculate,' i.e. to [cum], [jizz], [nut], etc.
