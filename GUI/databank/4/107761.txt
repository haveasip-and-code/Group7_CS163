courve
cour ve (CORE vah) noun, from Yiddish-1. any [not Jewish] woman that is a whore, slut, easy, etc ; 2. any of the above who wears too much make-up and perfume and dresses like a 'street walker' ; 3. any of the above that a [Jewish Mother] fears her son might, to the shame of the family, marry; often because she is pregnant
