Carbon Proton Synthesiser
More commonly referred to as a CPS, this ingenious contraption imprints a smiley face on the protons of a carbon atom using a high-powered laser. (see Hydro-lithium-demerol-amal-nitrate uranium scrupulator) ; Once the protons have been imprinted, one droplet of the atoms produces enough energy to power New York for 3 days.
