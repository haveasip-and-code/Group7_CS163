Badi'haki
Haki is a breed of werewolf from northern Africa. They are lightly skinned, skinny and hairy in their human forms, but grow bulky and fast in their wolf forms. They are to be considered extremely dangerous. There is only a couple left in the world, and three in the United States. ; During nights of no moon, the Badi'haki are completely defenseless and loose much of their strength as a human.
