ludes
1 - Slang term for the drug [methaqualude] or simple [quaaludes], a barbituate like Central Nervous depressant drug which causes clumsiness and fatigue, often used to treat insomnia and anxiety. ; 2 - 'TO BE ON LUDES': Idiotical, Dull Minded, Dim-Witted, Stupid, Lacking intelligence, Feeble Minded.
