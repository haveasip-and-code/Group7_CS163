Kirito
n. Pierrot vocalist. A person that chooses to do things regardless to what others tell him to do; does things to shock. Has a fetish for [murder] and [turtles]. Even though he may come off-beat, he produces versatile music and deep, multi-faceted lyrics. Tends to be a workaholic. Has a fascination with the word [kichigai]. An [egotist] who strives to do things his way. ; Has a strong sense of identity. A dreamer that has a sense of the harsh reality, but has found a way to live his dream. Known to be associated with [Kohta], [Aiji], [Jun] and [Takeo]. Said to be of the house of [murata].
