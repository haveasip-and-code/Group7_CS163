kwanh
1. The seat and source of female power; 2. the female vagina; 3. the antithesis and overcoming of machismo; 4. Anything vaginal in shape or context; Often used in the phrase 'Power of the Kwanh' as an affirmation and a rally cry. ; An alternative to the death-dealing and oppressive patriarchal references for the female anatomy that pepper both proper and slang English. It can only be spoken by women and is unable to be used derisively. Formulated by Steinwert and Waugh-Stewart during their years at Williams College.
