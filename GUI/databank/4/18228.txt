Monkey Lunchbox
The act of bidding and winning an item off Ebay, then realizing that you spent too much money and didn't want it anymore. ; The result is that you end up not paying for the item and then lie to the seller as to why you're not buying it. ; This is all done in an effort to avoid receiving negative feedback. ; * This term came about after winning a $50 bid for an old lunchbox that featured a chimpanzee on it. A lie was created and told to the seller- No negative feedback was given.
