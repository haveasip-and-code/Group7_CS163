Angelou
Character in the story 'Golden Waters'. Angelou is a spirit with immense power that many people long to possess. ; Angelou befriends the younger brother of the main character, Elleen. For a long time all characters believe that Angelou is nothing more than the young boy's imaginary friend until they learn the truth. Elleen then races against time to save her younger brother before Angelou takes him away to the spirit realm.
