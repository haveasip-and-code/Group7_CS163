layla
A song done by Derek & Dominos in their album Layla & Other Assorted Love Songs, in the year 1970 which had Eric Clapton as a member in its band. It also features Duane Allman. The song was written by Eric Clapton for the women he loved named Pattie Boyd who was his best friend's wife (George Harrison). The song had mixed reviews and had little airplay due its 7 minute lenght but it has been revived in the last few years and now a staple of classic music radio stations and on the SBC Yahoo DSL commercials.
