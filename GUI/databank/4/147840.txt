Ppsh
Pistolet-Pulemet Shpagina (Shpagin's Pistol-Machinegun)was designed by Georgii Shpagin in '41 to match the Finns' and Germans' submachine guns. Simple and reliable, with a compensator to stem muzzle climb, the Ppsh had a staggering rate of fire as well as high accuracy and destructive power. ; t.o. Operation: Blowback ; RPM: 900 ; Fire: Semi-auto or full auto ; Clip: 35r. box or 71 r. drum ; Muzle Velocity: 1600fps ; Weight: 12lbs w/ drum
