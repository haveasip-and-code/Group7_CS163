Plebing it
1.Expression used by one who must take a job(s) in customer service/waiting tables/collections/dog walking/lab experiments/telemarketing/cleaning services in order to pay one's bills. ; 2. Expression used by those who feel overqualified and outraged by their self-perceived low status, low income job. Especially since they have college degrees, usually from liberal arts schools. ; Related: 'To plebe it.' ; 'plebe moment' ; 'plebe-afied' ; 'plebe opportunity'
