Short for 'Constantinople', the former name of the city Istanbul in Turkey, which is no longer in use. ***(Background: In 1453, Ottoman Sultan Mehmed II conquered the city and made it the capital of the Ottoman Empire. Now under new rule, the metropolis was dubbed 'Istanbul' from the Greek phrase 'eis ten polin,' which meant 'in the city.'
