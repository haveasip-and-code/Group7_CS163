mizuhoswim
The act of [ejaculating] all over a model kit or action figure, usually of a girl from a Japanese [manga], [anime] or [videogame]. The term originates from the picture boards of [4chan], where someone once posted a [zip] file titled mizuhoswim.zip which contained several pictures and a video of an unknown person ejaculating over a [PVC] figure of [Mizuho] from the anime series [Onegai Teacher].
