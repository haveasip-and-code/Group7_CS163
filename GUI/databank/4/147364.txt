St. Louisism
A word or phrase unique to the big small town of St. Louis, Missouri. Examples: ; Hoosier: white trash ; toasted ravioli: small raviolis deep fried till crispy ; gooey butter cake: a breakfast cake, about 10 x 10 inches, ; filled with a buttery custard and covered in powdered ; sugar. ; pork steak: a slice of pork butt, about half inch thick, ; bone in, slow cooked on a barbeque pit and smothered in ; Maull's barbeque sauce ; 'Where'd ya go to high school?': the first question asked ; by one native to another person they are meeting for the ; first time who also is a lifelong St. Louisan.
