Pootsie
n. ; 1. One, male or female, whom is in a state of being grumpy, uncool, or just plain mean. ; adj. ; 1. A word used to describe anothers state of being grumpy, uncool, or just plain mean. ; Usage: The word 'pootsie' is better if used without an article (a, an, or the) if being used in the adjective form; It is just simply 'pootsie.' It is better to never, if possible, use an article before the word to add emphasis and funniness.
