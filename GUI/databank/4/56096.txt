Opie
A nickname that is typically used by blacks to refer to any white male. ; This modern usage of 'Opie' is most likely derived from the character Opie Taylor (played by Ron Howard) on 'The Andy Griffith Show' who was the hick son of a hick sherriff in a hick town called Mayberry. ; Others characters on this show have also spawned modern nicknames (See Gomer Pyle, Goober Pyle, Aunt Bea, Barney Fife).
