Ski Team
Ski Team spoken really fast is said to sound like 'Skeet Skeet', however, most members of ski teams across America (and in some parts of Europe) deny this. A few have come forward and admitted a double meaning, but nobody has confirmed the actual meaning of the words. ; Often used in conjuction with a hand signal in which, palm facing in, the middle and ring finger are held in by the thumb and index and pinky are held upright.
