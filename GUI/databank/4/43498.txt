scorgy
A combination of the words 'score' and 'orgy'. ; Place of Origin: Bel Air (MD) ; P.O. Speech:1.Exclamitory, ; 2. Adjective ; The word Scorgy implies a plentiful bounty of goodfortune, taking the excitment of the expression ' HE SHOOTS...HE SCORES!' and coupling it with the raunchy, sometimes catch 22 nature of 'orgy'. Originating in the halls of Fallston High school, scorgy is used to describe a multitude of things ranging from a group of skanks, to a performance on a 'Personal Finance' exam.
