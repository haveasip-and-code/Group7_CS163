hankty
Hankty: (adj) to be disrespectful, selfish, [ignunt], moronic, or [act a fool]. ; Note: 'hankty bitch' is a common phrase using the word ; Noun form: Hanktiness ; Though the word 'hankty' may have been in obscure usage before, it was given its current definition by a New Haven theater student in 2003. The word has swept the East Coast.
