Rhythmismer
Slang 'Ismist,' also see [Rhythmismist] ; 1. Subspecies of the NYC club life including individuals who self-identify their social, musical and venue orientation though membership to Rhythmism and association with other Ismists, as well as those whose regular participation on Rhythmism or attendance at Ismist events causes others to identify them as primarily connected to the nightlife scene through this particular web portal. ; 2. Anyone who begins an introduction with 'My name is X on the board' or inquires as to other's 'screennames' when out at a drinking or dancing establishment.
