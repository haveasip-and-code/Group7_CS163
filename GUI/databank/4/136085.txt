anakin christ
The name that several have given to Anakin Skywalker in the Star Wars series, due to the parallels of his character and Jesus Christ. ; 1. Both were 'immaculately' concevied. Jesus by the Holy Spirit, and Anakin by the Midichlorians. ; 2. Both were prophesized to be the savior of their people. Jesus for mankind's sins, and Anakin to destroy the sith and bring balance to the force.
