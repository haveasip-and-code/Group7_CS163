perverted
Something or someone that has deviated from what is considered right, correct, natural, moral, ethical, or its intended function. Examples of perversion would be 'perverted justice' or 'sexually perverted acts'. ; A perverted person is also known as a [deviant] or a [sicko]. Such a person will have deviated from a healthy sexuality into acts that may include one or more of the following: [homosexuality], [voyeurism], [sadism], [flashing], [masochism], [prostitution], [incest], [paedophilia], [bestiality], [rape], [swinging], or some other indecent act.
