calpis
1. Calcium enriched soft drink produced in Japan. Encompasses a fairly large product line wich includes carbonated and fruit flavored versions of the drink along with candy and frozen desserts. The most common version of the drink is 'Calpis Water,' which appears as a milky white liquid similar to milk, yet has a starchy sweet taste and a slightly acid aftertaste. ; 2. Japanese slang term for ejaculate.
