f-athetic
Continuing the highly commendable tradition of other 'f truncations', such as [f-ilarious] and [f-unlucky], 'f-athetic' is generally used as an adjective, or as an adverb 'f-athetically'. It can be used by itself as a cutting insult or as an expression of extreme annoyance. ; It is of course an amalgamation of the words 'fucking' and 'pathetic', used when time and utter condemnation is of the essence. ; 'F-athetic' ought to be reserved for instances where the utmost scorn must be poured on whoever or whatever has been singled out for this most harsh of criticisms.
