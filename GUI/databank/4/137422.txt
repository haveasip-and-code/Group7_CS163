RemusOmega
Also known as ReMuSoMeGa - Originated from Greek and latin text. Remus was one of the founders of Rome, along with his brother; Romulus. Remus was one of the greatest leaders of all time. Omega Is the last letter of the Greek alphabet. Also known as 'The Alpha and the Omega' Which means 'The Begining and the End (God)' ; The compound-noun translates into 'Last Ultimate Leader'
