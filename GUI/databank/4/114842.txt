freek dat
1. (general) a phrase used to identify something or someone that meets a personal standard of approval in which you would engage in it (bone it, hit it, do it, etc.) ; 2. (v) To rip a hot beat lyrically; to ride a beat/track; to put it down lyrically. The concept is that a beat or track is so hot, you got to get on it, same thought process as definition 1 but in terms of rapping over a hot beat. ; See [freak dat]
