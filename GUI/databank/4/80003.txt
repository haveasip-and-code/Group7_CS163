Stone Rose Roughnecks
One of the best clans on Xbox Live created by Stalker and RazorBladeKiss. Known as 'The True UK / US Alliance' the Stone Rose Roughnecks excelled at Rainbow Six and soon made their way to the 2004 Team Compete playoffs lead by captains SRR ox Farse xo and SRR AngeliuS. Before they could compete to much with it though the clan broke up and are now three different clans. ; R.I.P. SRR
