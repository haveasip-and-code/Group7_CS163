hinsukaiye
1. A type of draconic [wyvern] that dwells in a strange world called [Narhyn], only spoken of in stories by 'Relic', a female personage who owns the [Deviantart] account 'Akumu-Sama'. ; 2. A nickname taken up by Relic, the creator of the hinsukaiye creatures (see definition one) that she says means 'Stroke of Rising Sun' in Narhynian.
