Enumclaw
American Native name Meaning Home of the evil spirit( Probably because the volcano Mt Rainier erupted off and on while the native americans lived in the area). Enumclaw is located 40 miles S.E. of Seattle Washington in the foot hills of the Cascade mountan range. Famous people that have visited Enumclaw. Elvis filmed a movie for the 1962 worlds fair that was in Seattle. Part of the movie was filmed in Enumclaw country side.
