irasshaimase
'Irasshaimase!' is an honoriffic expression welcoming someone somewhere (usually into a place of business). It shows respect toward the performer of its action (the customer) by honoring them as opposed to humbling the speaker. ; The plain form of the word itself is irassharu, and is an exalted form of several verbs including kuru (to come), iku (to go), and iru(for animate objects to exist).
