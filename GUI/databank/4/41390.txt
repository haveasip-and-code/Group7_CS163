Alichino
1. (noun) A shoujo manga created by Kouyu Shurei. ; 2. (noun) Creatures that 'can take on many different forms... and possess a beauty beyond compare.' They grant wishes to humans in exchange for the wisher's soul, which they eat. Consuming a soul with the power of [Kusabi] gives them great power. However, people with the power of the [Kusabi] can also destroy these creatures by merely touching their bodies when they lack a pulse. Alichino are beautiful, yet they are cunning liars. They are also known as 'The Inviters'.
