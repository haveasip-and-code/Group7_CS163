Crossfade Records
A record label started in 1992 by T-Snake and DJ Prime, originally working in Hip Hop, House, Bass and Techno, but now a [Techno], [Electro], [Trance], [Ambient] and other [EDM] label featuring artists: T-Snake, Todd Sargent, Dragon and more, emphasizing the 'free the music' movement, encouraging free digital distribution of ALL music.
