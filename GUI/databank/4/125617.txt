Rapture
1. The act of seizing and carrying off as prey or plunder ; 2. The act of carrying, or fact of being carried, onwards; force of movement ; 3. a. The act of carrying off a woman. ; b. [Rape] ; 4.] b. the Rapture (of the Church, of the Saints): the taking up of believers to heaven at the Second Coming of Christ, according to some Millenarian teaching
