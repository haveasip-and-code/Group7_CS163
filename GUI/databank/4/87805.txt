untss
1. The phonetic spelling of the typical rap or techno beat where the 'un' is the bass (made deep in the throat) and the 'tss' is the cymbal (made with the tongue on the roof of the mouth.) ; 2. A declaration of satisfaction, achievement, or generic contentness. ; 3. Variation, unts, can be said 'u - n - t - s' to express definition 2.
