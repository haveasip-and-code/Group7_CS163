vapors
n. (va-perz) ; 1. Any of various diseases, including chancroid, chlamydia, gonorrhea, and syphilis, that are usually contracted through sexual intercourse or other intimate sexual contact. see [STD]. ; 2. A stinky [bagina]. A girl with the vapors typically is sexually promiscuous, consequently her vagina does not have time to air out and freshen up.
