kuronue
A character of [Yu Yu Hakusho], an [anime], and an old thieving partner of [Youko Kurama]'s. Kuronue appeared in the movie (Poltergeist Report), not the anime, and only then through trickery by an enemy and in Kurama's memories. ; He was killed in a bamboo trap while they were being chased. ; He always carried a crimson-stoned pendant, which was a family heirloom. ; He had bat wings.
