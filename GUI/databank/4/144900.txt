Master and the Wolf
1. n. adj. A ship of Professor Severus Snape and Remus Lupin, commonly used by Harry Potter fanfiction authors. It earns its name due to the fact that Snape is a potions master and Lupin is a werewolf. ; 2. n. The website that wields its name, which is a compilation of Snape/Lupin slash fanfiction pieces.
