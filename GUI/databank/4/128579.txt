Snacker
Noun. A varient of [Redneck]: Think Matthew McConaughey's character in 'Dazed and Confused.' The guy the underage kids ask to buy them cigarettes and beer, but instead of bringing back all the change, also gets a liter of Mountain Dew and a bag of Cool Ranch Doritos. Almost always seen wearing a mullet and driving an '81 Camero. Will have Zubaz pants in more remote areas.
