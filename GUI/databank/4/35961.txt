kodaked
verb, transitive, past-tense. ; Infinitive form: to kodak. ; Present/future tense form: kodakking. ; Definition: To involuntarily suffer near-total to total data loss to your hard drives. Especially if due to poorly programmed proprietary software that comes with new hardware. ; Background: Some versions of Kodak Easyshare software had a tendency to mung FAT32 partitions.
