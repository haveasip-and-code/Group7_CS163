use us
Utterance by the soul cube in Doom 3 once you've killed enough enemies. The soul cube is called 'use us' in order for the speaker to not sound like a D&D fanatic. ; When deploying the 'use us' you must say 'GO USE US' or else your 1st, 3rd, and 5th children will be stillborn. And you'll have to eat them. Not cooked.
