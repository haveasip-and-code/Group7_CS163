osdorp posse
A Dutch Hip Hop Group who made hip hop in dutch language famous. (also called Nederhop) They were the first and still the best. They are a 5-man crew; Def P (rapper) Seda (rapper, beat-creator), King (back-up, merchandise) Ijsblok (back-up, regelneef) and DJ Daan (DJ) from a neighbourhood in Amsterdam called Osdorp. Def P has intellectual lyrics and is an example of true (Dutch) hip-hop. www.osdorpposse.nl
