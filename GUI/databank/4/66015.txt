Ocho
1. Ocho originated from the Spanish word for eight. However in some Spanish speaking [ghetto]s, men who carried guns were referred to as Ochos instead of [nine]s. The term caught on quick and spread like wildfire. ; 2. A term for something that is cool, the roots are quite unknown, but it began in D.C. and spread West of there. The use of the word can differ, it can be a term of endearment to a term of hate.
