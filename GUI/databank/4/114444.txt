wangst
The feeling of overwhelming vicious hate towards your job. Most often occuring during 5 points: Mondays, when you wake up each weekday morning, the second you get to the office, right after lunch, and when you are asked to stay late. Also, most often occuring when you are the [WanganatoR]. Temporary relief for Wangst includes [skiving], and plotting to upset the company's network so that no work can be done. Permanant relief includes finding a new job which does not totally [suck]. Or maybe quitting my job to write: 'From teenage angst, to post-college wangst'. ; Origins of word come from ancient celtic tradition, or [Micah].
