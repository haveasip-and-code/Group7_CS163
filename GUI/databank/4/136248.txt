STIG
An acronym meaning 'Short Tempermental and Ignorant Guy.' ; Used to describe someone who is greatly involved in politics and angry at the corruption of his Government, and the stupidity of his President. This person wishes to take control of his country for the good of all who chant with him in his quest for world domination. ; STIG was first used and gained popularity within the Computer Programming classroom of Vintage High School in Napa, California. ; The acronym itself is used to describe the teacher of the class, Mr. Kamada, whom was nicknamed STIG or sometimes, STIGMADA - a play off of his last name, 'Kamada.'
