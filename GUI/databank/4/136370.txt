Choyse
Choyse origionated off the streets in a little town known as Tracy, California. It was used in a Taqueria in place of 'choice of meats.' Instead they put 'Choyse of meets.' ; Choyse now refers to being in the perfect state of mind, i.e. being [high],[drunk],[thizz]ing, or any time you feel good. ; Choyse also can refer to a really hot [breezy].
