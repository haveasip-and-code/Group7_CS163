psychoactive
An adjective; mainly used to describe drugs that alter ones mental process or behavior. ; Psychoactive drugs are usually catergorized into the groups of stimulants, depressants, hallucinogens, inhalants, anabolic steroids, narcotics, and some consider cannabis to be its own catergory. Cannabis can induce symptoms of every type of drug and its effects vary greatly in different people and during each ingestion.
