mungchow
An inanimate object that is ruined or sick beyond repair (i.e computer, electronics, toaster, etc.). ; Also can be used as an alternate to the term 'Mung' which is related to a vaginal secretion. The term 'Mungchow' suggests the ingestion of such secretions, implying anything that is 'mungchow', is messed up to a point to where it is the eater of bad secretions, and incapable of enjoying the flavor of red wine, popsicles, etc.
