Mollocka
-A disproportionately large person; one who far exceeds the physical standards of being considered human. ; -The word 'mollocka' originates from the names of two large creatures. The 'lock' is taken from the Lochness monster and the pronunciation of the word 'mollocka' is parallel to the pronunciaiton of Chewbacca, the large and shaggy 'Star Wars' character. The 'm-o' is taken from the first two letters of the word 'monster.'
