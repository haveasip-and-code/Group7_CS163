Shotaro Complex
A sexual complex similar to that of the [Lolita Complex]. ; The Shotaro Complex describes a sexual attraction to younger boys, usually by adult males. However, females may also have this complex. ; It is named for the boy hero of Tetsujin 28-Go (also known as Gigantor), which is also a popular generic name for young male characters in anime. ; The Shotaro Complex is often shortened to [shotacon].
