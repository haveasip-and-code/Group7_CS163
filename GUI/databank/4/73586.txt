quizmonkey
(Semnopithicus quizus) ; The native species of primate belonging to the region of Pu Chute in Western Chiwibble. These are often found feltching each other for grooming purposes and as a method of strengthining intra-troop bonds. They can be identified by markings resembling that of the [pengis]. Favourite foods include [chimp whale meat], banana [spling]s and [chode]cumbers. They occasionally migrate to the man fat tree in order to sample the delicacy of tail sap.
