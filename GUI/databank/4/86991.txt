boojied
deliberately antagonised, usually done in a playful manner to test the resolve of the boojied. It can be done by repeatedly shouting at speed Booji Tiz at the person to be boojied. It is a test of will as to who gives in. It is not intended to bully but as a sign of respect. Boojie is then applied to authority figures that get on your case for no good reason but have your best interests at heart. Motivation.
