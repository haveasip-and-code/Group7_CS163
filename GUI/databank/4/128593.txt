FSOL
Abbreviation of the Future Sound of London, a British electronic/ambient/psychedellic rock/dance act that pioneered ambient music in the early 90's, and created one of the most popular electronic tracks of all time, 'Papua New Guinea'. Have released numerous albums and mini-albums, as well as LPs and remix CDs. Also known an 'Amorphous Androgynous' and other pseudonyms.
