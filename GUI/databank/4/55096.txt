Green Biker Dude
The Green Biker Dude is the heroic green soldier from the intro of [Mega Man X2]. He is a role model to all generic soldiers, in many ways. He followed his dreams, he did his best, no matter what the circumstances, and he died fighting for what he believed in. ; The Green Biker Dude died a hero during the raid on the Reploid factory. He attempted to pop a wheelie, and he exploded. ; To this day, the memory of the Green Biker Dude lives on in the hearts of millions.
