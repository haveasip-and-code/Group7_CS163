A-Town Stomp
A dance consisting kicking right leg out first, then stomp. Then do the left leg, stomp. Kick the right leg twice, then stomp. Kick left, then stomp. Take right arm and swing into of face downward then 'pump'. Repeat with left arm. Right. Left. Do the Lil Jon high clap 4xs. Then rock back and forth 4xs. Take hand and perform a 'slice' action by neck. ; Usually performed by Lil Jon and Usher..and the rest of the ATL membaz.
