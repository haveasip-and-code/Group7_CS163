Aztec
Verb meaning: ; 'to say that you are moving on after an argument, but to never actually do so.' ; Named after a certain member of a forum who often had arguments, and spent several weeks seeking the [forgiveness] of other forum members. ; Various Meanings: ; To declare that one is moving on after an argument, without committing themself. ; To suggest moving on after an argument, but never actually doing so. ; To announce moving on even when there has not been an argument. ; The period of idle between a (sometimes non-existent, or very old) argument, and the period of forgiving.
