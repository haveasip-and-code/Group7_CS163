Don Hertzfeldt
Don Hertzfeldt is an amazing animator. His films usually contain cynical, random, off beat jokes. He is well known for his animation, 'Rejected'. He also teamed up with Mike Judge and created 'The Animation Show'. Along with those two, he's created a few others such as 'Lilly and Jim', 'Genre', and 'Billys Balloon'. Don is one awesome animator.
