sheesha
n. a glass-bottomed, long-necked bong-like gadget for smoking tobaccoo and/or hashish. ; the tobaccoo comes in friut molasses(apple and strawberry are the most used..but you can also get muskmelon ..or..gulp! green marrow,too..),and the aroma is good to try and be hooked on it for-fuckin'-ever! ; Down here in Amman, coffee houses offer a wide variety of tastes and contraptions to smokers but if you came to this city and wanna try a hubbly-bubbly ( the more-famous term for sheesha), try Balat Al-Rasheed tourism cafe..and welcome all o'yous [tourons].
