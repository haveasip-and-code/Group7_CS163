Kung Lek
Kung Lek is a romanization of a chinese definition meaning: 'Power Generation' and also meaning 'Strong Fundamentals'. ; It can also be a name of a person and is a name of a Kungfu set. Also known as a 'form' or 'taolu' and in some circles the Karate terminology is used and it is called a 'kata' ; the set is called 'Kung Lek Kuen' or gongliquan' ; Alternate spellings include 'Gong Lik' and 'gongli' as well as 'Kung Lik' ,'Kung li', 'Gungli' and 'Gung Lik'
