A measure of late/early striking when ringing. ; origin: a character 'J Peasemold Gruntfuttock' appearing in a British humour show 'Round the Horne' with: ; THE GRUNTFUTTOCK SAGA ; Broadcast: 9th April 1967 ; which included: ; Gruntfuttock of Arabia Captured by the Turks ; Gruntfuttock 'tis of Thee
