In the Barn
When playing the card game [euchre] and one team has obtained nine tricks or points, one teammate does the following: ; 1. announce to opponents and partner 'Dude! we're in the barn!' ; 2. balance the scorecards on ears (one on each) ; 3. link fingers together and outstretch hands with thumbs facing down, thus allowing their teammate across to 'milk' his/her thumbs ; 4. both teammates moo similtaneously
