Salty McPete
Named after creator, a long-time sailor in the British navy with three peg legs, if you get my meaning (hint hint). A two or more person contest that consists of becoming extremely intoxicated and then running through a crowd and, in Pete's own words, 'grabbin' as many boobys as ye can touch.' ; Extra points are awarded for tongue to boobage contact. Last person to pop a hard one wins.
