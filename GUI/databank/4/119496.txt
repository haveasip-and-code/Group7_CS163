Magic Kingdom
One of four of the theme parks in [Walt Disney World] Resort, in [Orlando], [Florida]. Sadly one one of [Walt Disney]'s park he could never see after his death in 1966. The Magic Kingdom opened five years later in 1971 after Walt's passing. Other parks in Walt Disney World like Magic Kingdom are [Epcot], [MGM Studios], and [Animal Kingdom]. The Magic Kingdom was also home to Cinderella's Castle the pride and joy of the park.
