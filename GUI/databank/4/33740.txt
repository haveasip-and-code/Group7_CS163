fan girling
Any girl extremely obsessed with any celebrity, t.v. show, movie, book, or other entertainment/media who is participating in fan actitivities such as message boards, [fanfiction], or conventions. ; Has been used in the past as derogatory but has become less and less in certain fandoms. Fan girling frequently happens with large amounts of [squee]. ; The noun would be [fangirl] or occasionally [fengirl], though the later is usually used as plural.
