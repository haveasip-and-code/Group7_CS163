WAIF
Noun - A poor, skinny, homeless [child] typically in Dickensian London. Normally has unnecessary filth about his [face] and/or clothing. The [butt] of many cruel jokes involving his financial status. ; Verb - the act of 'waifing' ; Adjective - A cruel insult, normally used for [people] smaller or less fortunate than yourself. ; Adverb - 'in the manor of a waif'
