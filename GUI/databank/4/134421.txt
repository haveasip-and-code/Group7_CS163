techtard
A contraction of 'Technological Retard' ; Technological + Retard = Techtard ; 1. Someone who is so 'technologically challenged' that they shouldn't be allowed within a 10 mile radius of anything electronic. ; 2. Someone who often misinterprets/gets confused with IT Support's suggestions/advice. Most Helpdesk operators would rather watch paint dry than talk to these users. ; See [techtarded]
