lord of olympia
1. Zeus, God of gods, he ruled over Olympia in Greek mythology. ; 2. An extremely rigged hero in Defense of the Ancients in WarCraft3 for Battle.net. He has the ability to fire lightning bolts from the sky that can deal up to 375 damage as well as a chain lightning that can deal 85 damage to 15 units. His final skill is the ability to deal nearly 600 damage to every hero in the game regardless of where they stand.
