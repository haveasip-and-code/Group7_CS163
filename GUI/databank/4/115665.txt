fornitura
Spanish twist on an English word, with several meanings and uses ; 1. a person of little to no self drive, who needs to be moved around and pushed around to get them to do something. ; 2. a friend who promised to do something and then renegued for no good reason, especially if this is his or her usual behavior ; can be shortened to 'forni' as in 'que forni eres' when talking to the person being described by the word
