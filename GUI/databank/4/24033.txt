parlor
1) An archaic term for a room within a home used for recreational indoor activities or the entertainment of guests; known as a 'living room' in modern language. ; 2) A room equipped and furnished for a special function within a business. (i.e. Ice-cream parlor, tanning parlor, gambling parlor, pool parlor) ; 3) Slang for a [brothel] or place of prostitution, or a hall or den used in secret to consume narcotics & illegal substances.
