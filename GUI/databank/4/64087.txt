Shar
Originating from Montgomery County, Maryland and a few individuals of AEHS Class of 2004, it's a combination of 'Shit' and 'Rawr' (Roar). ; [Shit] + [Rawr] = Shar ; Commonly used to emphasize [anger], [frustration], [annoyance], and just about anything really. ; Shar can also be used as a verb in different aspects.
