Johnny Kickchass
Johnny Kickchass is the original third member of the Insane Clown Posse, and older brother of ICP member Shaggy 2 Dope. He was with them since the days of Inner City Posse. He can be found on most Inner City Posse songs and Dog Beats, which appears on Forgotten Freshness Vol. 2. He quit half way through Carnival of Carnage so you can hear him referenced to in 'Juggla', and you can hear him rap in various song on the album.
