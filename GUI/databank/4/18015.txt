Sweet Home
A game created by [Capcom] that was not released in America. In the year 2000 a group of people known as [Gaijin] completely translated and localized the game. ; The game's elements are reflected in Resident Evil, even though the game was created years before. Some speculate that this is the first [survival horror] game by [Capcom], and spawned the rest later. ; This game is unavailable in [cartridge] form. Only [Rom]s exist.
