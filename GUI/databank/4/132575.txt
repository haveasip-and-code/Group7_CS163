10-2-2
Doesnt have to be the ugliest person in the club but it describes the woman (or man) who you ask home at 1.50am, hence '10 -2- 2 (ten minutes to 2 am), when all else has failed on the romance front. ; Usually not the sort of person you would go for, but sometimes you strike lucky. ; Often the remark 'Get your jacket, you've pulled' is made at some point.
