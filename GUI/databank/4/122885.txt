quackPOT
Quackpot: A person who has fixed ideas on medical matters, who decries alternative health methodologies, but does this from an irrational, biased and ignorant point of view. ; A quackpot will often claim medical and/or scientific knowledge or expertise, which, in reality, they do not have. ; Some quackpots have been known to pad out their CVs, claiming training, qualifications, jobs or roles that have no existence in reality, etc. ; A Quackpot will often claim that they influence key decision makers, when this is not, in reality, the case.
