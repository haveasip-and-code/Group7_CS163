hogshead
A large cask for liquids, usually containing liquor; a liquid ; measure containing 63 old wine-gallons (equal to 52 imperial gallons). ; Abbreviated hhd. ; This content was prescribed by a statute of 1423: 'The London hogshead of beer contained 54 gallons, that of ale 48 gallons; elsewhere the hogshead of ale or beer contained 51 gallons.'
