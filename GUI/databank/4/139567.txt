bretch
1. 'Stretch' as uttered from the mouth of someone, who like the apted-named character from 'Fat Albert,' suffers from mushmouth. ; 2. Exclaimed by one usually in the peak hours of his life, as a directive to misguided youth, who may claim to never, ever quit smoking. ; 3. Can be yelled in response to the original 'bretcher' as a threat or show of severe disrespect to his cultural duties.
