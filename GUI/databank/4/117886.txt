Pimp My Vibe
The act of an owner of a 'Pontiac Vibe' who decides to pimp out or modify his/her Vibe in the cheapest way possible. ; A real-life parody of the show 'Pimp My Ride', usually involving household odds and ends and other miscellaneous items to try to 'pimp out' their car. ; Making fun of every [Ricer] type of person who tries to put huge loud [fartcan] mufflers on their stupid little cars because they think it looks cool.
