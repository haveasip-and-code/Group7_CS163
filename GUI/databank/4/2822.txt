blue bill
A member of the Anti Ku-Klux-Klan group the Blue Bills. They consisted of around 150 members and would find out through spies in the Klan who was going to be attacked and set a trap for the Klan. Most battles between the two factions would go as this : Klan sends out 400 members on full force, they find 100 blue bills. Klan gets ass kicked due to bills being waayyy over armed. Bills win.
