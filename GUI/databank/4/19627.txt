innit
Abreviation of isn't it (Is it not) ; 'Innit' is put at the end of a question or statement meaning either: ; - Do you agree? ; or ; - Are you following me/Do you understand? ; 'Innit has classically been added at the end of a sentence that contains the verb 'to be', as 'ISn't it' obviously refers back to a conjugation of the this verb. (See example 1.) ; These days though, 'innit' can be used eventhough the verb in the main sentence is not a form of 'to be'. (See example 2)
