scamizdat
neologized by Grady Ward, referring to the continuing response to Church of Scientology litigation threats by publishing 'secret scriptures' to the Internet. c. 1995 in alt.religion.scientology. 'Scam' + 'samizdat' q.v. ; More generally, any publishing of texts to expose a scam, when those texts are the fiercely protected copyright of the alleged scammer.
