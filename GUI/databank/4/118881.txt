five points of scenesterdom
The five points of scenesterdom explain the universal truths of [scenester]s. Arranged in the shape of a star, the priority of each point is more the closer to the top it is. ; At the top of the star is music. ; In the middle is lifestyle and clothes. ; At the bottom is hair and hobbies. ; Although the exact details slightly differ between areas, in general if you have all five points covered you will get along with any scenester anywhere. ; This quasi-scientific method of measuring and understanding scenesters was originally created by Grace-Monday, a disgruntled half-scenester, in 2005.
