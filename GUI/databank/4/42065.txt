Tobi Vail
'Riot Grrrl'/ former drummer of the band Bikini Kill. She was also in many other bands including: Bikini Kill, Chopped Liver, Doris, The Feebles, Frenchie and the German Girls, ex-Gene Defcon, The Go Team, Mary Lou Lord (guest), My New Boyfriend, Phranc (guest), Severed Lethargy, Spray Painted Love. ; She is one of the contributors/founders of BAB (Bands Against Bush) and Ladyfest
