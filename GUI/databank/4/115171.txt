bhop
Bunny Hop ; (found in Half-Life games such as Team Fortress Classic and Counter-Strike) ; A skilled jump technique that enables the player who uses it to ; - move about 150% of their normal running speed ; - achieve greater jumping distances (about 150%) ; - be more unpredictable in his/her movement ; Bunny Hopping is totally legitimate, though some look down upon it and prevent it in their servers.
