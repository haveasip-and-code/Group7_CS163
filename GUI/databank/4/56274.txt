newbeat
n.- 1. post-punk intellectuals who believe in a pacifistic, artistic, and intelligent way of life. their anarchiac views are expressed through grassroot shockwaves surged through the public that reject them in digestable doses, as opposed to riots and physical protest. calm and collected people who hold the value of the ability of expression over that of mandate education. they seem to fluidly flow from clique to clique and befriend any social group. 2. the new generation of free-thinkers, beatniks.
