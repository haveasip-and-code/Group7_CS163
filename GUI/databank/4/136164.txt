dassok
The result of an extreme mutilation of the well used phrase 'That is okay'. The change occurs when the person types this pharse too much, generally after a thank you, and soon types it in short hand and even short short hand. The phrase has been changed as follows: ; 'That is okay' ; 'Thats ok' ; 'Dats ok' ; 'Dass ok' ; 'Dassok'
