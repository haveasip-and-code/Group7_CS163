sporkeh
AKA [sp0rkeh]. ; A computer virus that masquerades as a [hack] for the game Counter-Strike, often used by members of [myg0t]. Among other things, Sporkeh changes window text to a [l33t] derivative of 'sporkeh owns you,' switches screen relolution to 640x480, switches right-click and left-click on your mouse, slows the mouse cursor to a crawl, and prevents any EXE files from being run.
