Kupo
(1) ; 'Kupo' - The word that is uttered at the end of sentance by the creature [Moogle]. The meaning of this word is unknown. ; 'Unkupo' - This word's meaning is in a range of definitions, (Bad, Not Good, Horrible, etc.) ; 'Kupopo' - Is sometimes uttered at the beggining of a sentance, exact meaning is not certain but is sometimes said with questionable tone before asking a question or ending... But could also be uttered in normal sentances. ; (2) ; Kupo is a vocabulary word that is said by small winged creatures called, [Moogles]. The meaning of Kupo is not very clear at the moment but is used only by Moogles.
