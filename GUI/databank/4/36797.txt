KRB
Kevin/Rob/Blake possey: ; a violent and mischievous trio that wreacked sarcastic havoc on Thomas Stone Highschool in Waldorf Maryland. KRB is responsible for embarressment of innumerable victims and the begining of what would come to be known as Tip Wars. KRB invented the Kevin Goad song, and the Chriss Prairie song (with jazz interlude) and successfully completed one of the most dangerous and daring feats of them all: Operation Sound Off.
