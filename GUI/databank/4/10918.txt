erotica
Sexually arousing material (film, book, pictures or otherwise) that are also of a loving, romantic nature. It can be geared toward [straight] and [gay]/[lesbian] audiances. Also see [slash], [yuri], [yaoi], among others. ; As opposed to [pornography], which usually is just for the sexual aspect. Pornography has been critcized by [feminist]s as degrading to women.
