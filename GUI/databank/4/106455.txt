Douche
1)Product used to sanitize an unpleasant, dirty vagina. ; 2)Used to describe an individual who has shown themselves to be very brainless, inconsiderate, or tacky in one way or another, thus comparing them to the cleansing product for vaginas. ; 3)To make a commitment with an individual and then break it without informing the individual you made the commitment with. ; 4) A term of endearment.
