Armany
Lebanese-American restaurant owner who owned his own Pizza shop, Armany's Pizza. Often times wears real thin glasses and old t-shirts. Is famous for: his Armany's Pizza Rolls, chest hair, and his 'tea.' Favorite quotes: 'HEEYYY GUYS, WANNA TRY SOME GAHLIC BREAD?' 'HEY HOWS YOUR GIRLFRIEND?' ; Special Note: The Pizza shop was also the location of the first ever 'Duarte Lean' bite
