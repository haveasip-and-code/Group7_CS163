Pogo Punk
Pogo Punk is derived from the music genre of [Street Punk]. It primarily involves Japanese street punk bands now a days, but American Street Punk bands like The Casualties have had association with Pogo Punk. ; With a major Japanese label, Pogo 77, there are many bands put under this type of [Punk]. ; Also, Pogo is a dance used in Punk music, which means exactly what it looks like. To 'Pogo up and down'.
