Outright-Assholism
Outright-assholism is the practice of being an outright asshole. Not to be confused with it's antithetical art form known as [subtle-assholism]. ; The term was coined in an essay titled 'The Middle Class Renaissance: Otherwise Known As The Rise of Subtle-Assholism' to express the opposing force of assholism in the world today, to the up and coming force of subtle-assholism.
