Chillicothe
1. A small town in central Illinois, near Peoria. It Consists of great establishments such as: The Short Loin, Shore Acres Park, The Chilli Bowl, Blarney Castle, a classic Pizza Hut and a gas station I used to steal from when I was a kid. ; 2. The best place in America to get some [McDonalds] and feed french fries to birds next to the Illinois river. ; 3. A good place to drive to when you are bored and want to see where the legend was brought up. ; 4. A great area to live in in the '80's and play [8-Bit].
