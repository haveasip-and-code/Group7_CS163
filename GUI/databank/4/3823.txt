Baggit  The Game
A game which is played by running around catching as many negros as possible and stuffing them into a large burlap sack. The player with the most negros at dusk wins. They have to be alive or you will recieve a two point deduction. Each negro is two points. If you catch a [nigglet], you have to let it go. The game ends at dusk due to obvious reasons not stated.
