Youko Kurama
The full name of the [yu yu hakusho] chatacter, [Kurama]. Youko is a fox demon who, in order to escape permanent death by a hunter, sent his soul to the Living World (Ningenkai) from Demon World (Makai), where he was born ('reincarnated') as a human boy, [Shuiichi Minamino]. ; Then going by Kurama to those who know his demon secret, he meets up with [Hiei] first (with whom he is close friends), then the other characters. ; He is both Youko AND Shuiichi, which causes him its own set of problems.
