Les Mis
Short for Les Miserables, a kick-arse musical set during the beginnigs of the French Revolution. Often on the recordings Eponine has an annoying, pop-ish sound, but the character is great. Some of the other leads/large parts are Cosette, Jean Valjean, Javert, Marius, Fantine, Gavroche, Enjolras, Thenardier, and Mme. Thenardier.
