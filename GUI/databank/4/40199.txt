maiden
1) A young [lady], especially an unmarried one. ; 2) Short for [iron maiden]. A [Mideval] [torture] device used in [Germany]. Basically, it was a coffin. The head and body of a maiden were sculpted into it to give the machine more [dignity] and [elegance]. The insides of the doors were lined with [spike]s, which would pierce the victim, although not fatally, so that he/she would die a slow, painful death.
