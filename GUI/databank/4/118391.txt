improvisation
To make something up on the spot. ; Improvisation is common in jazz music, when a musician makes up a solo spontaneously, as opposed to playing a written solo. ; Sometimes a group of musicians will completely improvise an entire song. This is done almost exclusively by jazz musicians. ; All forms of art can be improvised. Art that is improvised may be called 'free' or '[freestyle]', like 'free poetry'. ; A popular form of improvisation is rapping, or [flowing].
