blachino
Noun: A male chinese-american who believes or acts as he was a black, or latin urban youth. Usually is found wearing hip-hop brand or style of clothing, spontaneously breaks out in dance (popping and locking). May frequently be heard shouting urban slang such as, 'Holla at your boy!' or simply, 'Holla!' ; Hence, combination of the words: black, latino, and chinese. ; Female: blachina.
