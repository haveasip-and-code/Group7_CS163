Boomspeed
An 'online auction image host'. In other words, its purpose is to hold [direct link] pictures for people in auctions. Although, its use is not limited to that; it can host any kind of file. Each account holds one megabyte, which should be plenty to host a lot of pictures. ; Similar to [Photobucket], except it doesn't have a horrible limit on bandwidth, but it isn't as user-friendly. ; It used to be free, but now an account requires paid money, except for the people who got an account before the change.
