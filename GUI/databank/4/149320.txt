Nurse Ratched
The antagonist in the 1962 Ken Kesey novel 'One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest' and in the 1975 movie of the same title. The overbearing, cruel, perfectionist nurse (ranked number 5 in AFI's list of Greatest Movie Villains) rules the asylum in the story with an iron fist. ; The name 'Nurse Ratched' can be applied as an insult to a person (usually a woman) of similar characteristics.
