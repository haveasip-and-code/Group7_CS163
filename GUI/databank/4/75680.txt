naco

People have modified the word to 'Naco' in order to refer those people who dont seem to have a good education, vocabulary and good-looking. ; 2) Used by mexicans and by some 'wanabbe' people ; 3) Usually to refer people of low class and bad vocabulary or when someone doesnt seem to have a good look etc..
