thug life
A state of mind most commonly found in the urban slums of major cities, In which males, but not limited to, who have grown up with out a constant father figure use to describe the mentality that was forced upon them by their inhospittable environment. This mentality is characterized by the incessant need to familiarize one's self with the more glamourous side of life, the ability to be able to forsake any and everything for personal gain, and the ability to live life the fullest. ; Contrary to common beleif, this descripition is not limited to one specific race, gender, or any denomination.
