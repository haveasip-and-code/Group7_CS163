Genius Failure Paradox
Basically a common social disorder in which a person (most likely male) is extremely detatched from the rest of society, due to their beliefs that they are smarter/richer/genrally better than everybody else. They tend to be detatched because they feel that getting help and in ; some extreme cases even talking to somebody with less assets than them is below them. People with GPF tend to live ; very lonely lives. See [hubris], [loser].
