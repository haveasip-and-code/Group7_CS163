Sandnes
Sandnes is a medium-sized city (after norwegian standard) in south-west of Norway. The city lies in a [fjord] and got mountains, flats, houses etc. on the right and the city itself on the left (the viewpoint is facing north). ; Sandnes is a great destination for tourists because of the location, attractions and shopping centres. ; From Sandnes you can travel directly to the famous Lysefjorden with car, boat, bicycle and on foot.
