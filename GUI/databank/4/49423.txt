The Bluff
NOUN ; 1. A specific area located in Northwest [Atlanta] west of [Georgia Tech] and the Georgia Dome, roughly ten blocks square to a side with Northwest Highway, Bankhead Highway, Ashby Street, and Simpson Road as its traditional borders. Known for its high crime rate and illegal drug activity, especially [Boy], or [heroin]. ; Xrefs: the phrases [Bankhead Nigga], [Boy], and [Georgia Dome] all originated in the Bluff. ; 2. More recently, a generic term for any urban high intensity drug trafficking area in the [Dirty South].
