haole
White person, American, Englishman, Caucasian, foreign, introduced, of foreign origin. ; The word haole existed before the arrival of Captain Cook. It's a very old word that appears in a story about the demigod Kamapua'a. 'He haole nui, maka 'alohilohi.' Also, the word does not mean without breath. The word ha'ole is a different word entirely.
