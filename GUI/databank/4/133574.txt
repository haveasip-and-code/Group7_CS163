Eastern Illinois University
A reasonably priced college located in [Charleston] (aka [Chucktown], [Illinois]. This university began as a teachers' college and has since grown to include a wide variety of majors. It takes a maximum of 20 minutes (if you are slow) to walk from any one given point on campus to another. ; However, the only other thing in [Charleston] is [Wal-Mart]. Because of this, there is a distinct lack of things to do in [Charleston] other than [party] or sit around doing nothing.
