ABC
Abbreviation for 'American Born Chinese.' Means a kid whose parents emigrated from China or Taiwan and are [fob]s. ; The common connotation is of a person who speaks Chinese with an American accent, English with a Asian accent, and can't use chopsticks properly. ; Most likely forced to pick up the piano or violin at a young age and/or sent to Chinese school. Often pressured to attain perfect SAT scores or straight A's in school.
