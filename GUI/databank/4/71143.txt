queerp
Used as a insult for instances when you hear someone yelling the word 'queer' as an insult towards someone else. You, in turn, would describe the insulter as a 'queerp.' ; A queerp says 'queer' in a specific way; it is said loudly and bluntly. A queerp, for the most part, is usually a [douche bag] and/or [cool guy].
