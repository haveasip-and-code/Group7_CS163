baba ga douche
A derogatory term for the stereotypical 'rag head' (which is also a derogatory, stereotypical term). It is derived from the combination of 'Baba Ganoush' and Douche Bag. ; Baba Ganoush is a dip made from eggplant and is very popular in India. ; It is a modern, Middle-Eastern-aimed version of 'Kraut' or 'Limey' but with the very cool and useful 'douche' added to the end.
