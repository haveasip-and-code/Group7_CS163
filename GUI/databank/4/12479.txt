bj hunting
A scavenger hunt around the Christmas season during which Baby Jesuses ([aka] bj's) are snatched from their nativity scenes and collected by hooligan teenagers. The bj hunt is a competition between teams of 2-4 members to collect the most Jesuses before the New Year. ; The most common occurance of bj hunting is in Gaithersburg, one of the ghettoest areas of [MoCo], where over 25 bj's were stolen over the 2003 Christmas season.
