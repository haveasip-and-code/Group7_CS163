newrp!
To discredit/erase everything said in previous sentence. Similar to 'Syke!' or 'Sike' but 'newrp' is usually said to interrupt someone mid sentence as if to turn them off like a television with a remote control. ; 'Newrp' is pronounced with a decending tone sound like as if a electronic device is being turned off ; 'newwwwrrrrpppp!'
