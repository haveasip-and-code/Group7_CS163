STDEANO
A combination of STD and 'Dino', a Spanish form of the name 'Dean'. ; Used to insult, primarily, Americans named Dean by insinuating that they are dirty and have a sexually transmitted disease or other unsavory ailment. ; Also customary is one person saying the letter 'S' followed by the letter 'T', and then a second person saying 'DEANO' upon sight of the offending person named Dean.
