Shining Force
A relatively niche strategy RPG series which was among the first Sega console titles to sell over a million copies. Lesser known in the western world, but nonetheless has a dedicated following. ; The Shining Force series is a subseries of the 'Shining Series', which includes other styles of RPG, and yet maintains story consistency/crossovers throughout the original titles. ; Recent additions to the series have not been so well received, but nonetheless the series is alive and growing in popularity as always.
