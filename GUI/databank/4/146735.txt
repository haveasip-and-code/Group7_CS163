Nural
Rock band out of La Crescenta, California that emerged in the late 90's and took the Los Angeles rock scene by storm and is still rocking to this day. THe band finished its first full length cd in 2005 on Hopeless Records entitled 'The Weight of the World.' Their unique sound combined with unmatchable tallent gained them fame all the way from the california coast line to japan (going the long way). Intense live preformances with energy filled rock draw large crowds. The band continues to play venues such as the Troubedour, Hardrock Cafe, Mosaic Cafe, Chain Reaction, and has played sets at the famous Warped Tour. THe band continues to rock stages and grows in popularity.
