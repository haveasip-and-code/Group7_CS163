conch
1. Any of various tropical marine gastropod mollusks, especially of the genera Strombus and Cassis, having large, often brightly colored spiral shells and edible flesh. ; 2. Person born in the Florida Keys, an island chain at the southernmost tip of Florida, also referred to as the 'Conch Republic' Anyone having lived in the Keys for 7 or more consecutive years may call themselves a 'freshwater conch'.
