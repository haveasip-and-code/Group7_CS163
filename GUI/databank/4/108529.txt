fabiers
n. a device used by ants to increase their elevation. They consist of very small pieces of wood on which ants attach their tiny little legs to, and walk. These fabiers, invented by Fabiola Mendez and Javier Solis (hence the name), are best depicted in a series of i's. ; -note that these are not to be confused with !!, which are ants carrying sticks, of *** which can either be ants going to heaven, or superant.
