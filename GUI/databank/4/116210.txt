Kouga
A character in the hit anime/manga series, [InuYasha], by Rumiko Takahashi. This demon controls man-eating wolves. He has shards of the Jewel in his right arm and feet. He uses [Kagome] in his battle with the gokurakucho. He falls in love with Kagome at first sight, and makes enough passes at her to anger Inuyasha. ; Kouga's name means 'Steel Fang.' ; Alternate spelling: [Koga]
