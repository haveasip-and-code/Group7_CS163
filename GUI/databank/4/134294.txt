Shinedown
A 'Southern Rock' band from Jacksonville, FL. Heavily influenced by Otis Redding, Led Zeppelin and Lynyrd Skynyrd. Those influences are heavily reflected in their music, as they have re-recorded Lynyrd Skynyrds' 'Simple Man'. Shinedown is made up of: Brent Smith(vocals), Jasin Todd(guitar), Barry Kerch(drums) and Brad Stewart(bass). ; First brought to the world with their debut album 'Leave a whisper', they toured on that album for 2 years. In October of '05 they released their 2nd album entitled 'Us and Them'.
