wong foo
Pronunciation: wong FOO ; Function: verb ; past tense: wong fooed ; taken from the film 'To Wong Foo, Thanks for Everything, Julie Newmar' ; the act of temporarily turning a dreary, nearly uninhabitable room (hotel, motel, etc) into a place worthy to stay by the use of fabrics (normally brightly coloured), appropriate furniture placement, pictures, etc. ; This is exemplified approximately 45 minutes into the movie.
