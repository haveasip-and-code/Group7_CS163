Yomi
A character from [Yu Yu Hakusho], and [Kurama]'s former right-hand man for thievery. Yomi appears in the later episodes of the series, somewhere in the 90's, doing the Makai saga. ; Is blind, has three ears on each side, long dark hair, and a soothing deep voice rather similar to Sakio's from earlier episodes (english dub). Yomi wants to unify Demon World.
