Fox News liberal
Someone who claims to be a [liberal] or [Democrat], but has positions and a voting record reflecting that of a [conservative] or [Republican]. See also [DINO]. ; One who is often called a 'Fox News liberal' is Alan Colmes, co-host (with Sean Hannity) of Hannity & Colmes; he is criticised by liberal commentators, including author and talk show host Al Franken, as weak and unable to effectively debate his decisively conservative counterpart.
