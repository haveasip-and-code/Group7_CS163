noisia
Noisia is also a three-man [Drum & Bass] production crew from Groningen, NL, comprising Thijs de Vlieger, Nik Roos and Martijn van Sonderen. ; They have recorded for a variety of labels including Shadow Law Recordings (USA), oBSEssions (NL), Citrus (NL), Renegade Hardware (UK), Movin' Shadow (UK), Subtitles (UK) and BC Presents (UK). ; Their name originates from seeing the word VISION written upside-down.
