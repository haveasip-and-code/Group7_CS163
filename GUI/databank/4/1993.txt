oralism
Noun, a method of educating deaf children by speech and lipreading alone, without and/or banning the use of sign language. ; With the result that deaf children grow up without learning eg. English language, and instead learns eg. British Sign Language from older children in school playgrounds. ; Such deaf people end up with a deaf sounding voice, and poorly educated with a reading age of 9, unemployed or in unskilled labour. ; Adj. Oralist.
