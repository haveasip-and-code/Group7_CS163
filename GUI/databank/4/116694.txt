A great gift for the near dead or barely living or maybe just for yourself. ; These alternative sodas are the most written-about soft drinks in the world. Virtually all the 'alternative' magazines have done articles on Skeleteens, and most of the big newspapers. Notorious or friendly, our stuff is newsworthy.
