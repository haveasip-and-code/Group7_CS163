X parasite
A [parasitic] [life-form] that obtains a new host body by destroying the original and creating a copy. ; An X Parasite samples DNA data from previous hosts and can then change it's shape accordingly. X Parasites have been known to copy the actions of the hosts so perfectly that often there are X Parasites walking in the streets of you local city and nobody even realises it. ; The X Parasite, if threatened, will destroy a life-form and steal it's DNA.
