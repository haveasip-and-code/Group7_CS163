anestrus
A period of sexual dormancy. ; Explanation: ; ANESTRI is a common play in scrabble and is the plural of anestrus. Anestrus literally means 'without estrus' or an absence of the female reproductive cycle. The official scrabble players dictionary provides an ambiguous definition: 'Periods of sexual dormancy.' Anestrus in the slang sense is a bastardization of and play on the scientific term.
