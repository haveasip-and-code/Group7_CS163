nanoflame
This word originated from a game involving a haunted house where ghosts said nanoflame to scare its victims instead of the usual 'boo'. It has since evolved to become a term used to refer to acts of stupidity. Widely used eapecially is the South-East asain region, where the game originated. ; 1.Meaning stupidity. used to refer to people or things, though it is usually more appropriate on the former. ; 2. Used as a subsitute for 'boo', as in the 'boo' used by ghosts. Used to scare people at blind corners.
