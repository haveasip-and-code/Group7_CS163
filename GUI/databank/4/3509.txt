schwa
1) An expression of excitement, or extreme happiness. ; 2) Acknowledgement of someone who has greeted you, or a greeting in itself. ; 3) A state of mind, in which one is relaxed. ; 4) A neural vowel sound used in the dictionary, depicted as a lowercase letter 'e', rotated 180 degrees counter-clockwise. ; __ ; Slang originated from [Hashmeer] of [Group X], who used the phrase 'Shiggity shiggity schwa.' Eventually, Jager, of The Hoodlums in Upstate New York, began to use Schwa inan every day context.
