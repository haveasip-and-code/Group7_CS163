cutepie
A word used to describe someone who is cute. The suffix -pie is put on the end to make the person saying it sound sweet. ; However, -pie can be put on the end of any word. ; Such as : delusionalpie, fagpie, jerkpie, meenpie, adorablepie, scarypie, disgustingpie, sexypie. ; Originated by Hii-kun from GaiaOnline, when she accidentally called her friend Steve a 'babypie'.
