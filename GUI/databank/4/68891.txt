hadji
noun/proper noun ; (origins) American soldiers of the Second Persian Gulf War report that this derogatory and semi-racist term for arabs may have originated with the cartoon character 'Hadji' from the 1964 television cartoon series 'Jonny Quest'.(Released to DVD September, 2004) Hadji, a dark-skinned, turban wearing, south-indian sidekick with mystical powers was also a main character in the 1996 series 'The Real Adventures of Jonny Quest'
