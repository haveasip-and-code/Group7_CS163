REACHtard
1:A derogatory term for anyone in the Realistic Educational Alternative for Children with Disabilities (REACH). Also see [REACH kid] ; 2:An insult; calling a person a REACHtard implies that such a person should be in REACH, or should have been in REACH; degrees above the word [retard], but like the word retard, it can be used in either a serious or a non-serious manner.
