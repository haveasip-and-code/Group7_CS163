Guailo
Person of Anglo-Saxon/Caucasian descent. Taken from the cantonese description of a 'white ghost/dead white man'. Meant to be used as any other racial slur/remark when refering to a person with a white-skinned complexion. ; Synonym: Cracker, Honkey, Redneck, etc. ; Antonym(s): Nigger, Spic, Wetback, Chink, slant, etc.
