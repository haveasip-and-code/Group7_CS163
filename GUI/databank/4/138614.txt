gut-shot
In poker, four out of five cards needed to make a straight if the middle number is the one missing. ; Opposite of [open-ended] (four cards in a row that will make a straight with either low or high card). ; A gut-shot straight draw is less likely to suceed because there are only four cards that can help you. [Open-ended] straight draws have better odds because there are eight cards that can make your straight.
