Makai
One of the '[realm]s' or 'worlds' discussed and dealt with in anime such as [Yu Yu Hakusho], specifically. In this anime, there are three: Makai-demon world; [Reikai]-spirit world; and [Ningenkai]-human/mortal world. ; It is from this place that all demons originated, infiltrating human world through portals. ; The control of these portals becomes a major issue in the series. ; Later on, introduced as being ruled by three strong demons, [Yomi], [Raizen], and [Mukuro].
