Survival Kids
Obscure [Game Boy] Color game about a ten-year-old and his/her strugle to survive and escape a seemingly uninhabited island, with eight possible ways to end the game. Near the end of the game, the player may aquire a second child of the opposite gender who cannot continue living without the main character's attention. ; Had an even more obscure sequel that sadly, never set foot on American soil. ; Konami (Creators of the first two games) is currently creating a sequel for the [Nintendo DS], entitled 'Lost in Blue'.
