buffalo wings
One of the greatest food creations of all time. Buffalo Wings are chicken wings that are breaded, and then coated in 'Buffalo Sauce' which is almost always liquified red pepper, but some restaurants add other ingredients. ; There is great debate amongs b-wing lovers about who has the best wings. It is generally agreed upon that Hooters has the best wings in the world, while Buffalo Wild Wings comes in a close second. ; Plus, they go great with beer.
