body like baywatch  face like crimewatch
Used to describe a man/woman who has a very attractive body which is let down by a face that looks like a mug shot. ; This is a word play on two popular TV shows in the UK, [Baywatch] with muscled and tanned bodies and crimewatch a TV show highlighting criminal activity with many mug shots of scary looking offenders or wanted criminals. ; See also [BayCrime]
