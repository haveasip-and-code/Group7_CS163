chingao
adjective ; 1. A variation of the Mexican Spanish word 'chingado', the past participle of the verb chingar, meaning to rape, violate, or fuck. Literally, chingao means fucked up. ; 2. Chingao is also used as an exclamation of suprise, lament, or as a basic gap filler. The use of this particular prononuciation of the term chingado, is most common in North Mexico and in the Southwestern U.S. from Eastern Arizona down to South Texas.
