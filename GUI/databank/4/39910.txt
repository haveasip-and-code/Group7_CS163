moxy
A word that would be better suited to being on the 'oldtimey-dictionary.com' website. If you are told that you have moxy it means one of three things: ; 1.) What the Hollywood world today calls 'it.' ; 2.) You have an great amount of courage or bravery to do what you want do to. ; 3.) You have Herpes (which was originally called Moxygoshthisitchesherpes but was shortened to just Herpes in the mid-60's)
