Scraggles
1 n. A kid that has the craziest hair you will ever see. All day he sits around, picking the mites out of his bush of hair, if his sister hasn't shaved that off yet. He wears shorts in all types of weather, even in the coldest winters of Michigan. ; 2 v. The act of acting like Scraggles. ie: wearing shorts in winter, having terrible hair, or just acting like an ass.
