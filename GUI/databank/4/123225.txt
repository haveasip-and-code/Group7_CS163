unsolicited
Descriptive of anything that a recipient did not actively choose to receive, but particularly: ; 1. [Email] or [IM] messages. See [spam]. ; 2. [Browser] or [Windows Messenger] windows. See [pop-up]. ; 3. [Malware] [download]s and [network transfer]s. See [adware], [spyware], [scumware], [virus], [trojan], and [worm].
