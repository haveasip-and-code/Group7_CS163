Choiceasphyxia
A combination of 2 words- Choice and Asphyxia. ; Hence made of 2 parts:- ; When and individual is confronted with an array of choice, within a particular category, and all are virtually identical. ; Because of the vomit of choices available, and the fear of exploring all the choices, the indiviudal panics. This is choiceasphyxia. ; Patience and the boredom of exploring all the choices to find which one best suits an individual is too daunting. So the individual ends up rejecting all choices and leaves- with nothing!
