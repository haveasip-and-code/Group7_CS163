warthog
As in [Warty] Warthog, codename of a distribution of Ubuntu Linux. As with other releases, it is named after animals ([Hoary] Hedgehog, [Breezy] Badger). ; In this case the Warthog (Phacochoerus africanus), a wild member of the [pig] family, that lives in Africa. ; [Warty] means that it has small cauliflower-like growth spots all over its body.
