Gimme Mine
To pull the hand across the back of one's neck while applying heavy pressure in a ripping method after they say something unintelligent or ridiculous. ; Typically played as a never-ending game while in school. When someone says something dumb, simply 'get yours' while saying loudly 'GIMME MINE' or 'GIMME MINES' or 'OOOOOH...I GOTTA GET MINE ON THAT ONE'.
