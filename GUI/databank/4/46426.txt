Vooduguru
Vooduguru (VooDoo Guru) ; A mythical beast from the east. Labeled as Right winged by those who don't know Vooduguru very well but known as more of a moderate by those who are closer. A rather stubborn individual who will argue a point to the death and is always willing to make any attempt to convert those to his viewpoint with whomever he comes into contact with.
