1) A food that is so indescribably good in taste that only 'Crazy Tasty' can come close to describing it. ; 2) A description for any action, event, or thing that is so indescribably awesome that only 'Crazy Tasty' can come close to describing it. ; 3) With anything that is so unbelievably good, the best word to describe it's excellence ; First invented by the Spam Corporation in the early 21st Century, it was used in their commercials and printed on their cans to describe the indescribable taste of their product.
