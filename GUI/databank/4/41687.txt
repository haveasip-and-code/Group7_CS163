PsYcho
PsYcho is a 6'2', 220 pound wrestler who was at the top of his game back in 2001 when he won the NRW World Championship. In 2002 he started alongside Bladez the Xtreme Wrestling Federation. Together they took the e-fed world by storm. Now in 2005 PsYcho is still co-owner of the strongest corporation XWF: NRW. He is currently dating the XWF Commissioner Terri Park. ; Finishing Manuever: PsYcho Bomb, Suicide Drop.
