ke35
The Chassis number to a 70's toyota corolla coupe.(75'-79') ; This 2-door came with 1.2 ltr carbie engine, but is commonly converted to 4A-GE power! This chassis was later used to make many newer cars including the K37 (Levin) and KE47, as well as the 'Sunchaser' mod's. The 2T enigne gave the later models the power they needed to dominate rally cross and pass the early celicas on the black top.
