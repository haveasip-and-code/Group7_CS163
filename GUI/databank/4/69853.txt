Animatronic
1.A type of [robot] used for entertainment, demos, etc, and looks like a real living being also known as [Audio-Animatronic] in [Walt Disney]'s term. ; 2.A robot used for shows at [Disneyland], [Chuck E.Cheese], [Showbiz], and other various theme parks and restaurants. ; 3.What almost killed a guest in a theme park in the movie [West World] with [Yul Brynner].
