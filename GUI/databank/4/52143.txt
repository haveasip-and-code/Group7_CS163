brew jammer
n. a beverage ranging from 2-15% alcohol by volume in which barley is converted to simple sugar then to alcohol via yeast. Equiv. beer, suds, stout, ale, barley pop, liquid bread, bock, lager, teddy brewski, liquid happiness. ; v. tr. brew jamming, brew jammed, brew jams. to participating in the act of consuming said brew jammer.
