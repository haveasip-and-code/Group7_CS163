Goneril
[Goneril] is the eldest daughter in Shakespeare's King Lear. ; Goneril treats her father well, until he becomes old, and gives her and her husband a plot of land and a lot of money. Then she starts treating him really crappily. ; A girl, or guy, who is described as a Goneril, is someone who abuses and takes advantage of those who love him/her the most.
