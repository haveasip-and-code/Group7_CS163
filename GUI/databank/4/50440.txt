altezza
1) [Toyota] Altezza. Also known as the Lexus IS Series in America. ; 2) A style of lights, usually clear with a red circular braking/parking light in the center, and signal/reverse marker at the bottom. It is the stock light of the Toyota Altezza/Lexus IS, and variations of this light are usually put onto even cheaper cars, such as the [Civic] and [Chevy Cavalier], by [Ricer]s.
