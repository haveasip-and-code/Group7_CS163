chumpster
Noun. American. 2005 ; An individual who prefers to pay for legal digital media downloads from sites like [iTunes] rather than illegally [rip]ping them from [P2P] networks such as [gnutella], [Kazaa] or [bittorrent] enabled sites. ; Derived from the American slang [chump] with the addition of the popular 'ster' suffix that became a naming convention circa 2001 for online sites in the ubiquitous success of [Napster]. See [Friendster].
