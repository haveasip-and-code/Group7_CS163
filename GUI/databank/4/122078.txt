B.P.S.
B.P.S. Stands for Blind Parent syndrome. Love is blind right? Especially motherly (or fatherly) love. ; Parents with B.P.S. may display some of the following symptoms- ; *Think their child is sooo much better than they actually are. ; *Don't understand why their child doens't place 1st ; *Can't understand why anyone would ever not like their child ; *Are confused when teachers, adults or even peers of their children have compaints about their child's behavior ; (also see[B.D.P.S.])
