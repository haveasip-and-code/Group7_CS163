fo'realdohhuh
Commonly used in the south as an affirmation of a known fact. A culmination of the two common phrases ; 'fo' real though?' and 'huh'. Alan Shcnore, an expert in the use of the phrase, aided in the development of it's modern uses back in 1998 during his high school years.The phrase is most commonly used in the form of a question at the end of a sentence but may be used elsewhere if possible.
