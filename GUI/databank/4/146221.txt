chitlan
chitlan are small soft haired or bristle haired animal, grows to the size of a shetland pony. affectionate and docile, these great little critters make for fantastic family pets. they have a quiet temperament and have no qualms as to the food you feed them as they can be likened to a goat with their broad culinary tastes. they can be expected to live from ten to nineteen years of age and require regular care and attention.
