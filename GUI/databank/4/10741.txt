nog lip
'nog lip' is a phrase used to describe a potentially embarassing situation when a man takes a sip of eggnog at a christmas party and gets some on his upper lip, unbeknownest to him. ; It can also refer to someone who has been given something resembling nog lip, such as a nice wide shot on the lip after a romantic entanglement. ; This phrase first appeared on the Phil Hendrie show in early December, 2004.
