transsensual
A (usually male) person attracted to (usually MTF) transsexuals. The transsexuals usually have been on hormones for an extensive period of time and have a mostly female body shape (and gender identity), except that they also have a penis. Transsensuals are sometimes referred to pejoratively as [tranny-chaser]s. ; See [transsexual], [transgender], [shemale].
