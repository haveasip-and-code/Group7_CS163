Mount Neeb
Mount Neeb is a mountain in Bristol - England. ; The mountain is legendary to followers of the neeb. Legend states that the mountain harnesses the true power of the neeb, and to achieve this, you must give offerings to the mountain, such as money, or sticks. The followers also claim that worshipping Mount Neeb is a vital part of life. Followers will square dance, skip, pray, and do many other activities on Mount Neeb. ; The mountain also offers marriages upon it. Although they may be expensive, they are way better than marriages upon the less significant Mount Noob. ; Also, the legendary movie The Wrath Of Mount Neeb was based on this very mountain.
