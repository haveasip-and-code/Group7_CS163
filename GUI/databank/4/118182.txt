GISM
GISM ( also G.I.S.M.) were a hardcore band (with strong metal leanings) formed in Japan in the early 1980s. The acronym G.I.S.M. has never had a constant meaning attached to it. Variations include God In The Schizoid Mind, Guy In Suicide Mission, Guerilla Incendiary Sabotage Mutineer, and Gnostic Idiocyncracy Sonic Militant. ; GISM were widely known for the extreme violence present in their lyrics, and live shows.
