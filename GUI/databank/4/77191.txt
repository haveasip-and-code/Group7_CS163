mammeor
n. singular (pl. mammeir) from the latin: mammus (divinely chosen to eat) . ; def: a very avid pasta eater; pasta lover; ; also: predestined to long journeys in unusual places (i.e. Ulisse become Ulisser ; in another epic story lost in the Greek archives of Athens around 356 a.d.). ; also (rare): not English proficient.
