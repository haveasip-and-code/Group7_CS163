Lolita Complex
A sexual complex similar to that of the [Shotaro Complex]. ; The Lolita Complex describes a sexual attraction to younger girls, usually by adult males. Females may also have it, but they are not as common. ; It is named for the title character in the novel by Vladimir Nabokov. The story involved a man who became obssessed with a 12-year-old girl. ; The Lolita Complex is often shortened to [lolicon].
