Ruthless Records
Independent Record Label under Priority Records that was owned by the late Eazy-E. Had many great acts on its roster including, NWA, Dr. Dre, Bone Thugs-N-Harmony, Ice Cube, MC Ren, Michel'le, The D.O.C, & Eazy-E himself. The label is still in business but after the death of Eazy-E it was left to his wife Tomica Woods Wright, who had basically run the label dead into the ground
