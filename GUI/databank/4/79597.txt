Pusherman
A man or woman involved in sale of illegal narcotics. What differentiates a 'pusherman' from a 'drug dealer' is that the 'pusherman' in notorious for loose morals, poor quality intoxicants and/or unfair prices. ; Usually the pusherman is a last resort in the search of prohibited substances. ; Fou further information download: ; 'Pusherman' by Curtis Mayfield.
