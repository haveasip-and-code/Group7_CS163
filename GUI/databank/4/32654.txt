What's up with those birds?
Phrase used when expressing disbelief or dislike for a given situation. As in, 'Why is this happening?' ; Born from a third grader's art project. The children were asked to write a story that corresponded with a picture they were given. This particular picture was of a man standing near a flock of birds taking off into flight. The child's story included the phrase, 'What's up with those birds?'
