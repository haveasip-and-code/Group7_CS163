Black Cats
Mostly well-known as a major brand of fireworks. In a classic episode of 'Reno 911', the two black cops, Officers Jones and Williams go to the home of a weird hippie who is stockpiling fireworks that are illegal. ; So Officer Jones picks up a package of Black Cat fireworks, saying 'You know Black Cat's are illegal in the state of Nevada.' The hippie responds with 'You guys are a couple of black cats.'
