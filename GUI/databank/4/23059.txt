Fafg

It became widely used, and has been curbed in GamingW forums, but is still commonplace on the World Wide Web. ; 1) Fafg is commonly used internet slang for [fag]. ; There is another meaning of fafg, created by Omega of GW. ; 2)Fafg is the number that comes closest to zero (0.000000000001) divided by 2 ; However, this definition is low in acceptance.
