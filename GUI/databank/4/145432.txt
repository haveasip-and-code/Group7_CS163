slick shoes
Slick shoes is a Christian punk-rock band that has records out on Tooth and Nail Records and Side One Dummy Records. Their music differs from most punk or punk-rock that one hears in that it is more musically stimulating, ie. having guitar solos and actual leads in songs, as opposed to just chord progressions. ; Members: ; Ryan Kepke - Vocals ; David Stoltenberg - Guitar ; Jonah Peterson - Guitar ; Kevin Clark - Bass ; Joe Nixon - Drums
