uburbanite
Pronounced U-burbanite, from [suburbanite]. ; Students of a large university, or residents of the surrounding area, whose lives (whether or not they like it) are impacted by the U on a daily basis. ; Inspired by the University of Minnesota's annoying habit of using the capital letter 'U' whenever possible, up to the point of subsituting it for the word 'you' whenever possible. This author is sure that other universities have a similar tendancies, but she has not experienced them.
