kahla
Kahla is a female girly girl who is often directly associated with the formation of [the bench group] she is often accompanied by a Jess and a Kate. The three form a formidable opponent to all things that are sane. When a Kahla is combined with a Jess they form a mysterious super power that has extensive capabilities in the area of insulting others. Kahla is also associated with Katherine, Laura and their own band of crimialistic, street-sign-loving bandits.
