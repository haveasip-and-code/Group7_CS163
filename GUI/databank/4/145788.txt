femme fatale
A beautiful, seductive, and usually evil female character in drama and literature. She is usually shown as a cruel, man-eating seductress. (Straight) men fall victim to her beauty and are eventually brought to ruin by her. The femme fatale is often a secret agent and/or a spy. ; Another word for femme fatale is [vamp]; due to the fact that the femme fatale is often a sexual [vampire], the term 'vamp' stuck when introduced by the silent movies. ; The femme fatale is usually played by a strong [mezzo-soprano] in [opera] and musical theater.
