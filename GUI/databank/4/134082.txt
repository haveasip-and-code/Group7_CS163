spade cooley
function: verb ; etymology: eponym coined for Donnell Clyde 'Spade' Cooley, the self-proclaimed King of Western Swing, who is remembered for stomping his (second) wife Ella Mae Evans to death in front of his daughter Melody. ; 1. to stomp someone to death 2. to stomp on something violently ; Usage note: this is also known as the Oklahoma Stomp after his hit single of the same name.
