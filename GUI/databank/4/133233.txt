Waltham -N. - Home to the greatest of Bostonians, with the truest Boston accent. Home of Bentley and Brandeis. Home of Waltham the Band. ; We are commonly called Walthamers, Walthamians, Walthamites, and sometimes even Hamians. ; We like to sit around praise our great city and reminisce about the good days when we were actually good at something. ; (the Wal-lex, Norumbega, Arbys, Steph P., among many other things) ; And with having the greatest number of hotel rooms in any city outside of Boston there are plenty of possibilities.
