Mr. Saturn
A bunch of alien characters found in the Super Nintendo ([SNES]) game 'Earthbound' (In Japan, the game is called 'Mother'). The character is meek, pink in color, short, cute, has a big nose, only one strand of hair. ; It is also featured in a [GameCube] fighting game called [Super Smash Bros. Melee] as a kicked-around object and shield.
