DYP
D.Y.P. - Draw Your Partner. ; A kind of the tournament in [Foosball] ('table soccer') in which You draw (at random) a part member from a basket (with a list of all player). Could be a 2 baskets DYP in which team is maked by players from 2 baskets (like Novice/Ama in one basket and SemiPro/Pro/Masters in second).
