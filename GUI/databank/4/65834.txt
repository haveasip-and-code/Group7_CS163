Hair Gel
Euphemism for marijuana. Perfect for using when talking to people in mixed company or leaving messages on answering machines about marijuana. Doesn't sound obvious like 'Chocolate' or 'Christmas tree lights' ; Your marijuana dealer can be called your 'Stylist' ; The act of smoking marijuana can be called 'Doing your hair' ; The piece you smoke your marijuana out of can be called your comb (pipe) or brush (bong)
