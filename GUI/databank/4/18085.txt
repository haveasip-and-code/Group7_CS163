PVPA
Acronym for Pioneer Valley Performing Arts, PVPA is actually Pioneer Valley Performing Arts Charter Public High School. A middle school was added in 2004. The school is a gathering place for the young artists of the Pioneer Valley, including quite a few teens from [Northampton] and the [Springfield] area. The school has four main areas of concentration theatre, dance, music, and visual arts. ; The school is currently located in Hadley, MA but is soon moving to South Hadley. ; www.pvpa.org
