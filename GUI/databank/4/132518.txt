Whirling Dervish
(n.) A person whose behavior resembles a rapid, spinning object. These actions are often spastic fidgeting and incessant babbling. The actions of the whirling dervish are irritating and annoying, often exhausting other people in the immediate vicinity. ; The phrase is derived from these 2 words: ; Whirling ; 1.) adj. moving or driven rapidly in a rotary or twisting motion. ; Dervish ; 1.) (n.) a member of an order noted for devotional exercises involving bodily movements. ; 2.) (n.) a Rocking Demon who eats souls.
