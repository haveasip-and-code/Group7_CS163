busticle
Used as an exclamation. Can be used in suprise, or in excitement. Is usually used when referring to something happening to someone else, as in a put-down. ; From the phrase 'Oh Bust!' which was used when one party would verbally [bust] on another and the bystander would say 'Oh Bust'. It has morphed from 'Oh Bust' to 'Oh Bus' to 'Bus' to 'Busticle', or 'Oh Busticle'.
