mijo
Spanish term of affection, meaning my sweetheart, baby, honey, etc. Used between lovers or other close people the way [honey] can be used. ; Also may mean son, or the female version [mija] means daughter. This derives from 'my son' or 'mi hijo'. 'Mi hijo' = 'M'ijo' = 'Mijo' so its basically a shortened version of this. Alternate versions: [mijito], [m'ijo]
