P2P
Can stand for: ; 1.Program-to-Paper meaning printing. ; 2.Pay-to-Play consisting of games which require you to pay everytime you want to play, similar to [PPV]. ; 3.Peer-to-Peer This is the most accurate acronym. It consists of netwroks which do not interact thru servers and go directly from client to client, and is also known as FileSharing.
