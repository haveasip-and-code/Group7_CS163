Gradius
Gradius is Konami's horizontal shoot 'em up series. ; Similar games are [R-Type], [Silpheed], [Phalanx], [Darius], [In the Hunt], [Z-Out], [Einhander], and [Katakis]. Over the years, the Gradius series has spawned a decent amount of sequels. The original Gradius was released in 1985. In the Gradius saga, you pilot the most advanced starship known as the [Vic Viper]. This series has always put you up against hulking, difficult bosses and in the PS2 installment, Gradius 5, the developers went over board and decided to take Gradius to a whole new level of intense action. Konami has also made a parody series on Gradius known as Parodius.
