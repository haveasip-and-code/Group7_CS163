Goldwasser
In 1689-1690 [King Esophies] ruled over northern-[Trastevere]. He was known for and also expelled of his power for his lisp. ; In his 1.5 years of rule, he had two biggest achivements. One being the [Palatino Bridge], which is still standing today. Yet he is more famous for individually watching over all of his gold cleaners. ; He names the gang of cleaners 'Goldwassers', this is today thought to be due to his lisp. ; The term is now rarely used.
