cayceduncan
the most lovely and wonderfully beautiful girl in the entire universe whom a young boy named phillip loves with every piece of his little heart and is determined to make his wife one day. the very universe was created by God himself so that phillip might find cayce and love and worship her for every day for the rest of his life. all existance abides so that her inexplicably remarkable beauty might be adored by phillip. she is infinitely lovely.
