BASBHAT
Lyric of infamous track by rappers [Dr Dre] and [Snoop Dogg] 'Bitches Aint Shit' from Dre's 1992 album 'The Chronic' shortened to abrieviated/acronym form and used in a derogative manner. 'Bitches Ain't Shit But Ho's And Tricks' becomes BASBHAT. The phrase has become common terminology in certain Surrey gang circles; see [SSC]
