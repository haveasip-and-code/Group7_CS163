Counter-strike (CS). An insanely popular modification for the Half-Life engine imfamous for its generally less-than-friendly community, strange non-realism, abusive server administrators, and high concentration of hackers. The modification was orinally headed by 'gooseman', and was adopted by Valve Software as a commercial product. Its only continuation to date, Counter-Strike: Source, was developed by Valve as a graphically enhanced version of the game. ; Loved by millions, hated by more or less the same, counter-strike has been the debate topic of many for a long time. ; Counter-Strike remains, to this date, the most played online game ever created.
