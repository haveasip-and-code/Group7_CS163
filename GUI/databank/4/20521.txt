mow
1.A mow has whiskers and fur, a tail and paws with paw pads. A mow is a mammal. It is mowful, and one defining characteristic of a mow is its radiating of mowness. ; 2. Sound made by a mow. Sounds like 'mow.' ; 3. An expression used to convey surprise, pleasure or just having heard a person say something. Sometimes replaces 'wow.'
