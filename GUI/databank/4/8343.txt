Road Rovers
Cartoon series that originaly aired on the Kids WB in 1996. It had two seasons that lasted untill early-mid 1997 at which point it was canceled. It was aired once again on Cartoon Network in 2000, that is the last known air date. ; The show was about a group of super powered canine-morphs (Canines that walk upright like humans) that went on missions and saved the world, etc... ; This show while containing a good amount of action, was all about it's comedy. ; Despite the gigantic fandom of this series, finding episodes of this is seemingly impossible, leaving the community rather faded out and dead.
