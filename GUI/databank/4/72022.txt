uplift
Def. 1 (verb): to genetically alter a non-human species in such a way that they achieve a level and style of intelligence approaching that of human beings. ; Def. 2 (noun): the process of uplifting a species. ; Def. 3 (noun): an uplifed species or individual members of such a species ; Bachground: Possibly first coined by David Brin in his series of science fiction books 'The Uplift Saga'.
