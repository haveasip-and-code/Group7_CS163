G-town Soldier
1. One from G-town or caught within the G-town lifestyle. ; 2. A person who lives in Georgetown, a large community in Savannah, Ga. ; 3. Someone who spends much of their time in G-town, grew up in G-town , went to school in G-town, or plays basketball or another sport in G-town on a regular basis. ; --In order to be a G-town Soldier, you must have a genuine love for Georgetown and have an active social or sportly life with other people who live there.
