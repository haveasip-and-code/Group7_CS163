rata

Same as [cona], [crica], paxaxa, pito, pipi (euphemism), pastel de pelo (brazilian portuguese), etc. ; It is an extremely rude word that should never be used in front of a lady, unless said lady is a classless whore, or you really, really want to. ; It cannot be used as an insult, but it can be used to make someone feel insulted.
