pink'd
Used in Online games where 'votes' given by players in a game towards another contribute to them being removed from the game. Pink'd Is the Term used to describe what happens to a person when a group of people (Usually [Clan] Members Or Friends) Team Up And All Vote One player so that they are removed from the game. This is for no reason other than they are the odd one out or some other pointless excuse. This Mass voting is normally introduced after a player (The Leader Of The [Clan] Or group) Says 'We Have A Code Pink Situation On Number *Number*' (*Number* representing the position the victim it in the current player list.)
