Pumpkin
Slang ; Adj. (pmpkn, pm-, png-) ; 'p&m(p)-k&n ; 1) Excellent; first-rate ; 2) Very attractive physically, etc ; 3) Marked by a lack of intelligence or care; foolish or careless ; 4) A lot of, existing in great quantity or ample supply. Enough to fill the orange vine fruit 'Pumpkin', that is widely cultivated in early autumn.
