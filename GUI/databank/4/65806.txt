lomeandyoue
1. Comes from the much-cliched word '[love].' The letter 'V' has been known to mean 'me and you.' Thus, 'lomeandyoue' is a simple substitution of that letter with the phrase 'meandyou.' ; 2. A deep, solemn expression of the most fervant and passionate of love. This term of endearment is only used between lovers who are so bold as to scoop in the deepedt of depths of their hearts to reveal what is the undeniable truth of their feelings.
