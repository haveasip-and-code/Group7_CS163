techno-fabulous
also known as 'techno-fab' or 'tech-fab'. ; Techno-fabulous describes those amongst the technically-savvy masses who also have effortless personal style, wit and charm. Whereas most IT professionals, programmers, gamers, gadget junkies and general computer-hobbyists have earned a reputation as being homely, socially awkward, and generally unconcerned with personal appearance, there exists a growing sub-culture of young, smart, attractive, well-groomed and stylish male and female members of the field that defy the stereotype. They are hereby proclaimed techno-fabulous.
