joan of arcadia
A great show on CBS starring the highly talented Amber Tamblyn. It follows the life of average teenager Joan and her family. Joan gets visited by God occasionally, and he gives her an obscure task to do. ; These tasks usually lead Joan into uplifting adventures, wacky encounters, or other emotional moments. ; Her friends, Grace and Adam, two social outcasts in school, have no idea that Joan talks to God. Joan tried to tell Adam once, but due to her trip to the hospital, he thought she was only imagining it. ; Joan learns that no matter how obscure a job for her is, it always touches and effects the people around her.
