Zeno's Paradox
A paradox in applied philosophy analogous to Heisenberg's Uncertainty Principle. ; Tenet 1 - Advice or information taken from Zeno at face value will always be wrong. ; Tenet 2- Investigation or contestation of any information taken from Zeno will always show it to be correct. ; Corollary to Tenet 2- When such a contest is resolved in Zeno's favor (as it always will be) the contesting party will be severely mocked. ; Therefore, interaction with Zeno will always result in the interacting party coming away wronged or mocked.
