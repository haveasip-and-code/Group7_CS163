A-Train
An angry Mexican, who along with his brothers Thomas y Dome moved to America and more specifically Peoria,Illinois in the hopes of listening to more Greenday albums. ; Known for tucking his long, poofy, black hair into the stretched out collar of his Misfits T-shirt. ; Referred to as P.C. or (Panzer Commander) for a brief period. ; A-Train is proud owner of an ant-eater. ; He is also credited with coining the now famous [819] symbol. ; Probably most famous for his quotes: Tuthbrush, forewind, Dooby Dooby Scoo, Air Jumbing and the classic: Chorts.
