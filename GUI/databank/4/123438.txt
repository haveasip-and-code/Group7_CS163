yautja
A hunting alien species based on the comics and fox series of the famous movie predator and predator 2 (Danny Glover/Arnold Schwarzenegger). Every 'Yautja' comes in different classes ; Unblooded Yautja:never did a hunt ; Young blood Yautja: still does its first hunts ; Blooded yautja: a warrior ; Elder yautja: a leader warrior ; Bad Blood Yautja:a criminal predator
