Vasco Ignus
1) A general term [for] stupidity and incompetence, implied in [cases] in which the [above] is applied in large quantities constantly. In other words, Vasco Ignus is an insult used to imply that there is never a period in time in which the person called such is not, has not been, or will never be stupid. You could call it perpetual stupidity. ; 2) Nicholas Wade Maynard
