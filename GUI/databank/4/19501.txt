illinoising
1. (A.) The quality of being both annoying and originating from or residing in the state of Illinois at the same time. ; 2. (A.) The quality of being annoying while being physically located in the state of Illinois. ; 3. (V.) The act of simultaneously being annoying and originating from or residing in the state of Illinois. ; 4. (V.) The act of simultaneously being annoying and being physically located in the state of Illinois.
