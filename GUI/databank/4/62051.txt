honza
A way of signing a letter, or forum post, similar to [sincerely] or [yours truly]. Can be substituted for a number of words, and used at any time within any context for almost any meaning. ; Honza was first discovered in 2003 when an anonymous surfer found this peculiar word used at the end of a post on a [linux] user group's message board. Belived to be of tang descent.
