dudek
>Dudek; Doing the Dudek; the Dudek shuffle> ; 1)A silly dance involving putting your hands up in the air and wobbling like your legs are made of jelly. ; 2)A means of distracting people by dancing in a comedic way ; Made famous by the crazy penalty shoot-out antics of Liverpool FC's Jerzy Dudek during the Champions League Final 2005. Spurned a minor chart hit 'Du the Dudek' by The Trophy Boys.
