OBP 13
'Original Batang Pinas' (OBPx3) is an asian street gang,that is affiliated with the Crips. The gang,also known as 'Oxford Blues Psychedelic',and 'Original Badd Pinoyz' originated in the south side of Makati,and is well known in the Philippines. The group is composed of different cliques or smaller gangs like the Macc-Town Mafia Crips, and is known for their violence and different criminal acts such as such as armed-robberies, drug-dealing,and auto theft. Their main rival is the True Brown Style gang.
