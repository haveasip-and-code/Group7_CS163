Hoosier
Is anyone who is less sophisticated than you. ; Origin. Dates back to the 1860's when Pro-South Baptist Indianans moved to St. Louis County and fought Abolitionist German Catholics in a number of battles during the Civil War. Most of the City was Pro-Union and refered to Southern sympathizing County residents as Hoosiers. ; Non St. Louis examples are found in the David Mamet penned remake of 'We're no Angels' starring Sean Penn and Robt. DeNiro. Penn's Character says... 'I look like a Hoosier or something.' Also in Joseph Pistones Autobiographical 'Donnie Brasco,' Sonny Black uses Hoosier as an epithet.
