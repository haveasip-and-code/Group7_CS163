Azkaban
1. The name of a prison for wizards and witches who use their magic for evil purposes. Guarded by the 'dementors' who can suck the soul right from the body, this prison leaves its guests with virtually no brain power or memory. Known for jailing the wrong person, or accidentally freeing the right one. The origin hails from the Harry Potter series of novels. ; 2. A sybolic place that the loser of a burn match is sent.
