descending
1. To move from a higher to a lower place; come or go down. ; 2. To slope, extend, or incline downward. ; 3. To come from an ancestor or ancestry. ; 4. To come down from a source; derive. ; 5. To pass by inheritance. ; 6. To lower oneself. ; 7. To proceed or progress downward, as in rank, pitch, or scale. ; 8. To arrive or attack in a sudden or an overwhelming manner.
