string cheese incident
A folk/rock 'jam band' that provides decent studio recordings and amazing improvisation on stage. SCI has a lot of influence from roots music, like bluegrass, folk, country, and acoustic blues. With an acoustic guitar, a mandolin, a bass guitar, a drummer/percussionist, and keyboards, The Cheese deliver an eclectic blend of instruments and musical styles, and from this produce amazing live shows called 'Incidents.' ; Deep lyrics in the songs, big dreadlocks on the grass.
