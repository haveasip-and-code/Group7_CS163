Sandwich King
A game played in a public or private cafeteria. Two people must agree to play, although after that point anyone in the cafeteria is considered to be playing. ; Anytime anyone who is playing Sandwich King sees someone else with a sandwich for lunch, the person punches, smashes, or otherwise rips or destroys the sandwich and yells 'Sandwich King!' ; No one knows who's playing, what the score is, or when it started.
