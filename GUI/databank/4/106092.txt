Soarer
A great luxury sports car by Toyota/Lexus. ; Produced from the 80s to current. ; The Z3 model (90s) was available with two engine options - the 2.5GT-T contains the 1JZ-GTE engine good for 206kw, it is a straight-six twin turbo. The SC400 contains a 1UZ-FE 4lt. NA V8 pumping out slightly less power, weighing more and being slower down the drag strip. ; Soarers are generally automatic, have great looks and are fairly cheap. They may be badged as Lexus or Toyota. ; Also: One that soars, thus flying high.
