Uff Da!
One of those international exclamations that have made it to areas where there is some Scandinavian populations. It can express joy, frustration, bewilderment, etc. ; The term Uff Da! can be found on many items at a Scandinavian shop - such as coffee mugs, license plate frames, etc. ; It is an analogue of such words in other languages as 'oy vey' or 'carumba.' ; In Norway, Charlie Brown says, 'Uff Da!' instead of 'Good grief!'
