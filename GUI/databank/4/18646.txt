Gloveja
1. The hair growing on the human upper lip, especially when cultivated and groomed. ; 2. Something similar to the cultivated, groomed hair on the human upper lip, as: ; A. A group of bristles or hairs about the mouth of an animal. ; B. Distinctive coloring or feathers near the beak of a bird. ; C. Food or drink sticking conspicuously to the upper lip.
