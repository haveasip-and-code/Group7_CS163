palmetto bug
The 'palmetto bug' is actually another name for the cockroach. ; But it's not the shy, brown-colored little guy that scurries under your desk. ; Palmetto bugs live in the deep south, from South Carolina to Florida. ; Palmetto bugs are significantly larger than their northern brethren, blacker in color, and bolder. They won't hesitate to drop on your head or run across your toes. ; Also, Palmetto bugs can fly. They are also better armored than cockroaches.
