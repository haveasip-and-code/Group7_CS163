VFS
VFS is an acronym for the action of Violent Fist Shaking. Violent Fist Shaking is also closely related to Violent Dink Shaking, [VDS]. Violent Fist Shaking is the rapid movement of one's fist in a back-and-forth motion, often accompanied by the shouting of 'Yesssss!' ; In instant messaging *VFS* can be used to signify the users action. *VFS* can also be accompanied by 'Yesssss!' ; The action of [VDS] can also be used in place VFS.
