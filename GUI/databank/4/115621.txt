Karnov
A character created by Data East who first appeared in the game Bad Dudes, then to other games such as his own game entitled Karnov. He is a fat, Russian, fire-breathing circus freak with a moustache and red pants. He has appeared in Bad Dudes, Karnov, Fighters History, Fighters History Dynamite(also known as Karnov's Revenge) and the ever popular Dance Dance Karnov.
