buckhorse
Not to be confused with a saggitarius or a centaur, the Buckhorse is a rare hybrid of the most desirable equine and human qualities. The extraordinary ability to pummel is a common trait found in the Buckhorse. Do not believe everyone who says they are a Buckhorse. If ever in doubt, the Buckhorse Salute shall be exercised, thereby ensuring the authenticty of the Buckhorse. ; Also simply known as 'horse' to close friends.
