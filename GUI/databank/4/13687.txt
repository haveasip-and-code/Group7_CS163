FIFO
First In, First Out. ; 1. This term indicates that once a [playa] has hit some [skins] or copped some [booty] he is First in, First Out. ; 2. It is commonly used in relation to the accumulation of hits a playa has had, being the amount of times he scores with a whore. ; 3. Once in effect, the FIFO rule, will exclude any [hoes] that have already hit by another [playa] and consequently does not add to one's score. ; 4. The FIFO rule must be observed under all circumstances.
