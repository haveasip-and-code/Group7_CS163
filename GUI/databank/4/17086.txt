Blodwen Buildings
Dominating the emmense Port Davies skyline, these triple towers located on the banks of the River Avon. ; They are the centre of the government of the county of Wales. ; At the top of each of the three towers is a penthouse apartment; the first tower, named The Edward I tower provides a city residence for the High Sheriff. The second, known as the Henry VIII tower provides a residence for the Deputy Sheriff, Lt. Col. Richard W. Norman CBE PhD. The 3rd tower, the Elizabeth II tower is the High Sheriff's families' residence.
