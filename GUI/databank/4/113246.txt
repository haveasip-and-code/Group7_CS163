yekke
Yekke refers to Jews originating from Germany. ; Sometimes used in a derogatory or cynical manner, it refers mainly to their attention to detail and addiction to being punctual. ; Used as an adjective, a punctual person would be referred to as being Yekkish. ; The origins of this title are unclear, ranging from referring to their short jackets (as opposed to East European Jews who wore long coats) to a conjugation of the Hebrew DaYeK - to be precise.
