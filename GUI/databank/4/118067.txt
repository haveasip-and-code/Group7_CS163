RP nazi
Roleplay (or roleplaying) [nazi]. Someone, usually in an online setting such as a [MMORPG], [MUD], or [MUSH], who tries to impose excessively strict, yet often nebulous definitions of roleplaying on others. ; Often times, a [RP] nazi rarely takes part in roleplaying themselves. They usually just watch for other people to slip out of character in the wrong context so they can pounce on them. A RP nazi is essentially a self-appointed verbal [fascist].
