Sinfonian
'THIS IT IS TO BE A MAN OF THE HIGHEST TYPE. TO BE AND NOT SEEM; TO DO AND NOT SIMPLY TO TALK; ; TO HAVE THE RIGHT IDEAL, THE TRUE MOTIVE AND PATIENTLY TO TRANSFORM CONDUCT IN ACCORDANCE WITH IT ; SO LET IT BE FOR SINFONIA.' ; - Ossian Everett Mills, March 15, 1912 ; A Sinfonian is a member of Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia. This is a fraternity of musicianly men who strive for the advancement of music in America.
