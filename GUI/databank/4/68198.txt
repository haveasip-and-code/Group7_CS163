Swashbuckler
Multiple meanings: ; 1. An extravagant swordsperson or adventurer of some sort. ; 2. A sword-weiling hero/villain/person ; 3. A play, movie, or book dealing with a swashbuckler, one who swashbuckles. ; IMPORTANT NOTE: SWASHBUCKLERS ARE NOT ALWAYS PIRATES!
